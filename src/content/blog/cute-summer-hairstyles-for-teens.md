## 16 Cute Summer Hairstyles for Teens Easy to COPY

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens-copy-in-2025-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Beachy Waves with Braided Crown

If you’re looking for a boho-inspired summer hairstyle, try beachy waves with a braided crown. It’s perfect for a day at the beach or a summer festival.

**Start by curling your hair into loose waves. Then, create a small braid on each side of your head and pin them together at the back to form a crown.**

This style works great with long, sun-kissed blonde hair, but you can rock it with any hair color. Pair it with a floral sundress for a complete look.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/spoidfjuoweijroweiroire-771x1024.png)

### 2. Messy Bun with Scrunchie

For those laid-back summer days, a messy bun with a scrunchie is your go-to hairstyle. It’s quick, easy, and effortlessly stylish.

**Just gather your medium-length hair into a high bun and secure it with a colorful scrunchie. The messier, the better!**

_This look is perfect for when you’re hanging out at the park or running errands._

Furthermore, it keeps your hair off your neck, which is a lifesaver on hot days. Pair it with a casual tank top and denim shorts, and you’re ready to go!

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/ioweruiweuriweuriweuoiwrr-771x1024.png)

### 3. Half-Up Space Buns

Want to add a fun twist to your summer hairstyle? Try half-up space buns! This playful look is perfect for teens with shoulder-length hair.

**Simply divide the top half of your hair into two sections and create small buns on each side. Leave the rest of your hair loose for a relaxed vibe.**

This style looks amazing with pastel pink hair, but any color works. It’s ideal for a day at a carnival or any fun summer outing. Wear a tie-dye t-shirt to match the playful vibe of your hairstyle.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/opieuriweojrweoijruweoipruwere-771x1024.png)

### 4. Fishtail Braid

For a more intricate summer hairstyle, the fishtail braid is a beautiful choice. It might look complicated, but once you get the hang of it, it’s pretty simple.

**Start by dividing your hair into two sections, then take a small piece from the outer edge of one section and cross it over to the other.**

Repeat this process until you reach the end of your hair. This style is perfect for long, chestnut brown hair. Pair it with a white lace blouse for a chic picnic look.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/joipjoidjfiohpsdiupfhruwerfew-771x1024.png)

### 5. High Ponytail with Ribbon

Elevate your classic high ponytail by adding a vibrant ribbon. It’s a simple way to make your hairstyle stand out.

**Gather your straight, jet-black hair into a high ponytail and tie it with a colorful ribbon. This look is sleek and stylish, perfect for a day out in the city. It works great with any hair color and length.**

Wear a striped sundress to complement the playful yet sophisticated vibe. This hairstyle is not only cute but also keeps your hair out of your face, making it ideal for busy summer days.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/jdsoipfjewoijrieojfoijef-771x1024.png)

### 6. Braided Pigtails

Braided pigtails are a timeless summer hairstyle that never goes out of style. They’re fun, easy to do, and perfect for medium-length hair.

_**Simply divide your hair into two sections and braid each one. This look is great for a day at the park or any casual summer outing.**_

**It works especially well with auburn hair, adding a touch of warmth to your look.**

Pair it with a polka-dot romper for a playful, retro vibe. You’ll look adorable and feel comfortable all day long.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/oisjruweioruweiuriweoyrweo-771x1024.png)

### 7. Curly Bob with Headband

If you have a short, curly bob, a headband can be a game-changer. It adds a touch of style while keeping your curls in place.

**Choose a trendy headband that complements your outfit and slide it on. This look is perfect for natural, voluminous curls.**

_It’s ideal for a casual day out, whether you’re grabbing coffee with friends or enjoying an outdoor café._

Pair it with a casual t-shirt and jeans for a laid-back, stylish look. You’ll love how easy and chic this hairstyle is.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/oifdsuiofueioruiewriuewyiore-771x1024.png)

### 8. Waterfall Braid

The waterfall braid is a stunning summer hairstyle that’s sure to turn heads. It’s perfect for long, wavy hair and adds a touch of elegance to any look.

**Start by braiding a small section of hair near your hairline, then drop a piece and pick up a new one to create the waterfall effect.**

_This style looks amazing with caramel-highlighted hair, but any hair color will shine with this braid._

Wear a Bohemian maxi dress to complete the look. This hairstyle is perfect for a romantic summer day in a field of wildflowers.

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles-for-teens/aoispdfjrueiojhfdhsfhkjsdhff-771x1024.png)

### 9. Top Knot with Side Bangs

A top knot with side bangs is a chic and practical summer hairstyle. It’s perfect for keeping your hair off your face while still looking stylish.

**Gather your straight, dark brown hair into a high top knot and let your side bangs frame your face.**

This look is great for a day at the boardwalk or any outdoor adventure. Pair it with a crop top and high-waisted shorts for a trendy, summer-ready outfit.

This hairstyle is not only cute but also super easy to do.

### 10. Low Braided Bun

For a more sophisticated summer hairstyle, try a low-braided bun. It’s elegant and perfect for special occasions or just elevating your everyday look.

**Gather your long, golden blonde hair into a low ponytail, braid it, and then wrap it into a bun at the nape of your neck.**

This style looks great with any hair color. Pair it with an off-the-shoulder top for a romantic vibe.

This hairstyle is perfect for exploring a quaint village square or any charming summer outing.

### 11. Sleek High Ponytail with Braided Accent

Add a unique twist to your high ponytail with a braided accent. This sleek and stylish look is perfect for long, straight hair.

**Gather your hair into a high ponytail and braid a small section on the side of your head, blending it into the ponytail.**

_This style is ideal for an athletic day out, like playing sports or working out. Pair it with an athletic outfit for a complete look._

This hairstyle keeps your hair out of your face while adding a cool, edgy touch.

### 12. Loose Waves with Floral Clips

Loose waves with floral clips are a perfect summer hairstyle for a feminine and charming look. Curl your medium-length hair into loose waves and add delicate floral clips to one side.

**This style is great for light brown hair, adding a touch of nature to your look. Wear a sundress and head to a sunflower field for the ultimate summer vibe.**

This hairstyle is perfect for any outdoor event, making you look effortlessly beautiful and ready for any summer adventure.

### 13. Pixie Cut with Side Part

If you have a pixie cut, styling it with a deep side part can add a chic and edgy touch. This look is perfect for dark hair and gives you a sophisticated appearance.

**Simply part your hair deeply to one side and style it accordingly. This hairstyle is great for a stylish summer jumpsuit and an urban setting.**

Whether you’re exploring the city or hanging out with friends, this look is easy to manage and always on-trend. You’ll love how low-maintenance and fashionable this hairstyle is.

### 14. Dutch Braids

Dutch braids are a versatile and trendy summer hairstyle. They’re perfect for keeping your hair neat and stylish.

**Start by dividing your long, thick hair into two sections and braid each one close to your scalp. This style works great with raven black hair, adding a bold touch to your look.**

Pair it with a sporty tank top and shorts for an active day by the lake. This hairstyle is not only practical but also looks fantastic in any summer setting, from the beach to a hiking trail.

### 15. Bubble Ponytail

For a fun and playful summer hairstyle, try a bubble ponytail. It’s perfect for long hair and adds a unique twist to a classic ponytail.

**Start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail, then add hair ties every few inches to create “bubbles.”**

_This look is great for brunette hair and adds a fun element to your style. Wear a graphic T-shirt and shorts for a **[casual day at the amusement park](https://www.scantotalk.com/amusement-park-hairstyles/)**._

### 16. Crown Braid

A crown braid is an elegant and timeless summer hairstyle. It’s perfect for shoulder-length hair and adds a touch of royalty to your look.

**Start by braiding your hair around your head to create a crown effect. This style looks amazing with auburn hair, adding warmth and depth.**

Pair it with a simple sundress and explore a blooming garden. This hairstyle is perfect for any summer event, making you feel like a princess while keeping your hair stylishly in place.
