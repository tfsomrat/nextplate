Are you tired of the same old short haircuts and looking to grow your hair out? Maybe you’re wondering how to style your long hair without it looking messy or unkempt.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/bangs-hairstyle-2-683x1024.webp)

**Or perhaps you’re just in need of some fresh ideas to keep your long locks looking stylish and manageable. Whatever the case, you’ve come to the right place.**

_Long hair on men can be incredibly versatile and offers a ton of cool, trendy styles to choose from._

**So, let’s dive into these 20 long hairstyle ideas that will help you rock those long locks with confidence and style.**

## 20 Long Hair Styles for Men in 2025

_If you’re looking to switch up your hairstyle, long hair offers a plethora of versatile and stylish options. Here are 20 long hairstyle ideas for men that will elevate your look._

### 1. Classic Man Bun

You can’t go wrong with a classic man bun. Just gather all your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.

It’s a sleek and well-groomed style that’s perfect for both casual and formal occasions. Whether you’re heading to the office or a night out, this look has got you covered.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/sdoifwefjisdohfuweirwer-771x1024.png)

### 2. Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is ideal for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still show off your length.

Part your hair down the middle and pull the top half back into a small bun or ponytail. Let the rest flow naturally for a relaxed and stylish look.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/moiehriejhfrihiuewrew-771x1024.png)

### 3. Loose Waves

Embrace a relaxed, beachy vibe with loose waves. This style is all about that effortless, just-out-of-bed look.

Simply use a light texturizing spray and tousle your hair with your fingers. It’s perfect for weekends, casual outings, or whenever you want to keep things laid-back and cool.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/rtrtgrfgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 4. Slicked Back

For a polished and sophisticated look, try slicking your hair back. Apply a light hold gel or pomade and comb your hair straight back from your forehead.

It’s a timeless style that works well for business meetings or any event where you want to look sharp and put together.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/drtgtrhtryertretreter-771x1024.png)

### 5. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is an easy and stylish way to keep your long hair manageable. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.

This simple yet elegant look is perfect for daily wear and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/tytryreterterterter-771x1024.png)

### 6. Messy Bun

The messy bun is the go-to style for a casual, effortless look. Just gather your hair into a bun, but don’t worry about making it perfect.

Let a few strands fall loose around your face for a relaxed and slightly undone appearance. It’s great for lazy days or quick errands.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/wdgyuwegdwuduiyaiuydiuysdsad-771x1024.png)

### 7. Braided Man Bun

Combine the coolness of braids with the simplicity of a man bun. Start by braiding sections of your hair, either from the front or sides, and then gather it all into a bun at the back.

This unique style adds an extra touch of flair and is sure to turn heads.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/sdvdfgbfdgsdfsdfdsfd-771x1024.png)

### 8. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add texture and movement to your long hair. Ask your barber for layers that blend seamlessly, giving your hair a natural flow.

It’s a versatile style that can be worn straight, wavy, or even curly, making it perfect for any occasion.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/oisdfioeriweiorweoirweoiurioew-771x1024.png)

### 9. Beach Waves

Get that effortlessly cool, windswept look with beach waves. Use a sea salt spray to add texture and then tousle your hair with your fingers.

It’s the perfect style for summer days, beach outings, or whenever you want to channel those laid-back vibes.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/oipoijioaioeriweooirew-771x1024.png)

### 10. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is both practical and stylish. Gather your hair high on your head and secure it with a hair tie.

It’s a simple way to keep your hair out of your face while showcasing your length. Perfect for workouts, running errands, or casual days out.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/nsdifheihriehrwerieirjeoifs-771x1024.png)

### 11. Side Part

A deep side part can add a touch of sophistication to your long hair. Part your hair deeply to one side and let it drape over your shoulder.

You can tuck the other side behind your ear for a more polished look. It’s a great style for both formal and casual settings.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/fghbfdgbdfgfdsgdssdfsdfdsssdf-771x1024.png)

### 12. Cornrows

Cornrows offer a bold and edgy look for long hair. Have your hair braided into tight, neat rows close to the scalp.

You can go for straight back rows or intricate patterns for a unique twist. It’s a style that stands out and stays put, making it great for active days.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/sdfsdlkjckljwsdkfjkerfkeifre-771x1024.png)

### 13. Viking Braids

Channel your inner warrior with Viking braids. This style involves multiple braids, often including a central braid running from your forehead to your nape.

Pair it with a full beard for a rugged and commanding appearance. It’s a bold look that’s sure to make a statement.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/sdkmnkvasdioejureoire-771x1024.png)

### 14. Long Undercut

The long undercut combines the best of both worlds. The sides and back are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left long.

You can style the top in various ways, from slicked back to loose and messy. It’s an edgy, modern look that’s versatile and stylish.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/oisfdioeuriueoiwrueifjdshiuesde-771x1024.png)

### 15. Faux Hawk

For a daring and bold look, try a faux hawk. Style your hair into a high, voluminous crest in the center, with the sides kept shorter but not shaved.

It’s a dramatic style that’s sure to turn heads and is perfect for nights out or whenever you want to stand out.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/lksdnkhsaiijhiowejriojefs-771x1024.png)

### 16. Curly Mane

If you have natural curls, let them shine with a curly mane. Keep your curls long and voluminous, focusing on maintaining the curl pattern and adding moisture for shine and definition.

It’s a striking look that showcases your natural texture beautifully.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/kiuyiyuityuuiyuyuytgyygty-771x1024.png)

### 17. Dreadlocks

Dreadlocks are a timeless and distinctive style for long hair. Whether you prefer thin or thick locks, you can wear them loose or pull them back into a ponytail or bun.

Dreadlocks require commitment and maintenance, but the unique look is well worth the effort.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/sdoifjeoirfieofriewriuweore-771x1024.png)

### 18. Straight and Sleek

For a polished and sophisticated look, straighten your hair for a sleek finish. Use a flat iron to achieve a smooth, glossy appearance.

This style often pairs well with a center or side part and is perfect for formal events or when you want a more refined look.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/uiuitgyufgyufyufutyftu-771x1024.png)

### 19. Man Bun with Undercut

Combine the sleekness of a man bun with the edginess of an undercut. Shave the sides and back, leaving the top long and pulling it back into a bun.

This style offers a striking contrast and is perfect for those who want a modern, stylish look that stands out.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/dsiopewifrsiodfhiriheoirew-771x1024.png)

### 20. Top Knot

The top knot is a versatile and modern style. Gather your hair into a high knot on the top of your head, with the sides either shaved or left longer for contrast.

It’s a stylish and practical option that works well for both casual and formal settings.

![](/images/long-hair-styles-men/alerjeoijfiohnfisdhifheioshrfoerfe-771x1024.png)
