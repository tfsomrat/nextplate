## Tips for Summer Nails

Keeping your nails in tip-top shape during the summer can be a bit tricky, but with the right tips, you’ll have them looking fabulous in no time!

**First, hydration is key – both for you and your nails. Drink plenty of water and use cuticle oil regularly to keep your nails and cuticles moisturized.**

Second, always apply a good base coat before your nail polish to prevent staining and to help your manicure last longer.

**Don’t forget to use a top coat as well to protect your design from chips and enhance the shine.**

Third, embrace those bright, vibrant colors and fun patterns that scream summer. Think tropical hues, playful patterns, and lots of glitters.

**Give your nails a break now and then. Letting them breathe without polish for a few days can prevent them from becoming brittle.**
