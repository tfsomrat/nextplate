Prom night is a once-in-a-lifetime event, and your hairstyle should be nothing short of spectacular.

**For those blessed with curly locks, we’ve compiled an exclusive guide featuring 20 dazzling curly hairstyles that will make you the belle of the ball.**

## 20 Perfect Curly Hairstyle Ideas for Prom ### 1. Curly Updo with Floral Accents

Gather your curls into a loose updo and secure them with bobby pins. Add fresh or silk flowers for a whimsical touch. This hairstyle offers a romantic, nature-inspired look, perfect for feeling like royalty on prom night.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/fgfgffgf-771x1024.png)

### 2. Half-Up Half-Down with Curls

Pin the top half of your curls back, letting the rest cascade down. This style combines elegance with ease, showcasing your curls while keeping hair out of your face for a comfortable and fabulous look all night.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/sdsdsdsdsd-771x1024.png)

### 3. High Curly Ponytail

Gather your curls into a high ponytail and secure with a strong hair tie. Wrap a strand around the base for a polished finish. This voluminous style is ideal for dancing, offering both sleekness and bounce.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/fgfgfgfgfgfgfgf-771x1024.png)

### 4. Curly Side Braid

Braid your **[curls loosely to one side](https://www.scantotalk.com/curly-dyed-hair/)**. This style highlights natural texture while keeping hair in place. It adds sophistication and a bohemian touch, making it a unique choice for prom.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/ghjhjjhgjh-771x1024.png)

### 5. Crown Braid with Curls

Braid a section around your head’s crown, letting the rest fall naturally. This hairstyle blends elegance with playfulness, creating a princess-like look perfect for a magical prom night.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/ghfghghghghg-771x1024.png)

### 6. Curly Bob with Side Part

Part your curls deeply to one side, framing your face beautifully. This chic and modern style adds volume and dimension, making your curls the star of the show for a polished prom look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/dgfgfutytyt-771x1024.png)

### 7. Vintage Curls

Use a curling iron or rollers for tight, defined curls. Style them into a retro shape reminiscent of old Hollywood glamour. This timeless look makes you feel like a movie star on your special night.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/kliloioioi-771x1024.png)

### 8. Curly Bun with Tendrils

Gather your curls into a loose, messy bun, leaving a few tendrils to frame your face. This romantic and sophisticated style adds elegance without being overly polished, ideal for a charming prom look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/oioioioioioioi-771x1024.png)

### 9. Curly Faux Hawk

Pin the sides of your curls back, leaving the middle section voluminous. This bold yet elegant faux hawk makes a statement, perfect for those looking to stand out with a unique prom hairstyle.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/hjkkuiuiuiuiui-771x1024.png)

### 10. Curly Low Ponytail

Gather your curls into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. This sleek and sophisticated style is understated, allowing your natural curls to shine in a minimalist yet stunning prom look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/yuokkkk-771x1024.png)

### 11. Waterfall Braid with Curls

Create a waterfall braid on one side, blending it into your natural curls. This intricate and whimsical style offers a beautiful, detailed look, perfect for those who love unique hairstyles.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/ppopoppp-771x1024.png)

### 12. Side Swept Curls

Sweep your curls to one side and secure them with bobby pins. This glamorous style showcases your curls while keeping hair off your face, offering a classic and elegant look that turns heads.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/klklkll-771x1024.png)

### 13. Curly Chignon

Gather your curls into a low chignon at the nape of your neck. This classic and elegant updo keeps your curls neatly in place, perfect for a sophisticated and polished prom look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/klklkkl-771x1024.png)

### 14. Curly High Bun

Pile your curls into a high bun on top of your head, securing them with bobby pins. This dramatic style adds height and glamour, ideal for those seeking a bold, statement-making prom hairstyle.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/lkjlkjlkjlkjlkj-771x1024.png)

### 15. Pinned Back Curls

Pin back the sides of your curls, letting the rest fall naturally. This simple yet beautiful style keeps hair out of your face while highlighting your natural curls, offering a relaxed and elegant look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/jjlkjlkjlklkjlk-771x1024.png)

### 16. Curly Mohawk Updo

Pin the sides of your curls up, leaving the middle section voluminous in a mohawk style. This edgy and unique updo is perfect for making a bold statement on prom night.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/lkjlkjlkjkljkjhkjhkjj-771x1024.png)

### 17. Curly Top Knot

Gather your curls into a top knot on the crown of your head, securing them with bobby pins. This chic and easy style showcases your curls while keeping hair off your face, ideal for a modern, effortless look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/jlklkjlkjlkjlkjlkj-771x1024.png)

### 18. Braided Headband with Curls

Braid a section of hair from ear to ear, leaving the rest in natural curls. This intricate style adds elegance and detail, perfect for those who love unique and beautifully designed hairstyles.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/lklklklkllkjlkjklkj-771x1024.png)

### 19. Glamorous Hollywood Curls

Use a large curling iron or rollers for big, bouncy curls. This classic, voluminous style channels old Hollywood glamour, perfect for a sophisticated and star-worthy prom look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/mjkkjhjkhkjh-771x1024.png)

### 20. Messy Curly Bun

Gather your curls into a loose bun at the back of your head, leaving tendrils to frame your face. This relaxed yet elegant style is easy to achieve, ideal for a laid-back and chic prom look.

![](/images/curly-hairstyles-for-prom/llllklljlkjlk-771x1024.png)
