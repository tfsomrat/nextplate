Whether you want something simple and quick for busy mornings or a creative style that keeps your hair out of your face during a long day of classes, there’s always a solution.

From **braids** to **buns** and **ponytails**, these school-appropriate styles blend convenience with cuteness, ensuring you look put together all day.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/Mother-of-the-Groom-Hairstyles-Medium-2-1024x614.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve curated 21 cute hairstyle ideas that will make getting ready for school a breeze.**

Each style is easy to recreate at home and designed to stay neat throughout the day, offering a range of options that suit different hair lengths and textures.

**Let’s dive in!**

## Cute Hairstyles for School

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/Cute-and-Easy-Hairstyles-for-School-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Braided Bubble Ponytail with Ribbons

This hairstyle combines **braids** with **bubble ponytail sections** for a fun, structured look. To achieve it, start by creating several small braids on the top of the head. Pull the braids together into a high ponytail, securing with a **ribbon** or scrunchie for extra charm.

Divide the ponytail into sections, securing with elastic bands and gently pulling the hair in each section to create a **bubble effect**. This style is perfect for keeping hair neat and in place throughout a busy school day.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-23.jpeg)

@[chloeysdooftheday](https://www.instagram.com/chloeysdooftheday/)

### 2. Cornrow Braids with Low Ponytail

This style features **small cornrow braids** running toward the back of the head, meeting at a **low ponytail**. Start by parting the hair and braiding small sections close to the scalp. Gather the braids into a low ponytail, securing it with a **colorful bow** for a playful look.

This hairstyle is both practical and stylish, ideal for long school days as it keeps the hair tidy while adding an element of fun with the bow accessory.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-24.jpeg)

@[penteadosmel](https://www.instagram.com/penteadosmel/)

### 3. Colorful Twisted Space Buns

This vibrant hairstyle incorporates **colored hair ties** and **twisted braids** leading into mini **space buns** on the top of the head. To recreate this, part the hair into sections and braid them toward the top. Twist the ends into mini buns, securing with **elastics or clips**.

Add a splash of fun with colorful hair ties or **ribbons** for a playful school hairstyle. This look is youthful and perfect for students wanting to show some creativity while keeping their hair off their face.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-25.jpeg)

@[inthelifeoftayt](https://www.instagram.com/inthelifeoftayt/)

### 4. Quadruple Pigtail Twists

This style divides the hair into four sections, forming **pigtails** in the back. Part the hair into four even sections and secure each with colorful **elastic bands**. Twist the hair in each section before securing the ends.

The twists are easy to manage and can last throughout a school day, making this style both functional and cute. Adding different colored elastics creates an adorable school-appropriate look that stands out without being too complex to recreate.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-26.jpeg)

### 5. Double Buns with Bright Bows

This hairstyle features **two sleek buns** on the top of the head, each adorned with a **bright red bow**. Start by creating a middle part and dividing the hair into two equal sections. Twist each section into a **tight bun**, securing it with hair ties or pins.

Finish the look by adding **colorful bows** for a playful school-day style. This hairstyle is both practical and stylish, keeping hair out of the face while offering a cute and **bold accessory** that can be customized with different bow colors.

### 6. Twisted Piggies with Pink Bows

This adorable hairstyle combines **twists** with **small pigtails**, perfect for younger children. Divide the hair into small sections and twist each one, securing them with elastic bands as you go. Gather the twists into two **pigtails**, placing them high on the head.

Add **cute bows** on top for a polished look. This style works well for active school days, as the twists hold the hair in place while the pigtails keep things cute and playful.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-28-1024x1024.jpeg)

### 7. Double Dutch Braids with Glitter

For a little sparkle in the school day, try this **double Dutch braid** look. Start by parting the hair down the middle and braiding each side using the **Dutch braid technique**. Add small elastic bands at intervals down the braid for extra hold.

To make this style special, add **glitter or hair tinsel** along the parting line for a fun, sparkly twist. This hairstyle is perfect for kids who want to add a bit of glam to their school day while keeping their hair neatly styled.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-29.jpeg)

### 8. Braided Ponytail with Ribbons

This style combines the neatness of a **French braid** with the elegance of a low ponytail. Begin with a center part and start French braiding from the top to the middle of the head. Once you’ve braided both sides, gather the hair into a **low ponytail**.

Secure the look with elastics and finish with a bright **ribbon bow**. This hairstyle keeps the hair secure, ensuring it stays in place all day, while the ribbon adds a fun pop of color.

![](/images/cute-hairstyles-for-school/image-30.jpeg)

### 9. Twisted Ponytail with Glitter and Bow

This **twisted ponytail** adds a magical touch with **glitter accents** and a **soft ribbon bow**. Start by twisting small sections of hair from each side toward the back of the head, securing them into a low ponytail. Apply some **hair-safe glitter** to the back of the hair to add a sparkle effect.

Finally, finish the look by tying a **white bow** at the base of the ponytail. This hairstyle is perfect for school events or themed days when a little extra sparkle is desired, yet it keeps the hair practical and out of the way.

### 10. Half-Up Wave with Twisted Details

This style features **soft waves** with a **half-up twisted crown**. Begin by using a curling iron or crimper to create loose waves throughout the hair. Then, take two small sections from the front, twist them, and secure them at the back with **small elastics**.

This is a quick and easy hairstyle, perfect for school days when you want to add some texture and detail. The twists keep the hair from falling into the face, while the waves offer a relaxed and cute vibe.

### 11. Simple Low Ponytail

For a minimalist yet chic look, this **low ponytail** is a quick go-to hairstyle. Simply gather the hair at the nape of the neck and secure it with a **simple elastic band**. Leave some face-framing strands for a more effortless style.

This look is perfect for busy school mornings when time is limited but you still want a polished appearance. It’s a classic style that works well for students who prefer no-fuss hair.

### 12. Double Braids with Decorative Bows

This look features **two neat braids** that are finished with **decorative bows** for a pop of fun. Start by creating a center part and then braid each side of the hair using a traditional three-strand braid technique.

Secure the ends with elastic bands and tie **colorful bows** to complete the look. This style is perfect for younger students who love a polished, cute, and school-appropriate look that stays neat throughout the day.

This hairstyle is ideal for keeping hair tidy during the school day, while the bow adds a fun and youthful touch.

Add elastics down the length of the ponytail, gently tugging the hair between elastics to create the bubbles. This playful yet tidy hairstyle works well for school and can be customized by changing the colors of the elastics to match uniforms or outfits.

This hairstyle combines a **simple braid** at the crown of the head, leading into a sleek **low ponytail**, secured with a **bright scrunchie**.
