## 30 Teen Makeup Looks That Are Easy to Copy

**When it comes to makeup, there are endless possibilities to express your unique style.**

_Here are 30 teen makeup looks that you can try, each offering a fresh and fun way to enhance your natural beauty._

### 1. Natural Glow

For a fresh and natural look, you can use a light BB cream to even out your skin tone.

Add a touch of pink blush to your cheeks and a bit of highlighter on your cheekbones. Finish with a nude lip gloss to keep the focus on your glowing skin.

![](/images/teen-makeup/trfdgfdg-771x1024.png)

### 2. Bold Eyeliner

Make a statement with bold, winged eyeliner. You can pair it with neutral eyeshadow to keep it balanced.

Complete the look with a soft pink lip. This look is perfect for when you want your eyes to be the star of the show.

![](/images/teen-makeup/trytreertr-771x1024.png)

### 3. Glossy Lips

For a dewy, fresh appearance, focus on glossy lips. Use a tinted lip gloss in a vibrant color like cherry or coral.

Keep the rest of your makeup minimal with just a touch of mascara and a light BB cream for a natural finish.

![](/images/teen-makeup/fdgfdgfdgfd-1-771x1024.png)

### 4. Pastel Eyeshadow

Experiment with pastel eyeshadows in shades like lavender, mint green, or baby blue. These colors are fun and youthful.

Keep the rest of your makeup simple to let your eyes pop and be the center of attention.

![](/images/teen-makeup/hgfjftg-hg-771x1024.png)

### 5. Sun-Kissed Bronze

Achieve a sun-kissed look with bronzer. Sweep it across your cheeks, forehead, and nose.

Add a golden highlighter and a peachy lip gloss for a radiant finish. This look is perfect for a warm, glowing appearance.

![](/images/teen-makeup/hgdfgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 6. Classic Red Lip

Opt for a classic red lip for a timeless look. Pair it with simple eye makeup—just a touch of mascara and a clean, fresh face.

This bold lip color is perfect for making a statement with minimal effort.

![](/images/teen-makeup/retrtre-771x1024.png)

### 7. Glitter Accents

Add a touch of glitter to your eyeshadow for a fun and festive look. Keep the lips and cheeks neutral to avoid overdoing it.

You can use glittery eyeshadow or a glitter liner to create this playful look.

![](/images/teen-makeup/trytrytrytryt-771x1024.png)

### 8. Smokey Eye

Create a dramatic smokey eye with shades of black, gray, and silver. Pair this bold eye look with a nude lip to keep the focus on your eyes.

This look is great for evening events or when you want a more dramatic appearance.

![](/images/teen-makeup/vbnvcbvcbvc-771x1024.png)

### 9. Colorful Mascara

Try colored mascara in shades like blue, purple, or green to add a fun twist to your usual makeup routine.

Keep the rest of your makeup simple to let your lashes stand out. This look is perfect for adding a pop of color to your day.

![](/images/teen-makeup/rtytrytrtry-771x1024.png)

### 10. Blush Overload

Emphasize your cheeks with a bold blush. Choose shades like bright pink or coral for a vibrant look.

Keep the lips and eyes minimal to let your cheeks be the focal point. This look gives a fresh, youthful vibe.

![](/images/teen-makeup/xzcxzcxz-771x1024.png)

### 11. Soft Pink

Create a soft and sweet look with pink eyeshadow, blush, and lip gloss. This monochromatic style is perfect for a fresh and youthful appearance.

![](/images/teen-makeup/tyutrytr-771x1024.png)

### 12. Metallic Eyes

Use metallic eyeshadows in shades like gold, silver, or bronze for a glamorous look.

### 13. Neon Pop
