## How to Achieve Lavender Acrylic Nails?

**Achieving lavender acrylic nails involves several essential steps, beginning with preparing your natural nails by pushing back cuticles, shaping, and buffing.**

After applying a nail dehydrator and primer for better adhesion, either nail tips or forms are used to extend the nails.

**The lavender acrylic powder is then applied using a brush dipped in liquid monomer, shaping and smoothing it to the desired thickness. Once the acrylic is set, the nails are filed and buffed for a smooth finish.**

To complete the look, a high-gloss topcoat is applied and cured under a UV or LED lamp, resulting in elegant, glossy lavender nails.
