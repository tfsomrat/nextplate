When you’re out on the diamond, the last thing you want is hair in your face or a style that unravels before the final inning. Whether you’re playing competitively or joining a local weekend league, having a secure and stylish hairstyle is a game-changer—literally. These 15 softball hairstyles are not only functional but also chic enough to show off your personality and confidence. Plus, many of them double as great gift ideas for the athlete in your life. From no-slip braids to ponytail hacks that withstand sweat and motion, you’ll find practical inspiration to stay focused and look good doing it. And when needed, we’ve included some handy Amazon finds to level up your game-ready look.

## 1. Classic High Ponytail with a Twist

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/cedricsalon_1737669914_3552155159604373935_264354228-1-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[cedricsalon](https://www.instagram.com/cedricsalon/?e=59e629bf-1629-4568-bdb0-dda0e3b1df37&g=5)

A high ponytail is a softball staple, but adding a twist can make it both stylish and more secure. Start by using a strong elastic and wrap a small strand of hair around the base to hide the band. The height keeps your neck cool and vision clear, while the clean finish gives a polished look. This style works well for all hair types and textures, especially when paired with an anti-slip scrunchie. Look for sweat-resistant elastic bands on Amazon that provide grip without breakage. It’s a go-to style that says you’re here to play—and win.

## 2. Double Dutch Braids for All-Day Hold

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/jehat_1630274624_2651258168044682698_3613587-1-1024x1024.webp)

**source @**[jehat](https://www.instagram.com/jehat/?e=47a2015c-833c-4000-941b-f675a3f9c36f&g=5)

Double Dutch braids aren’t just trendy—they’re incredibly functional on the field. They anchor your hair down from the crown to the nape, minimizing movement even during the most intense plays. Whether you’re sliding into home or sprinting after a pop fly, these braids will stay locked in. For thick or curly hair, this style is a lifesaver. Apply a bit of styling gel for added hold and finish with no-crease elastics at the ends. Consider gifting a braid toolkit that includes sectioning clips, a rat tail comb, and small bands for the ultimate game-ready setup.

## 3. Low Braided Bun for a Sleek Look

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/topbeautysalon.la_1732996998_3512955895201999764_1070151111-837x1024.webp)

**source @**[topbeautysalon.la](https://www.instagram.com/topbeautysalon.la/?e=f4def867-b3a2-4904-85b3-843d79e04b94&g=5)

A low braided bun combines elegance with athletic efficiency. This look keeps your hair off your face and neck without sacrificing aesthetics. Start by braiding a ponytail and wrapping it into a bun at the nape, securing it with bobby pins and a strong-hold hair tie. Ideal for humid game days, it prevents frizz and reduces distractions. Plus, it looks great under a cap or helmet. On Amazon, look for spin pins or secure-hold bun makers that make this style quicker to pull off. It’s a thoughtful gift for players who like a no-nonsense yet refined look.

## 4. French Braid Ponytail for Maximum Control

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/alexgaboury_1714674281_3359253806875943515_280216151-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[alexgaboury](https://www.instagram.com/alexgaboury/?e=00480f92-1afe-40a2-b27a-0f3f8b7abf83&g=5)

Combining two classic hairstyles, the French braid ponytail offers firm control and sporty style. Start by French braiding from the hairline to the crown and secure the rest in a high ponytail. This hybrid design keeps your hair out of your eyes while offering movement at the ends. It’s a great choice for medium to long hair and works beautifully with texturizing spray for grip. Add a sporty headband from Amazon for extra security—especially useful in long tournaments or doubleheaders. This hairstyle holds up without constant touch-ups.

## 5. Bubble Ponytail for a Fun, Secure Option

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/anarmasalon_1656199589_2868732536323274276_4606297467-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[anarmasalon](https://www.instagram.com/anarmasalon/?e=de99fef7-e6ee-4a34-8e55-0dbdbd873ebf&g=5)

The bubble ponytail brings playful flair while keeping your hair under control. Start with a high or mid ponytail, then add elastics every few inches to create “bubbles.” Gently tug at each section for volume. It’s perfect for players who want a bit of personality on the field without compromising function. This is also a great way to keep thick or layered hair in check. Look for no-slip elastic packs in assorted colors on Amazon to match team uniforms or personal style. It’s a standout gift idea that blends fun and function.

## 6. Braided Headband with Low Ponytail

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/braidsbycharu_1735669836_3535377283748081697_23594174628-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[braidsbycharu](https://www.instagram.com/braidsbycharu/?e=bd60e8c3-2b6d-4be6-bdfa-940717a20c32&g=5)

For players who deal with flyaways or shorter layers, a braided headband leading into a low ponytail is the answer. This style keeps the front strands in place while giving you the comfort of a loose ponytail. It’s especially helpful on windy days or when hats and visors are off the table. Use a texturizing mousse for grip and add clear elastics to keep the braid secure. You can find pre-looped braided headbands on Amazon that mimic the look with less effort—great for quick prep or as a gift for younger players.

## 7. Boxer Braids That Don’t Budge

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/boxbraidstyles_1704756183_3276054768106291467_1422502485-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/boxbraidstyles/?e=0c2e59fe-f81f-4fab-aa3e-72b7ff351102&g=5)[boxbraidstyles](https://www.instagram.com/boxbraidstyles/?e=0c2e59fe-f81f-4fab-aa3e-72b7ff351102&g=5)

Boxer braids (a tighter version of double Dutch braids) are a powerhouse style in the softball world. These braids hug the scalp tightly and last through hours of action, sweat, and wind. They’re particularly good for intense games where you need zero distractions. Apply a bit of pomade or braid balm beforehand to prevent frizz and improve longevity. Amazon offers kits with edge brushes, braid gel, and fine-tooth combs that make styling easier—perfect as a practical and stylish gift for any serious athlete.

## 8. High Bun with a Scrunchie

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/chloepiche_hair_1714550400_3357490134869042609_4731094692-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/chloepiche_hair/?e=ae39fbb7-6869-4ac1-98c8-8d8da44fcbdf&g=5)[chloepiche\_hair](https://www.instagram.com/chloepiche_hair/?e=ae39fbb7-6869-4ac1-98c8-8d8da44fcbdf&g=5)

Simple yet effective, the high bun is a go-to when you need your hair completely out of the way. Wrap your hair into a top knot and secure with a sweat-wicking scrunchie. It’s easy to style between innings and offers serious cooling benefits during summer tournaments. Use a setting spray to reduce flyaways and keep the bun looking neat. Amazon has an amazing range of velvet and athletic scrunchies that stay in place even during fast movement. It’s a perfect stocking stuffer or birthday gift for any softball player.

## 9. Side Braid with a Cap

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/stella.ang_.makeup_1641169822_2742653715197410315_2202010026-934x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/stella.ang.makeup/?e=3733d991-7935-467c-8bc9-67938926f41d&g=5)[stella.ang.makeup](https://www.instagram.com/stella.ang.makeup/?e=3733d991-7935-467c-8bc9-67938926f41d&g=5)

The side braid offers a charming twist to the typical game-day look. It keeps your hair consolidated to one side while allowing enough space for a cap or helmet. It’s also incredibly quick to do, making it ideal for early morning practices or casual games. This style works best with a little sea salt spray to add grip. Consider gifting a sporty cap with a ponytail hole—Amazon offers styles designed specifically for athletes with longer hair.

## 10. Pigtail Braids for a Youthful, Playful Vibe

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/everylittlestrand_1732122048_3505616283750761977_7071570045-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[everylittlestrand](https://www.instagram.com/everylittlestrand/?e=8fefd8d9-56e8-479f-995c-e0e9659e5f5a&g=5)

Pigtail braids aren’t just for kids—they’re making a serious comeback in women’s sports style. Easy to create and effective in keeping hair in place, these double braids work great for high-energy games. You can switch between Dutch and French styles depending on preference. Add some colorful hair ties or team-themed ribbon for flair. Gifting a hair accessories set from Amazon is a great idea for younger players or anyone who loves personalizing their game-day look.

## 11. Crown Braid for Glam and Function

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/hairstylesby_charlotte_1734178800_3522869917110398146_8374606615-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[hairstylesby\_charlotte](https://www.instagram.com/hairstylesby_charlotte/?e=8838f031-e9d5-4a31-9455-29ae96673d8f&g=5)

Want to add a bit of glam while keeping your game face on? The crown braid circles your head like a halo and tucks all your hair away neatly. It’s a style that looks beautiful and performs like a champ. Great for longer hair, this braid can handle heat, movement, and wind with no problem. Apply strong-hold hairspray to lock in the look. Consider gifting a crown braid styling kit that includes hairpins, sectioning tools, and finishing spray—many are available in ready-to-wrap Amazon bundles.

## 12. Rope Twist Ponytail for Quick Styling

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/braidsbycharu_1731112635_3497148712995179179_23594174628-1024x1024.webp)

**source @**[braidsbycharu](https://www.instagram.com/braidsbycharu/?e=f0aede9d-ffd7-44d9-9383-ebf26b8beb79&g=5)

Short on time but need your hair locked down? Try a rope twist ponytail. Divide your ponytail into two sections and twist each one, then twist them around each other and secure at the end. It gives a clean, modern look that’s surprisingly sturdy. This is perfect for mid-length hair and looks great paired with a visor. Find spiral hair ties on Amazon that won’t snag or cause breakage—they make for a functional and fun gift.

## 13. Fishtail Braid for Intricate Grip

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/moniqueevansfl_1616706316_2537438950218701468_189611735-983x1024.webp)

**source @**[moniqueevansfl](https://www.instagram.com/moniqueevansfl/?e=0a351a36-7b49-4aeb-a192-4e5bc1cf2377&g=5)

The fishtail braid offers a more intricate finish while keeping hair completely controlled. It’s especially good for thick or textured hair that needs more taming. Start with a high or low ponytail, split into two, and weave by alternating small sections. The result is a firm braid that resists unraveling even through action-packed innings. Gift a detangling brush and braiding comb combo from Amazon for anyone who wants to master this gorgeous and practical style.

## 14. Half-Up Braided Crown

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/bfbhair_1581358903_2240923359493812589_1576269382-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[bfbhair](https://www.instagram.com/bfbhair/?e=e99fc2c2-431d-4c09-aade-0461c406a974&g=5)

If you’re torn between keeping your hair down and needing some control, the half-up braided crown is a happy medium. Create two small braids from either side of your head, pull them back, and pin them across like a crown. The rest of the hair stays loose but out of the face. Perfect for cooler weather or light practices, it allows for freedom and femininity. Look for styling clips and strong-hold pins on Amazon—these make an excellent gift set for players who value versatility.

## 15. Wrapped Ponytail for a Sleek Finish

![](/images/easy-softball-hairstyles/ponytailhairstyles_1646154010_2784464117429609413_22739994723.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/ponytailhairstyles/?e=9e433890-5fea-4547-911b-131f70572db3&g=5)[ponytailhairstyles](https://www.instagram.com/ponytailhairstyles/?e=9e433890-5fea-4547-911b-131f70572db3&g=5)

The wrapped ponytail gives a polished look while still holding up under pressure. Start with a mid or high ponytail and wrap a strand of hair around the base, pinning it underneath. Add light gel to control frizz and get a sleek, pro-level finish. This style is a favorite for tournament days when you want your game face to match your look. On Amazon, find ponytail holders with hidden pins built-in—they make this style effortless and perfect for gifting.

These 15 softball hairstyles blend function, fashion, and personality, ensuring you look and feel your best every inning. And if you’re shopping for the perfect gift for a softball player, many of these styles pair beautifully with simple Amazon finds that elevate the everyday routine. Because when you’re ready for anything—style included—you’re unstoppable on and off the field.
