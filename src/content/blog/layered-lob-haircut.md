The **layered lob haircut** is a timeless style that blends elegance with a modern edge.

Whether you’re looking for a sleek, polished look or something more relaxed and tousled, the layered lob provides the perfect balance of sophistication and ease.

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/Layered-Lob-Haircuts-1-1024x614.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 20 stunning layered lob haircut ideas that showcase the variety of ways you can wear this versatile cut.**

From soft waves and feathered ends to bold bangs and textured finishes, there’s a lob style for everyone.

## Layered Lob Haircuts

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/Layered-Lob-Haircuts-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Textured Layered Lob with Feathered Ends

This layered lob features **feathered ends** for an airy, light finish, adding plenty of texture and movement. The hair falls slightly below the shoulders, with layers that gradually blend in, giving a soft, voluminous effect.

**Framing the face with longer layers** helps balance the style, and the textured ends make the hairstyle appear more lively. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a layered cut with **feathered tips** and use a volumizing spray to maintain its natural bounce. Ideal for women seeking an easy-going yet polished style.

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/image-137.jpeg)

@[manjub25334](https://www.instagram.com/manjub25334/)

### 2. Voluminous Layered Lob with Side Part

This lob emphasizes **volume and bounce**, with layers that add texture and lift to the hair. The side part enhances the fullness, creating a glamorous, full-bodied appearance. Perfect for **fine to medium hair**, this cut helps achieve a voluminous look with soft curls or waves.

Style it using a **round brush and blow-dry** for volume at the roots and finish with a curling iron to add slight waves, giving the hair body and movement.

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/image-138.jpeg)

@[hair.by.suzi](https://www.instagram.com/hair.by.suzi/)

### 3. Classic Blunt Lob with Soft Layers

This **classic blunt lob** is paired with subtle soft layers to add dimension while maintaining the integrity of the cut. The layers are strategically placed, just enough to provide shape and structure without losing the blunt lob’s clean finish.

The **length slightly grazes the shoulders**, making it versatile for styling in waves or keeping it straight. To recreate this style, opt for a **blunt cut with minimal layers** and style with a flat iron for a sleek, polished look or a curling iron for a tousled effect.

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/image-139.jpeg)

@[studiomuse\_hairsalon](https://www.instagram.com/studiomuse_hairsalon/)

### 4. Shaggy Lob with Wispy Layers

The **shaggy lob** embraces a **boho-inspired** vibe with its messy, wispy layers and undone texture. This style combines **choppy layers** that frame the face and add movement throughout the cut, making it perfect for those with wavy or textured hair.

To achieve this carefree style, ask your stylist for choppy, **uneven layers**, and enhance the look by using a **texturizing spray** to amplify the hair’s natural texture.

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/image-140.jpeg)

### 5. Shaggy Lob with Textured Layers

This **shaggy lob** with textured layers embraces a carefree and bold style. The layers create a lot of movement, adding a voluminous and messy effect to the hair. The bangs are short and choppy, contributing to the edgy feel of the haircut.

Ideal for curly or wavy hair, this cut works well with natural texture. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for **razor-cut layers** to emphasize texture. Style it by using a **texturizing spray** and scrunching the hair to enhance the shaggy, lived-in finish.

![](/images/layered-lob-haircut/image-141-1024x1024.jpeg)

### 6. Feathered Lob with Curtain Bangs

This **feathered lob** with curtain bangs is perfect for a soft and sophisticated look. The layers are **feathered from mid-length** down to the ends, giving the hair natural movement and body. The curtain bangs frame the face beautifully, creating a balanced and elegant vibe.

This style works well on straight or slightly wavy hair. To get this look, blow-dry with a round brush to create volume and add light waves using a curling iron. Finish with a **light styling cream** to keep the feathered ends soft and defined.

### 7. Soft Wavy Lob with Layers

This **soft wavy lob** incorporates gentle layers that add volume and texture without being too dramatic. The subtle waves give it a relaxed and natural appearance, making it ideal for everyday wear. The cut is perfect for medium hair textures and complements a casual, effortless look.

To achieve the waves, use a **curling wand** for loose, soft waves, and finish with a **texturizing spray** to enhance the layers. The style has an easy-going yet polished effect, perfect for those who love simplicity with a touch of elegance.

### 8. Layered Lob with Wispy Bangs

This **layered lob** features long, wispy bangs that blend seamlessly with the layers. The layers begin at the chin, adding volume and movement to the hair while maintaining a sleek, modern look. The wispy bangs create a soft frame around the face, perfect for balancing features.

To style, blow-dry the bangs forward and use a **round brush** to smooth the rest of the layers, finishing with a flat iron to enhance the sleekness. This look is great for straight or slightly wavy hair and gives a fresh, youthful appearance.

### 9. Textured Wavy Lob with Side Part

This **textured wavy lob** is full of volume and dimension. The layers create natural movement while the side part adds a chic, asymmetrical touch. This style is ideal for anyone with wavy or curly hair, as the layers enhance the natural texture, creating a carefree and voluminous look.

To achieve this style, use a **curl-enhancing mousse** and a diffuser to define the waves. The layers ensure that the waves don’t appear too heavy, making this lob look effortlessly stylish and modern.

### 10. Stacked Layered Lob

The **stacked layered lob** is a dramatic and structured cut, focusing on volume at the crown with layers stacked towards the nape of the neck. The layers are gradually tapered, giving the style a round, voluminous shape.

This cut works great for those with **straight, thick hair** as it gives the hair a sculpted look. To achieve this style, your stylist should focus on creating short layers at the back while leaving the front longer. Style with a **round brush** and blow-dry for a smooth, polished finish.

### 11. Sleek Layered Lob with Side-Swept Bangs

To achieve this look, blow-dry your hair smooth using a **flat brush**, focusing on creating volume at the roots. The result is a sleek, effortless style that works perfectly for any occasion, adding a hint of elegance to everyday wear.
