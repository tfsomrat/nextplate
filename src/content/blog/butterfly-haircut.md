## What is a Butterfly Haircut?

The butterfly haircut is a layered hairstyle that creates the illusion of shorter hair at the front and longer hair at the back.

**The style typically features long layers around the face that can be styled to look like the wings of a butterfly, hence the name.**

![](/images/butterfly-haircut/butterfly-haircut-683x1024.jpg)

These layers frame the face beautifully and add volume and movement to the hair.

**The back of the hair remains longer, creating a striking contrast and allowing for versatile styling options.**

This haircut is especially popular for its ability to enhance natural texture and add a playful, airy feel to the overall look.
