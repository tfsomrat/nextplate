Are you getting ready to head back to school and wondering how to style your hair for the new year?

**Are you tired of the same old ponytail and looking for fresh, fun, and easy-to-do hairstyles that will keep you looking fabulous every day?**

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/bangs-hairstyle-3-683x1024.webp)

_Whether you’re rushing out the door in the morning or want to try something new for a special school event, I’ve got you covered._

**I’ve put together 15 amazing back-to-school hair ideas that are perfect for any occasion.**

From chic buns to playful braids, these styles will not only save you time but also help you stand out in the hallways.

**So, let’s dive in and find your next go-to hairstyle!**

## 15 Back to School Hair Ideas to Copy ### 1. Classic Ponytail with a Twist

Looking for a fresh twist on the classic ponytail? Try adding a small braid wrapped around the base of your high ponytail.

_**This simple yet chic style elevates your everyday ponytail, making it perfect for school. It’s easy to do and keeps your hair neat all day.**_

**Whether you’re heading to class or hitting the gym after school, this hairstyle will keep you looking stylish and put-together.**

Moreover, it works great with any outfit, from casual jeans and a tee to your favorite school uniform.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/shdieruihweirweyriuewr-771x1024.png)

### 2. Messy Bun with a Headband

For those mornings when you’re running late but still want to look cute, a messy bun with a headband is your go-to hairstyle. It’s effortless and gives off a relaxed, laid-back vibe.

**Simply throw your hair into a messy bun and pop on a colorful, patterned headband to add a touch of personality. This look is perfect for keeping your hair out of your face while still looking fashionable.**

It’s also a great way to show off your collection of headbands and add a splash of color to your school outfit.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/sdfhisdriweuriuweriuew-771x1024.png)

### 3. Half-Up Half-Down with Loose Waves

The half-up half-down hairstyle with loose waves is a perfect blend of casual and elegant. Start by creating soft, beachy waves with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight.

**Then, pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a pretty clip or tie. This look is great for showing off your beautiful waves while keeping your hair out of your face.**

It’s a versatile style that works well with any school outfit and is sure to turn heads in the hallways.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/sdjiopweurhweiurweirhiwerew-771x1024.png)

### 4. French Braid Pigtails

French braid pigtails are a cute and practical hairstyle for a busy school day.

**Start by parting your hair down the middle and creating two French braids from the front hairline to the nape of your neck. Finish each braid in a pigtail for a playful look.**

This style keeps your hair secure and out of the way, making it perfect for sports or other school activities. Plus, it’s a fun way to switch up your usual braid routine and adds a touch of youthful charm to your school look.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/gyugyugyuayustdyusadsad-771x1024.png)

### 5. Sleek Low Bun

For a polished and sophisticated look, try a sleek low bun. This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to look extra put-together.

**Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a bun, securing it with bobby pins. Use a bit of hair gel or spray to smooth down any flyaways for a sleek finish.**

This low bun works well with formal school uniforms and is ideal for presentations or school events where you want to make a good impression.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/iuhyuiggfgtytrd-771x1024.png)

### 6. Top Knot with Side Braids

Add some flair to your usual top knot by incorporating side braids. Start by braiding two small sections of hair on each side of your head.

**Then, pull all your hair into a high-top knot, incorporating the braids as you twist and secure the bun. This style is trendy and fun, perfect for adding a bit of interest to your everyday look.**

It’s also a great way to keep your hair up and out of the way while still looking stylish. Pair it with a casual school outfit for a chic and effortless vibe.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/kdslfwejrewpjfrijfhsdfds-771x1024.png)

### 7. Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a beautiful and intricate hairstyle that’s easier to create than it looks. Start by dividing your hair into two sections and take a small piece from the outer edge of one section, crossing it over to the other section.

**Repeat this process, alternating sides until you reach the end of your hair, then secure with a hair tie.**

The fishtail braid is perfect for school as it keeps your hair neat and adds a touch of elegance to your look. Wear it over one shoulder for a relaxed yet sophisticated style.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/dsfvdsfwerewrererer-771x1024.png)

### 8. Bubble Ponytail

For a fun and playful hairstyle, try a bubble ponytail. Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail and securing it with a hair tie.

**_Then, add additional hair ties down the length of the ponytail, spacing them out evenly to create “bubbles.”_**

**Gently tug on each section between the hair ties to add volume and create the bubble effect. This style is great for adding a unique twist to your regular ponytail and is sure to stand out.**

It’s perfect for casual school days or even spirit days when you want to show off your creative side.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/dlksfjoiweruwehriuehriue-771x1024.png)

### 9. Crown Braid

The crown braid is a stunning hairstyle that wraps around your head like a crown, making you feel like royalty. Start by parting your hair down the middle and braiding each side.

**Then, wrap the braids around your head and secure them with bobby pins. This style keeps your hair out of your face and is perfect for any school occasion.**

It’s elegant yet practical, making it a great choice for busy school days when you still want to look your best. Pair it with a cute dress or your favorite school outfit for a charming look.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/xbfdgyrfhnrtytrytryt-771x1024.png)

### 10. Side Swept Curls

For a glamorous back-to-school hairstyle, try side-swept curls. Use a curling iron to create soft, voluminous curls, then sweep them all to one side and secure them with bobby pins if needed.

**This look is perfect for school events or days when you want to add a touch of elegance to your outfit.**

The side-swept curls frame your face beautifully and add a bit of drama to your everyday look. It’s a great way to show off your curls and make a statement in the hallways.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/dskjhfweiuriuehrfeiuhfsd-771x1024.png)

### 11. Braided Headband

A braided headband is a cute and practical hairstyle that keeps your hair out of your face while adding a touch of style.

**Start by taking a small section of hair from behind your ear and braiding it. Then, wrap the braid across the top of your head like a headband and secure it with bobby pins.**

Leave the rest of your hair loose or pull it into a low ponytail. This look is perfect for adding a bit of charm to your school outfit and works well with any hair length or texture.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/dsfksdhiewhrehrfiuehrfuiehfsd-771x1024.png)

### 12. Double Dutch Braids

Double Dutch braids are a sporty and stylish hairstyle that’s perfect for school. Start by parting your hair down the middle and creating two Dutch braids, starting from the front and going all the way down to the ends.

**This style keeps your hair secure and out of the way, making it ideal for sports or active school days.**

It’s also a fun way to switch up your usual braid routine and adds a touch of edge to your look. Pair it with your favorite sporty outfit for a complete look.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/dfjhiuehriuwerweuriuewriue-771x1024.png)

### 13. Twisted Half-Updo

The twisted half-updo is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that’s perfect for school. Start by twisting small sections of hair from both sides of your head and securing them at the back with a clip or hair tie.

**Leave the rest of your hair down in loose waves for a soft and romantic look. This style is great for keeping your hair out of your face while still showing off your beautiful waves.**

It’s a versatile look that works well with any school outfit and is perfect for both casual and special occasions.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/oifsdhyiwesriuwehriuewrfe-771x1024.png)

### 14. Pin-Up Style with Hair Clips

For a vintage-inspired look, try a pin-up hairstyle with decorative hair clips. Start by creating soft waves with a curling iron.

**Then, pin back one side of your hair using decorative hair clips or bobby pins. This style is perfect for adding a touch of retro charm to your school outfit and works well with both casual and dressy looks.**

It’s a great way to show off your collection of hair accessories and add a bit of personality to your hairstyle. Perfect for those days when you want to feel a little extra special.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/mnksdlhehdhsjkhdsjkfhjkd-771x1024.png)

### 15. Slicked Back Ponytail

A slicked-back ponytail is a sleek and modern hairstyle that’s perfect for school. Start by brushing your hair back and securing it in a high ponytail with a hair tie.

**Use a bit of hair gel or spray to smooth down any flyaways and create a sleek finish. This style is great for days when you want to look polished and put together with minimal effort.**

It’s a versatile look that works well with any outfit and is perfect for both casual and formal school events. Plus, it keeps your hair out of your face and looks neat all day.

![](/images/back-to-school-hair/fsdrewrwerwerwerew-771x1024.png)
