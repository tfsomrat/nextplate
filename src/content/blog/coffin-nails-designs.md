## What are the Coffin Nails?

Coffin nails, also known as ballerina nails, are a popular style of nail shape that mimics the look of a traditional coffin or a ballet slipper- hence the name.

**This shape is [characterized by](https://www.scantotalk.com/pink-nails/) [n](https://www.scantotalk.com/pink-nails/)[ails that are tapered](https://www.scantotalk.com/pink-nails/) towards the ends with a straight, squared-off tip.**

It combines the tapering of stiletto nails with the bluntness of square nails, creating a striking, fashionable look.

**Coffin nails can be worn long or short, but they typically require some length to achieve the tapered effect.**

This style offers a wide canvas for various nail art designs, making it a favorite for those who enjoy creative and detailed manicures.
