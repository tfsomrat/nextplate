When it comes to achieving sleek, polished hair, nothing beats the versatility of **flat ironed hairstyles**.

Whether you’re looking for a smooth, glossy finish or a sleek base to pair with braids, twists, or creative updos, the flat iron is the go-to tool for transforming any hair texture into a sleek masterpiece.

![](/images/flat-ironed-hairstyles/flat-ironed-hairstyles-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 23 flat ironed hairstyles that showcase the beauty and versatility of straightened hair.**

From chic fishtail braids to playful bubble ponytails, these styles add a modern twist to sleek hair.

## Flat Ironed Hairstyles

![](/images/flat-ironed-hairstyles/flat-ironed-hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Soft Waves with a Sleek Finish

This hairstyle features **soft waves** created with a flat iron for a sleek yet natural look. The hair is gently waved through the middle and ends, leaving the roots smooth. This combination of sleekness and texture adds a soft, effortless vibe while maintaining a polished finish.

To achieve this style, flat iron the hair while twisting the iron slightly at the mid-lengths to form loose waves. Use a heat protectant to maintain shine and minimize damage. Perfect for casual outings or a subtle glam look, this style emphasizes the natural movement of the hair without going overboard.

### 2. Sleek Bob with Volume

This **sleek bob** combines straightness with subtle volume for a chic, modern look. The bob sits just above the shoulders and is flat ironed for a sharp, sleek appearance, while the slight inward curl at the ends adds a touch of movement. The volumized roots give it a bit of lift without compromising the sleekness.

To get this look, flat iron the hair from root to tip, making sure to slightly curl the ends inward for added structure. Use a volumizing spray at the roots before ironing. This bob is perfect for those looking for a simple yet impactful style.

### 3. Sleek Half-Up Dutch Braid

This half-up hairstyle with a **Dutch braid** adds an element of texture to flat-ironed sleek hair. The hair is parted on one side and braided along the scalp, while the rest of the hair is flat ironed for a sleek, straight finish. This contrast between the braid and straightened hair creates a unique and stylish look.

To achieve this, start by sectioning off the top part of your hair and braid it into a Dutch braid, securing it at the crown. Then, flat iron the remaining hair for a smooth, glossy finish. It’s a great style for adding interest to simple, sleek hair.

### 4. Sleek Chignon with Glitter Detail

This **sleek chignon** is a sophisticated updo with a touch of glamour thanks to glitter detailing. The hair is flat ironed to achieve a smooth texture before being gathered into a low chignon at the nape of the neck. The addition of glitter or shimmer along the part or bun elevates the look to something perfect for formal events.

To create this style, flat iron the hair for a polished texture, then gather it into a low ponytail and twist it into a chignon. Use bobby pins to secure the bun, and apply glitter along the part for a festive touch. This is an ideal hairstyle for elegant occasions.

### 5. Sleek Balayage Waves

This **sleek balayage hairstyle** combines smooth, straightened roots with soft, flowing waves towards the ends. The flat iron is used to create a sleek finish at the top, while the balayage highlights are accentuated by the soft waves at the bottom, giving the hair dimension and shine.

To achieve this look, flat iron the roots and mid-lengths of your hair to maintain a smooth finish. Then, twist the flat iron slightly at the ends to create subtle waves. Finish with a light shine spray to enhance the color and sleekness. This style is great for showing off dimensional color and works well for both casual and formal settings.

### 9. Voluminous Side-Swept Sleek Hair

This **voluminous side-swept hairstyle** showcases flat ironed sleek strands paired with bold volume at the roots. The flat iron creates a smooth and shiny texture, while the hair is dramatically swept to one side to give it height and dimension.
