Are you dreaming of the perfect **[hairstyle for your big day but feeling a bit overwhelmed](https://www.scantotalk.com/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/)** by all the choices, especially with those lovely curls of yours?

**Wondering how to showcase your natural curls in a way that feels both true to you and stunningly bridal?**

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/Curly-Wedding-Hair-576x1024.jpg)

_Or maybe you’re curious about how to incorporate modern twists into traditional curly hairstyles._

**From effortlessly chic to elegantly elaborate, I’ve curated 20 diverse curly wedding hair ideas that will not only inspire you but also solve any dilemma you might have about styling your curly locks.**

Whether you’re looking for something soft and romantic or bold and dramatic, these ideas are designed to help you find the perfect look that feels as good as it looks.

**Let’s dive into these styles!**

## 20 Curly Hairstyles You Must Try in Weddings ### 1. Classic Updo with Soft Curls

This hairstyle exudes timeless elegance. You can gather your curls softly at the back of your head, securing them into a graceful updo.

**Let a few loose tendrils escape to softly frame your face, adding a touch of romance.**

This look pairs beautifully with a traditional veil or a simple hairpin for a touch of sparkle. It’s perfect for the bride who loves a classic look with a hint of softness.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/foweriejoijrewrew-771x1024.png)

### 2. Loose Curly Bun

If you’re going for a more relaxed vibe, why not try a loose curly bun? You can pull your curls back softly to form a low bun at the nape of your neck.

This style works wonders at a bohemian or a beach wedding, giving off a laid-back yet stylish aura. Accessorize with small flowers or a rustic hair vine to enhance the boho feel.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/oifewiorewiorweoire-771x1024.png)

### 3. Half-Up, Half-Down Curls

For those of you who can’t decide between wearing your hair up or down, the half-up, half-down style is your go-to.

**Pin part of your curls up while letting the rest cascade down your back in bouncy spirals.**

Embellish with a floral or jeweled clip to catch the light beautifully. This hairstyle is versatile and works great for both formal and casual weddings.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/sdlkjfoiwejrpweurew-771x1024.png)

### 4. Side-Swept Curls

Embrace a glamorous look with all your curls gathered to one side.

**This asymmetrical style adds a dramatic touch to your wedding attire, especially with a sparkly clip or decorative comb to secure the curls in place.**

It’s a fantastic choice if you’re wearing a dress with an elaborate neckline or shoulder detail, as it draws attention to the area beautifully.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/sdljfoiweirewoiwere-771x1024.png)

### 5. Curly Ponytail with a Twist

Revamp the classic ponytail by adding curls and a twist! Pull your hair back into a high ponytail, integrating braids or twists for a more intricate look.

This style keeps your hair off your face but allows for playful movement at the back. It’s particularly striking with a backless dress or a high collar.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/siofjeoijrweoiroiejrijedsfsd-771x1024.png)

### 6. Crown of Curls

Why not wear your curls like a crown? Pile them high on your head and secure them to create a voluminous, regal look.

This hairstyle pairs perfectly with a tiara or a simple yet elegant flower crown, making you feel like royalty on your special day.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/foiwerieorewiriuweorewr-771x1024.png)

### 7. Boho Braids and Curls

Combine loose braids with your curls for a quintessential bohemian style. This laid-back yet romantic look is ideal for an outdoor wedding or a ceremony with a rustic theme.

You can intertwine small flowers or greenery into the braids to capture the essence of nature.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/foiweriijklvsdknfkjsdnfsdfd-771x1024.png)

### 8. Waterfall Curls

Opt for a waterfall braid that gracefully allows your curls to flow down like a cascade.

This enchanting style is eye-catching and works exceptionally well with lighter hair colors where the texture can shine. It’s a lovely choice for a fairytale-themed wedding.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/sdfcxvkjhheurewrew-771x1024.png)

### 9. Curly Bob with a Veil

If you have short curly hair, style it in a bob and pair it with a chic bridal veil.

This combination is sophisticated and stylish, perfect for the modern bride who enjoys simplicity with a twist of tradition.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/cx.vklshsdewrew-771x1024.png)

### 10. Natural Afro

Embrace your natural hair by styling your curls into a stunning afro.

This bold and beautiful look can be enhanced with small flowers, pearls, or even a delicate veil pinned at the back. It’s a powerful statement and celebrates natural beauty most elegantly.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/xmlkcgsosdfsedfds-771x1024.png)

### 11. Vintage Curls with Headband

Channel your inner old Hollywood starlet with glamorous vintage curls. Add a sleek headband or a glittering tiara to elevate the style into something truly cinematic.

This look is perfect for an evening wedding where you can shine under the lights.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/knvxkcjhieosfsdfsdf-771x1024.png)

### 12. Low Chignon with Curls

A low chignon made of soft curls offers an air of sophistication and grace.

This hairstyle is ideal for a formal evening wedding. It’s understated yet elegant and works beautifully with a variety of hair accessories, from subtle pearls to a bold jeweled clip.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/x.cvmlkjreoijrwerwerwe-771x1024.png)

### 13. Curly Fauxhawk

For the daring bride, a curly fauxhawk offers an edgy twist on traditional wedding hair.

Style your curls upward and toward the center to create this bold look. It’s sure to turn heads and make a memorable statement on your big day.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/iuehfiuhsakjfdhakjsdsad-771x1024.png)

### 14. Twisted Curls Updo

Create an intricate look with an updo composed of twists and curls. This sophisticated style is perfect for the bride who desires a refined and elegant hairstyle.

It’s particularly stunning when accessorized with a few strategically placed hairpins or a delicate floral accessory.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/dksjfipoxcvjihruerewr-771x1024.png)

### 15. Curly Pixie Cut

If you have a short curly pixie cut, jazz it up for your wedding day with some sparkling hair accessories.

Think of small jeweled clips or a floral band that adds a pop of color. This look is chic and perfect for the minimalist bride.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/xckljoierjwerjwehrowerwe-771x1024.png)

### 16. Grecian Goddess Curls

Loose curls paired with a golden leaf headband offer a touch of Grecian elegance.

This style is ideal for a romantic outdoor wedding, especially one set in a garden or by the sea. It’s effortlessly beautiful and utterly enchanting.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/fldgkoptjrrektrejtpretr-771x1024.png)

### 17. Curly Bun with Understated Accessories

A simple curly bun can be transformed into something spectacular with the right accessories.

**Opt for small, understated clips or a minimalist hair vine to wrap around the bun.**

This look is clean and elegant, perfect for a bride who loves a sleek and sophisticated style.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/mvxckjshfhuiuwerewrew-771x1024.png)

### 18. Tousled Curls with Floral Accents

Soft, tousled curls with fresh flowers woven throughout create a dreamy and whimsical look.

This style is particularly fitting for a spring or summer wedding, where the natural beauty of the blooms can truly shine.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/xzkjlhfiuhiuhsdisadsa-771x1024.png)

### 19. Sleek Top with Voluminous Curls

Combine the sleekness of smoothly pulled-back hair with the playful drama of voluminous curls at the back.

This hairstyle offers a beautiful contrast and is especially striking with a dress that features a detailed back.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/dsoiruewyrweiurwerwe-771x1024.png)

### 20. French Braid with End Curls

Start with a French braid at the crown and let it transition into loose curls at the ends.

This unique style adds a touch of casual elegance to your wedding day look and is incredibly flattering for almost any face shape.

![](/images/curly-wedding-hair/mvjnlkjshfusdfuwerwe-771x1024.png)
