## 22 Hairstyles for Women to Try on the 4th of July ### 1. Patriotic Braided Crown

You can create a stunning patriotic look with a braided crown adorned with red, white, and blue ribbons.

**This hairstyle is perfect for keeping your hair out of your face while showcasing your festive spirit.**

Simply weave the ribbons into the braid, creating a beautiful and unique style that’s sure to turn heads at any Fourth of July celebration.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/dfgfdgfdsgfsd-771x1024.png)

### 2. Star-Spangled Space Buns

For a fun and playful look, try star-spangled space buns. Create two high buns and add some red and blue glitter along with star-shaped hair accessories.

This hairstyle is not only festive but also practical for a hot summer day. It’s a great way to show off your patriotic spirit while keeping your hair stylishly in place.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/sadsadsa-771x1024.png)

### 3. Wavy Ponytail with Flag Ribbon

A wavy ponytail tied with an American flag ribbon is an easy and chic way to celebrate Independence Day.

**Create loose waves with a curling iron and gather your hair into a high ponytail.**

Secure it with a ribbon featuring the stars and stripes for a simple yet festive touch that’s perfect for any Fourth of July event.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/dsfdsfsdfsd-771x1024.png)

### 4. Red, White, and Blue Hair Extensions

Adding red, white, and blue hair extensions is a quick and easy way to get into the holiday spirit.

**You can clip in these colorful extensions to straight or wavy hair for an instant patriotic look.**

This style is perfect if you want to add some flair to your hairstyle without committing to permanent color.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/dsfsdfhfgf-771x1024.png)

### 5. Firework French Braid

A firework French braid is a beautiful way to incorporate the colors of the American flag into your hairstyle.

**Braid your hair and add tiny star clips and glitter spray in red, white, and blue.**

This elegant yet festive style is perfect for any Fourth of July party, giving your hair a sparkling, celebratory look.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/dfbfgfvfvc-771x1024.png)

### 6. Curly Hair with a Flag Headband

Enhance your natural curls with a festive flag headband. This simple accessory can transform your everyday look into a holiday-ready hairstyle.

Choose a headband featuring red, white, and blue stripes to complement your curls and show off your patriotic pride effortlessly. It’s an easy and stylish way to celebrate the Fourth of July.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/fghgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 7. Independence Day Ombre

Go bold with an Independence Day ombre hairstyle. This involves dyeing your hair in an ombre effect that transitions from red to white to blue.

It’s a striking look that’s perfect for those who love to make a statement. The vibrant colors will ensure you’re the center of attention at any Fourth of July celebration.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/bvbvxcxcvx-771x1024.png)

### 8. Ribbon-Wrapped Ponytail

A ribbon-wrapped ponytail is a chic and easy way to incorporate patriotic colors into your hairstyle.

**Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and wrap red, white, and blue ribbons around the base.**

This style is quick to achieve and perfect for adding a festive touch to your look without too much effort.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/vcvxcvxcxc-771x1024.png)

### 9. American Flag Bun

Create a low bun and decorate it with star and stripe hairpins in red, white, and blue for a patriotic touch.

**The American flag bun is a sophisticated yet festive hairstyle that’s perfect for a more formal Fourth of July event.**

It’s an elegant way to show your patriotism while keeping your hair neatly styled.

![](/images/4th-of-july-hairstyles-for-women/hgfhghfghfg-771x1024.png)

### 10. Festive Fishtail Braid

A festive fishtail braid with red, white, and blue threads woven through it is a beautiful and intricate hairstyle for the Fourth of July.

The added colors make the braid stand out and give it a unique, patriotic flair. This style is perfect for those who want a detailed and eye-catching look for the holiday.

### 11. Twisted Half-Updo

A twisted half-updo with red, white, and blue barrettes is a great way to celebrate the Fourth of July.

**Gather the top half of your hair and twist it, securing it with colorful barrettes.**

This style keeps your hair off your face while showcasing your festive spirit in a subtle yet stylish way.

### 12. Starry Night Curls

Enhance your loose curls with star-shaped clips in red, white, and blue for a starry night effect.

This hairstyle is perfect for a whimsical and fun Fourth of July look. The star clips add a touch of magic to your curls, making you look festive and ready for any celebration.

### 13. Patriotic Top Knot

A top knot with a red, white, and blue scrunchie is a quick and easy way to get into the holiday spirit.

**This style keeps your hair up and out of the way, which is perfect for a hot summer day.**

The colorful scrunchie adds a festive touch, making it a simple yet stylish choice for the Fourth of July.

### 14. Fourth of July Headscarf

Tie your hair up with a red, white, and blue patterned headscarf for a chic and patriotic look.

**This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet festive style.**

### 15. Glitter Roots

**Glitter roots are a fun and eye-catching way to celebrate the Fourth of July.**

This style is perfect for adding a bit of sparkle to your hairstyle and making sure you stand out at any celebration.
