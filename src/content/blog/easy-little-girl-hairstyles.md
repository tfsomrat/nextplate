Looking for fresh and simple ways to style your little girl’s hair? Whether you need quick fixes for busy school mornings, playful looks for weekend fun, or elegant styles for special occasions, we’ve got you covered.

In this article, you’ll discover 25 easy and adorable hairstyles that are perfect for every situation. From classic ponytails to whimsical braids, these styles are designed to keep her hair neat, manageable, and oh-so-cute.

## How to Choose the Best Hairstyles for Your Little Girl?

Choosing the best hairstyles for your little girl can be a fun and rewarding experience. Here are some tips to help you make the best choice:

1.  **Consider Her Hair Type**: Different hairstyles work better with certain hair types. Whether she has straight, wavy, curly, or oily hair, chooses styles that complement her natural texture.
2.  **Think About Her Activities**: Active kids need hairstyles that stay in place. For school or sports, opt for styles that keep hair out of her face and are easy to maintain.
3.  **Factor in Her Preferences**: Let her have a say in her hairstyle. If she feels good about her looks, she’ll be more confident and happy.
4.  **Ease of Styling**: Choose styles that are easy to create and don’t require a lot of time or effort, especially for busy mornings.
5.  **Occasion Appropriateness**: Match the hairstyle to the occasion. Simple styles are great for everyday wear, while more intricate styles can be reserved for special events.
6.  **Use Accessories**: Hair accessories like clips, bows, and headbands can add a fun and personalized touch to any hairstyle.
7.  **Comfort**: Ensure the hairstyle is comfortable and doesn’t pull too tightly on her scalp, which can cause discomfort or damage.

## 25 Easy Little Girl Hairstyles ### 1. Classic Ponytail

Effortless and timeless, the classic ponytail gathers all her hair at the back, secured with a tie. It’s quick, practical, and ideal for any setting, keeping her hair neat and out of the way.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/gfgfgf-771x1024.png)

### 2. Braided Ponytail

Elevate the classic ponytail by braiding its length. This style not only looks charming but also ensures her hair stays tidy and manageable, making it perfect for active days.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/dgfgutuyuy676-771x1024.png)

### 3. High Bun

Chic and practical, a high bun keeps her hair off her neck. Twist her hair into a bun atop her head, securing it with pins or a tie. It’s an easy, stylish option for warm weather.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/tytytytyttyt-771x1024.png)

### 4. Double Buns

Double buns, or space buns, are playful and trendy. Part her hair into two sections, create high ponytails, twist them into buns, and secure. This look is whimsical and perfect for fun outings.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/dfgfgfgfg-771x1024.png)

### 5. Half-Up Half-Down

Combine elegance and practicality with the half-up half-down style. Secure the top section of her hair with a clip or tie, letting the rest flow freely.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/uiuiuiiu-771x1024.png)

### 6. French Braid

The French braid is a classic, elegant hairstyle. Start at the top and gradually add hair sections as you braid down. It keeps hair tidy and stays in place all day, perfect for busy schedules.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/uyuytutyuyt-771x1024.png)

### 7. Dutch Braid

Add a stylish twist with a Dutch braid. Cross sections of hair under each other, creating a braid that stands out. This intricate braid adds a trendy touch to her look, ideal for any occasion.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/ioioioi-771x1024.png)

### 8. Side Braid

For a relaxed charm, try a side braid. Sweep her hair to one side and braid it loosely. This simple yet pretty style is comfortable and perfect for casual activities or family gatherings.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/yuyuyuyyu-771x1024.png)

### 9. Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid offers a unique, stylish look. Split her hair into two sections, alternating small pieces from each side until complete. This intricate braid is perfect for special occasions.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/tyytytytyt-771x1024.png)

### 10. Waterfall Braid

Showcase her locks with a waterfall braid. Braid normally but let sections fall out, creating a cascading effect. This intricate style is ideal for events where she needs to look enchanting.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/oioioiioio-771x1024.png)

### 11. Pigtails

Classic and playful, pigtails are quick and practical. Part her hair down the middle and secure each section with a tie. Perfect for keeping hair out of the way during active play.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/iuoioioiuoiu-771x1024.png)

### 12. Braided Pigtails

Enhance traditional pigtails by braiding them. Part her hair, create pigtails, and braid each one. This cute and functional style ensures her hair stays neat.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/bnjjhjhjh-771x1024.png)

### 13. Top Knot

A top knot is neat and stylish. Gather her hair at the top, twist it into a knot, and secure it. It’s a quick, chic solution for busy mornings and keeps her cool during active play.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/lkklkll-771x1024.png)

### 14. Messy Bun

For a casual look, opt for a messy bun. Twist her hair into a loose bun, letting a few strands fall naturally. This carefree style is perfect for relaxed days and weekend outings.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/llkjlkjlkj-771x1024.png)

### 15. Twisted Buns

Twisted buns are unique and playful. Part her hair, twist each section tightly, and wrap it into buns. This delightful look is perfect for a day at the park or family events.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/adsfdddf-771x1024.png)

### 16. Headband Braid

A headband braid is both practical and pretty. Braid a small section from behind one ear, across the top of her head, and secure behind the other ear. It’s stylish and keeps hair off her face.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/serererer-771x1024.png)

### 17. Bubble Ponytail

Add fun with a bubble ponytail. Start with a regular ponytail, then add ties every few inches down its length, creating “bubbles.” This playful style is perfect for adding extra fun to her look.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/tytyeytyetyt-771x1024.png)

### 18. Space Buns

Space buns are trendy and adorable. Part her hair, create two high ponytails, twist each into a bun, and secure. This fun hairstyle is great for parties and playful days out.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/yutyutyutyu-771x1024.png)

### 19. Topsy Tail

The topsy tail adds a twist to a regular ponytail. Create a low ponytail, make a gap above the tie, flip the ponytail through, and pull tight. This stylish look adds flair to her everyday hair.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/fhjhjhjhj-771x1024.png)

### 20. Bow Bun

A bow bun is creative and cute. Gather her hair into a high ponytail, split it into two sections, and shape each into loops for a bow. Secure with ties and pins for a princess-like hairstyle.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/yuiyutynjt-771x1024.png)

### 21. Flower Braid

Create a fairy tale look with a flower braid. Braid her hair, widen the edges, coil it into a spiral, and secure it to form a flower shape. This enchanting style is perfect for special events.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/54654-771x1024.png)

### 22. Crown Braid

A crown braid offers a regal touch. Braid a section from the front, continue around the head adding hair, and secure. This elegant style is perfect for formal events.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/546544-771x1024.png)

### 23. Braided Bun

Combine elegance and neatness with a braided bun. Create a braid, twist it into a bun, and secure it. This versatile style is perfect for school or special occasions.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/yiuiuiuiuiu-771x1024.png)

### 24. Side Ponytail

Add flair with a side ponytail. Gather her hair to one side and secure it. Simple yet stylish, this look is comfortable and cute, perfect for casual days and school.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/tyuyuytutyut-771x1024.png)

### 25. Ribbon Ponytail

Elevate a basic ponytail with a ribbon. Create a ponytail, tie a ribbon around the hair tie, and let the ends hang. This charming touch is perfect for special occasions.

![](/images/easy-little-girl-hairstyles/jmkjhjkkkjkh-771x1024.png)
