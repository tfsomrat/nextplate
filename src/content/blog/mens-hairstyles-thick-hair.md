Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your thick hair? Do you feel overwhelmed by the endless options and unsure which one will suit you best?

![](/images/mens-hairstyles-thick-hair/Mens-Hairstyles-Thick-Hair-1-1024x576.jpg)

_Thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse – on one hand, you have the volume and texture that many envy, but on the other, it can be challenging to manage and style._

**That’s why I’ve put together this list of 20 fantastic men’s hairstyles specifically for thick hair.**

Whether you’re looking for something bold and edgy, or classic and refined, these ideas are here to help you find the perfect look.

**Let’s dive in!**

## 20 Mens Hairstyle for Thick Hairs

![](/images/mens-hairstyles-thick-hair/Mens-Hairstyles-Thick-Hair-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Pompadour

![](/images/mens-hairstyles-thick-hair/20-Mens-Hairstyles-Thick-Hair-683x1024.jpg)

**@[Ciapa Denis](https://web.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100008475159135&__cft__[0]=AZVcziEbinKhh7NeoBHemyi8PRLIyOGU0-XKKNmhTz79BiHQiPI7RAup4uq_ii1SXwT3Uk3PNoWabkvIQnZSv34HwCNtqMMJSIZQkFFvZwRsvyhyYd6aXTshCvPcm5xpQjdIlBHOjztW1rOTKB8lOqdnVWhuu5bV-Xq5Fwa7NHKh4w&__tn__=-UC%2CP-R)**

**To achieve this, you need to keep the sides shorter while leaving the top longer. The trick is to blow-dry your hair upwards and then back for that iconic puffed look.**

This style works great for both formal events and casual outings, giving you a versatile, polished look that stands out.

**@[Dugout Barber Salon](https://web.facebook.com/dugoutbarber?__cft__[0]=AZVDCfLOwbvBO3iCGYyJqxYd4yfomsr8mgmiI2rtHvz19gPLKt75f_Y1JHJb2ohIGD8vQNnKF4pedfp6i10y8jId3WJVzx1QcJDuWdMxW0b6Tc30t4RPSDdBGTmXjwXnKoU2C1IklhB37uDx0oxGN4QE8I4Yx0qblortQb1bAmB9aJ9Qoehvndr5EpKegwugrjLdSlYFfmfdLRjcldn4Fq42&__tn__=-UC%2CP-R)**

An undercut is perfect for those who want a bold, edgy look. With thick hair, you can make this style pop.

**The sides and back are shaved short while the top is left long, allowing for a variety of styling options. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy – the choice is yours.**

This style is versatile and can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

### 3. Quiff

![](/images/mens-hairstyles-thick-hair/20-Mens-Hairstyles-Thick-Hair-2-683x1024.jpg)

**@[Momentum Hair Salon](https://web.facebook.com/momentumsalon?__cft__[0]=AZVkDE1lYS0Iepposip_4ZA11IM4lVK5bSCKZ85gLddadXtmXpKuC6vUEo3zJPVrX8OsYss4o3MB6ypVdt2XcFu86nYvb7KdqF2dUA8jvlimwPgO7RpdfJQx3yBmIM2Ebm7cvAstB2Q8o7eutP9y9XuLAObW35o0UMCRq-MZGSTJEHTJ4XjXWXB5PgnTC3EO-Bt11Or-zdaQYL1L8pwUCcz6&__tn__=-UC%2CP-R)**

The quiff is a stylish, slightly tousled look that adds a bit of edge to your appearance. It’s similar to the pompadour but more relaxed.

**To style a quiff, you’ll want to blow-dry your hair while brushing it upwards and back. Use a lightweight styling cream to keep it in place without making it look too stiff.**

This style is perfect for both casual and semi-formal settings, giving you a fresh, modern look.

### 4. Slick Back

![](/images/mens-hairstyles-thick-hair/20-Mens-Hairstyles-Thick-Hair-3-683x1024.jpg)

**@[24kfades](https://www.instagram.com/24kfades/)**

If you’re aiming for a sleek and sophisticated look, the slick back is a great option. It’s simple yet stylish, making it ideal for both professional and social settings.

**To achieve this style, apply a strong-hold gel or pomade to your hair and comb it back until it’s smooth and shiny.**

This look works exceptionally well with thick hair as it holds its shape better. It’s a classic look that’s easy to maintain and always looks polished.

### 5. Fade

![](/images/mens-hairstyles-thick-hair/20-Mens-Hairstyles-Thick-Hair-4-683x1024.jpg)

**@[volodimir\_barber](https://www.instagram.com/volodimir_barber/)**

The fade is one of the most versatile and popular haircuts out there. It’s characterized by a gradual transition from short hair at the sides and back to longer hair on top.

**You can combine it with almost any hairstyle, from a pompadour to a quiff or even a buzz cut. The best part? It’s super easy to maintain.**

Whether you prefer a high, mid, or low fade, this style will give you a clean, modern look.
