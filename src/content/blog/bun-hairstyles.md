## What is Bun Hairstyles?

A bun hairstyle is a **versatile and classic way to style the hair by pulling it back from the face,** twisting it around itself, and securing it in a circular coil at the back or top of the head.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/bun-hairstyles-you-must-try-in-2025-683x1024.webp)

Buns **can be styled in various ways, making them suitable for both casual** and formal occasions.

**_Here are a few reasons why bun hairstyles are popular:_**

1.  **Practicality**: Buns keep hair tidy and out of the way, which is especially useful during physical activities, hot weather, or when focusing on work.
2.  **Versatility**: There are many variations of the bun, from sleek, polished ballerina buns to relaxed, messy buns. Each style can convey a different mood and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.
3.  **Ease of Creation**: While some bun styles require more effort and skill, many basic buns can be quickly and easily fashioned with just a few hair ties and pins.
4.  **Fashion Statement**: Buns have been a part of fashion trends for centuries and continue to be adapted in modern hairstyling, often incorporating elements like braids, twists, and accessories.
5.  **Suitability for All Hair Types**: Buns can be created in almost any hair length (except very short hair) and texture, making them a universal hairstyle option.

Bun hairstyles are a f**unctional, stylish, and enduring choice for anyone looking to manage** their hair efficiently while still looking great.

## 25 Bun Hairstyles for Copy ### 1. Classic Ballerina Bun

The Classic Ballerina Bun is your go-to for a sleek, polished look that screams elegance. You can achieve this by pulling your hair back tightly and securing it high on your head.

**This bun is perfect for formal events or when you want to channel your inner prima ballerina.**

Don’t forget to smooth out any flyaways with some hairspray for that flawless finish!

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/sadwasdsadsa-771x1024.png)

### 2. Messy Bun

If you’re going for an effortlessly chic vibe, **[the Messy Bun is your best friend](https://www.scantotalk.com/messy-bun/)**. Simply gather your hair as if you’re making a ponytail, twist it loosely, and secure it with a hair tie.

Let some strands fall freely to frame your face. This style is super versatile and perfect for casual outings or when you just want to relax at home.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/tyftydtgdfgdfgdfg-771x1024.png)

### 3. Low Chignon

For a touch of sophistication, try the Low Chignon. This bun sits at the nape of your neck and works wonders for any formal gathering.

You can smooth your hair back or add some gentle twists before securing the bun. Pair it with elegant earrings, and you’re ready to steal the show at any upscale event.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/szsadsadsadasdas-2-771x1024.png)

### 4. Top Knot

The Top Knot is fantastic for keeping your hair out of your face while still looking ultra-stylish. Pull your hair up to the crown of your head and secure it into a tight bun.

This look is not only practical but also adds a modern twist to your outfits, making it perfect for both a day at the office and a night out.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/joijiohihskhsakjdhskjadsa-1-771x1024.png)

### 5. Braided Bun

Add some flair to your bun with a braid. Start by braiding your hair from any point, side, back, or top, and wrap the braid around to form a bun.

Secure with pins. This style adds a bohemian touch to your look and is great for occasions when you want to stand out a bit more.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/dfgkdfjlkgfdksdjkfhsdjkfd-771x1024.png)

### 6. Twisted Bun

The Twisted Bun is all about texture. Twist sections of your hair and wrap them around each other before pinning them into a bun.

This technique gives your hair a more dimensional look, perfect for both formal and casual settings. It’s a fantastic way to add a twist to a simple bun!

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/fgdgfbfdgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 7. Half-Up Bun

Can’t decide between having your hair up or down? Go for the Half-Up Bun. This style allows you to enjoy the best of both worlds.

Pull the top half of your hair into a bun and let the rest cascade down your back. It’s playful, easy to do, and works great for any occasion.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/fdgfgerwerwerwerew-771x1024.png)

### 8. Space Buns

Embrace your quirky side with Space Buns! Part your hair down the middle, create two high ponytails, and twist each into a bun.

Secure with pins or hair ties. This style is super fun and youthful, perfect for festivals, parties, or when you’re just feeling a little adventurous.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/rdgfdgfdgdtert454354frgfdgf-771x1024.png)

### 9. Sock Bun

For a bun that’s perfectly round and voluminous, try the Sock Bun. Cut the toe off a sock, roll it into a doughnut shape, and place your ponytail through it.

Spread your hair around the sock and secure it. This technique gives you a chic, polished bun that’s ideal for professional settings or formal events.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/rtgfdgfdgretretertrtretre-771x1024.png)

### 10. Side Bun

Switch things up with a Side Bun, positioned at one side of your head. This bun is effortlessly stylish and can be as loose or tight as you prefer.

It’s great for a romantic dinner date or a casual day out. Add some small braids or twists before securing the bun for an extra touch of elegance.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/asfdwerewrwerwerwerwe-771x1024.png)

### 11. Dutch Braid into Bun

The Dutch Braid into Bun starts with a neat braid at the nape of your neck that ascends into a sophisticated bun.

**It’s a wonderful choice for weddings or formal events where you want to add a unique twist to your outfit.**

You can enhance this look with decorative pins or flowers for that extra special touch.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/sdfwerewrwerwerwer-771x1024.png)

### 12. Double Dutch Buns

For a playful and symmetrical style, try Double Dutch Buns. Begin with two Dutch braids at the sides of your head and twist each into a bun.

This style is energetic and fun, perfect for outdoor concerts, workouts, or any time you want to add a little joy to your day.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/fiosdjuweoikjsdkjffsd-771x1024.png)

### 13. Bubble Bun

The Bubble Bun is unique and eye-catching, formed by creating sections of the bun that puff out like bubbles.

**To achieve this, tie your hair into a ponytail, add elastic bands every few inches, and gently pull the hair to form bubbles.**

It’s a great conversation starter and adds a playful element to your look.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/fdhgiuhrfjkdn-fbmsdhfsdfsdf-771x1024.png)

### 14. Looped Bun

The Looped Bun offers an elegant alternative to the traditional bun. Instead of wrapping your hair through the hair tie, leave it looped to create a sophisticated half-bun shape.

This style works beautifully for business meetings or formal dinners, giving you a polished and refined appearance.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/isodfwejhksdhfkjsdfsdf-1-771x1024.png)

### 15. Pinned Curls Bun

Create a Pinned Curls Bun by first curling your hair with a curling iron and then pinning the curls loosely at the back of your head.

This style provides volume and texture, making it perfect for those who want a glamorous yet slightly undone look. It’s especially stunning for evening events or a night out.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/sdkjfhsdfuyweriefsdfsd-771x1024.png)

### 16. Fishtail Braid Bun

Incorporate a fishtail braid into your bun for an intricate, detailed look. Start by creating a fishtail braid and then wrap it into a bun at the back of your head.

This style is artistic and sophisticated, suitable for occasions when you want your hair to be a focal point.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/ksdfjifiiojijiewjrksdndnnfds-771x1024.png)

### 17. Bow Bun

The Bow Bun transforms your hair into a charming bow shape at the back of your head.

**This whimsical style is surprisingly easy to create and is perfect for parties or when you’re feeling playful.**

Pair it with a cute outfit, and you’re ready to make a sweet and stylish statement.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/jsdiofweirdksfjsdifdshfkjsdf-771x1024.png)

### 18. French Twist Bun

A French Twist Bun is a more intricate variant of the classic twist. Gather your hair, twist it up tightly, and tuck the ends under before pinning.

This style exudes an air of classic French elegance, ideal for formal settings or when you want to showcase timeless grace.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/uyuityutytrttfgffhjfgghfgdfg-771x1024.png)

### 19. Pull-Through Bun

The Pull-Through Bun looks complex but is quite easy to execute. Create a ponytail, split it into sections, and pull each section through the last to create a layered, braid-like effect.

This style is dynamic and modern, great for those days when you want your hair to look both polished and interesting.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/oisdfjwejhrkjdhsrkjhsdfjsdkfsdf-771x1024.png)

### 20. Crown Bun

For a regal look, try the Crown Bun. This style involves creating a bun that encircles your head like a crown.

It’s particularly lovely for bridal hair or any occasion where you want to feel like royalty. Accessorize with small flowers or jewels for an even more majestic look.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/dskjfh8eohrewhfkjsdhfkjdsfsd-771x1024.png)

### 21. Knotted Bun

For a texturally rich and interesting style, try the Knotted Bun.

**This involves taking sections of your hair, knotting them together, and then securing the resulting knots into a bun at the back of your head.**

This style is great for adding a rustic or bohemian flair to your look, making it perfect for outdoor weddings or artsy events.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/oisdfjweiurhfdshfkjsdfhuysdhfdsfds-771x1024.png)

### 22. Scarf Bun

Inject some color and texture into your hairstyle with the Scarf Bun. Simply tie a scarf around your bun, letting the ends flow freely or tucking them in for a neater appearance.

This adds a vibrant touch to your ensemble and can be a great way to match your hairstyle with your outfit, ideal for summer picnics or a beach day.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/ksdfjiowejrsehfkjsdhfkjsdhfkjsdfds-771x1024.png)

### 23. Bun with Bangs

The Bun with Bangs combines the neatness of a bun with the charm of bangs framing your face.

**Whether you opt for straight, curtain, or side-swept bangs, this style can suit any face shape and adds a soft, approachable element to your look.**

It’s perfect for casual coffee dates or when you want to soften a more formal outfit.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/zxcxzfesdfsdfsdfsdfds-771x1024.png)

### 24. Waterfall Bun

The Waterfall Bun features strands of hair cascading around the bun, creating a beautiful waterfall effect.

**Start by pulling your hair into a ponytail, create a loose bun, and then pull small sections of hair to drape around the bun.**

This elegant hairstyle is fantastic for romantic dinners or sophisticated social gatherings.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/nsdhijfhsdjhjdkshfsdkhlfkljsdafhdakjfdsa-771x1024.png)

### 25. Upside Down Braid Bun

Begin with an adventurous twist by braiding your hair from the bottom up (starting at the nape and working towards the crown) and then finishing it off with a bun on top.

The Upside Down Braid Bun adds a playful yet chic look to your styling options, making it a standout choice for fitness classes or when you’re up for an inverted, fun approach to traditional hairstyles.

![](/images/bun-hairstyles/dsmnfxkvheiuwrhsdfhkjsdhfkjsdfdsfd-771x1024.png)
