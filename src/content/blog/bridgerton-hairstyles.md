## What is Bridgerton Hairstyles?

“Bridgerton” hairstyles are **inspired by the [Netflix series “Bridgerton,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bridgerton)“** which is set during the Regency era in England.

**This period is known for its distinctive fashion and, of course, its unique hairstyles, which are characterized by their elegance, intricacy, and often voluminous forms.**

![](/images/bridgerton-hairstyles/Bridgerton-Hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

_These styles include an array of updos, curls, and braids adorned with accessories like ribbons, pearls, and floral decorations._

**Let’s talk about how you can achieve one of these quintessential “Bridgerton” designs—the classic Regency updo.**

### Here’s a straightforward step-by-step guide to creating a Regency updo:

1.  **Prep Your Hair**: Start with clean, dry hair. For best results, use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair some lift and texture, which will help in styling.
2.  **Create a Base**: If your hair lacks volume, consider using a bumpit or a small cushion at the crown of your head to create that iconic Regency poof. Secure it with bobby pins.
3.  **Section Your Hair**: Divide your hair into sections. A typical approach is to leave out some strands at the front to frame your face later and then work with the bulk of your hair at the back.
4.  **Backcomb**: Gently backcomb the sections at the back of your head to add volume. This is crucial for getting that full, lush look typical of the era.
5.  **Form the Updo**: Pull your hair back and twist it into a low chignon or bun at the nape of your neck. Don’t worry about getting it perfectly smooth; a bit of texture adds to the charm.
6.  **Curl the Front Sections**: Use a curling iron to curl the strands you left out at the front. Depending on your preference, you can create tight ringlets or loose waves. Pin these curls lightly around your face or tuck them behind your ears.
7.  **Add Accessories**: No “Bridgerton” hairstyle is complete without accessories. Pin a decorative hair comb, pearl pins, or even fresh flowers into your updo. Choose something that complements your outfit and the occasion.
8.  **Set Your Style**: Finally, spray your hair with a strong hold hairspray to ensure your updo stays put throughout your event.

And there you have it—a “Bridgerton” hairstyle that’s both beautiful and not too difficult to achieve.
