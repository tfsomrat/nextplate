Medium-length hair offers a perfect balance of versatility and manageability, making it an ideal canvas for a variety of stunning hairstyles.

Whether you’re aiming for a casual, everyday look or something more polished for special occasions, medium-length hair allows for effortless experimentation.

**In this article, we unveil 25 easy yet stylish hairstyles that will elevate your medium-length hair, allowing you to express your unique personality and style with minimal effort.**

Get ready to discover a world of chic and convenient hairdos that are perfect for any occasion!

## 25 Easy Hairstyles to Try on Medium-Length Hairs ### 1. Loose Waves

Achieve a relaxed look with loose waves by using a curling iron to create soft curls. Gently run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up. This effortless style suits any occasion, adding a casual elegance to your appearance.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/ghytytrytr-771x1024.png)

### 2. Half-Up, Half-Down

Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip, leaving the rest loose. This classic style keeps hair off your face while showcasing your locks. It’s simple yet versatile for various settings.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/ioijkjlkj-771x1024.png)

### 3. Messy Bun

Create a chic messy bun by pulling your hair into a loose bun and allowing some strands to fall naturally. Ideal for lazy days or when you’re on the go, this style is effortlessly stylish and casual.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/kihihoioijhoi-771x1024.png)

### 4. Low Ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie for a low ponytail. This simple, sleek, and versatile hairstyle fits both professional and casual environments effortlessly.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/ljkjkjkjkl-771x1024.png)

### 5. Top Knot

For a chic look, style your hair into a high top knot. Gather your hair into a high bun on top of your head. This practical hairstyle keeps hair out of your way while adding sophistication and style.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/oiouoiuiuoi-771x1024.png)

### 6. Braided Crown

Create a crown effect by braiding small sections from each side of your head and pinning them at the back. This elegant hairstyle adds a regal touch, perfect for special occasions or elevating everyday looks.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/jhkjkkkjkjh-771x1024.png)

### 7. Side Braid

Braid your hair to one side and secure it with a hair tie for an elegant side braid. This quick and easy style adds a refined touch, suitable for both casual outings and formal events.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/joijjjlk-771x1024.png)

### 8. Beach Waves

Apply sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch with your hands to achieve beach waves. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser for added volume. This style offers a relaxed, summer-ready vibe.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/jkjlkjlkj-771x1024.png)

### 9. Straight and Sleek

Use a flat iron to achieve straight and sleek hair, adding a shine serum for extra gloss. This polished and professional look is ideal for the office or anytime you need to appear put-together and sophisticated.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/jhkjhhkhjk-771x1024.png)

### 10. Twisted Half-Up

Twist two sections from the front of your hair and pin them at the back for a twisted half-up style. This simple yet elegant look is perfect for casual outings or romantic occasions.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/lkjlkjlkjlk-771x1024.png)

### 11. French Twist

Roll your hair into a twist at the back of your head and secure it with bobby pins for a French twist. This classic and sophisticated hairstyle is ideal for formal occasions, exuding elegance.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/ererere-1-771x1024.png)

### 12. Fishtail Braid

Create a stylish fishtail braid by dividing your hair into two sections and alternately crossing small pieces over to the other side. This intricate-looking braid adds a unique twist to your everyday style.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/gfdgfgfdgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 13. Double Dutch Braids

Start braiding two sections from the front of your head, working your way to the back for double Dutch braids. This trendy and practical style keeps hair out of your face and adds a sporty vibe.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/ghghghgh-771x1024.png)

### 14. Ponytail with a Twist

Twist sections of your hair before incorporating them into a ponytail for a unique look. This quick and easy style adds a fun twist to a classic ponytail, making it a bit different yet simple.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/dsfdsfdsfsd-771x1024.png)

### 15. Space Buns

Divide your hair into two sections and create a bun on each side of your head for space buns. This playful and trendy hairstyle is perfect for festivals, parties, or adventurous looks.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/erererererererere-771x1024.png)

### 16. Sleek Low Bun

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it into a tight bun for a sleek low bun. This sophisticated and polished hairstyle is ideal for formal occasions, providing an elegant appearance.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/bcvbcbcbcv-771x1024.png)

### 17. Boho Braids

Create small braids throughout your hair for a bohemian touch. This laid-back, festival-ready style adds a whimsical and carefree vibe, perfect for summer or casual outings.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/mjhjhjhjh-771x1024.png)

### 18. Waterfall Braid

Let sections of your hair fall as you braid to create a cascading effect for a waterfall braid. This beautiful and unique style is ideal for special occasions, offering an elegant touch.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/hgnghvb-771x1024.png)

### 19. Headband Tuck

Place a headband over your hair and tuck the ends into the band for a quick and easy updo. This chic and polished look is created in minutes, perfect for when you’re in a rush.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/nmnmnmnm-771x1024.png)

### 20. Pinned Back Waves

Curl your hair to create soft waves and pin back small sections from the front for pinned-back waves. This romantic and easy hairstyle adds elegance to both casual and formal settings.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/nbnbnbvnvbnv-771x1024.png)

### 21. Bubble Ponytail

Tie your hair into a ponytail and add more hair ties down the length, puffing out each section to create “bubbles.” This fun and eye-catching style is perfect for trying something different.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/jjjhjhghgjg-771x1024.png)

### 22. Vintage Curls

Use a curling iron to create tight curls and then brush them out slightly for vintage curls. This retro-inspired look adds old Hollywood glamour, making it ideal for special occasions.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/gngngngngng-771x1024.png)

### 23. Half-Up Bun

Gather the top half of your hair into a small bun and leave the rest down for a half-up bun. This trendy and easy hairstyle keeps hair out of your face while showcasing your length.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/ihhikhkhjhjh-771x1024.png)

### 24. Clipped Back

Use stylish clips to pin back sections of your hair from the front for a clipped-back look. This quick and stylish hairstyle adds a decorative touch, perfect for a simple yet chic appearance.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/lkuukjkjh-771x1024.png)

### 25. Voluminous Blowout

Use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and bounce for a voluminous blowout. This glamorous look is perfect for special events or when you want to feel put-together and luxurious.

![](/images/easy-hairstyles-for-medium-length-hair/gngfhfgfgfdgfdgfdgfdgfdgfdgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)
