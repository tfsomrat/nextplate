Witchy hairstyles have always been more than just a statement they are an embodiment of mystery, magic, and individual style.

Whether you’re drawn to dark and edgy vibes or prefer a more ethereal and nature-connected look, **witchy hairstyles** let you express your inner sorceress with flair.

![](/images/witchy-hairstyles/witchy-hairstyles-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ll explore 21 enchanting witchy hairstyles that range from rebellious mullets to soft, romantic waves.**

Each of these looks incorporates elements of magic whether it’s through bold color, intricate braids, or playful accessories.

## Witchy Hairstyles

![](/images/witchy-hairstyles/Witchy-Hairstyles-ideas-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Split Black and Silver Witchy Waves

This two-toned hairstyle is perfect for a **witchy vibe**. The hair is parted down the middle, one side is jet **black** while the other is a contrasting **silver**.

The blend of dark and light creates an enchanting and ethereal effect. To achieve this look, start by dyeing half of your hair black and the other half silver or platinum. Use a large barrel curling iron to create loose, voluminous **waves**.

This hairstyle works best with longer hair and can be styled with subtle hair accessories like crystal pins or moon clips to enhance the **witchy aesthetic**.

![](/images/witchy-hairstyles/image-376.jpeg)

### 2. Edgy Green and Brown Buzz Cut

For a more daring witchy look, try an **edgy buzz cut** with a touch of **green magic**. This hairstyle features a close buzz cut on the sides with a little length left at the back.

The **brown base** is accentuated with neon **green streaks** on the top, creating a unique and rebellious witchy look.

Achieving this requires shaving the sides short while leaving a slight fringe to dye the green sections. It’s a low-maintenance style but packs a powerful punch.

Pair this hairstyle with **bold makeup** and witchy accessories like a spiked choker or pentagram earrings to enhance the gothic vibe, making it a perfect option for someone embracing their darker, mystical side.

![](/images/witchy-hairstyles/image-377.jpeg)

### 3. Electric Blue Witchy Curls

If you want to channel your inner **witch** with a bold color, go for **electric blue curls**. This vibrant blue hue paired with **textured, voluminous curls** creates an otherworldly look.

Achieve this by dyeing your hair an intense shade of **blue** and then using a small curling iron to create tight, voluminous curls that are full of texture.

This witchy hairstyle looks stunning with dramatic **eye makeup** and dark attire, adding a touch of magic and mystery to your look.

The bright color paired with the bold curls will give you a **sorceress vibe**, perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hairstyle.

### 4. Yellow and Purple Witchy Bob

This quirky **witchy bob** combines a striking color palette of **yellow and purple**. The hair is cut into a short bob with straight **bangs**, dyed a vivid yellow at the top with a bold purple underneath.

The two-tone color gives a mystical, otherworldly vibe. To achieve this look, bleach the hair before applying yellow on top and purple on the bottom sections.

The **blunt bangs** add to the edgy feel, making it a standout style for witches who want to be noticed.

Pair it with dark, mystical makeup to emphasize the contrast between the colors and complete your **witchy transformation**.

### 5. Soft Pink Witchy Waves with a Victorian Hat

This **witchy soft pink hairstyle** exudes charm and elegance. The long, voluminous waves in a pastel pink shade create a soft, ethereal look that feels straight out of a magical world.

Paired with a **Victorian-style wide-brimmed hat**, this hairstyle brings an old-world charm mixed with mystical allure. To achieve this look, dye your hair a soft pastel pink, then curl your hair with a large curling iron for loose, romantic waves.

Top the style off with a delicate **Victorian hat** adorned with lace or flowers to add a whimsical, witchy touch. This style works well for someone wanting a dreamy yet magical aesthetic for a Halloween or themed event.

### 6. Pixie Mullet with Dark Roots

This **pixie mullet** combines edgy modernity with witchy allure. The hair is cut short on top, creating a tousled **pixie-like effect** with longer mullet-style layers at the back.

The top is dyed in a soft **pastel pink**, while the **dark roots** and tips add contrast, giving the hairstyle a shadowy, mystical edge. To create this look, ask your stylist for a short, textured pixie with longer back layers.

Add pastel dye to the top while keeping the roots dark to maintain that **contrasting effect**. This hairstyle’s edginess works perfectly with bold makeup, like sharp eyeliner or face tattoos, adding a modern, witchy twist to your look.

### 7. Sleek Black Witchy Bob with Bangs

### 8. Layered White and Purple Mullet

### 9. Lilac Mermaid Waves
