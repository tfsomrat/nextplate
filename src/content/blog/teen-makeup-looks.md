## 23 Teen Makeup Looks ### 1. Natural Glow

For a fresh-faced look, go for a natural glow. You can achieve this by using a dewy foundation or BB cream to even out your complexion.

**Add a soft pink blush to the apples of your cheeks and fill in your eyebrows with a light brown pencil.**

Finish with a hint of clear lip gloss and a touch of mascara for a radiant, everyday look.

![](/images/teen-makeup-looks/sdwerufgbyufgjsdfds-771x1024.png)

### 2. Bold and Bright

Feeling daring? Try a bold and bright makeup look! Start with vibrant turquoise eyeshadow, blending it across your eyelids.

**Add a fierce winged eyeliner to make your eyes pop. For your lips, choose a hot pink lipstick that stands out.**

Lightly contour your cheeks and sharply define your brows for a look that’s sure to turn heads.

![](/images/teen-makeup-looks/mvoihjweiorjkjhfsdfd-771x1024.png)

### 3. Pastel Princess

Channel your inner pastel princess with this soft and dreamy makeup look. Use a light lavender eyeshadow for a delicate touch on your eyelids.

![](/images/teen-makeup-looks/dmsoifwepoifjdshfuweorfew-771x1024.png)

### 4. Goth Glam

If you’re into darker aesthetics, goth glam is for you. Use a pale foundation to create a flawless base, then go dramatic with black lipstick and heavy eyeliner.
