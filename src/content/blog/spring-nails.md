Spring is in the air, and it’s the perfect time to refresh your nail game with designs that capture the season’s vibrant spirit.

As flowers start to bloom and colors around us come alive, spring nail art embraces these elements, mixing pastel hues, floral patterns, and playful accents to mirror the beauty of nature.

![](/images/spring-nails/spring-nails-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, 25 Spring Nail Designs, we’ve curated a collection of eye-catching and unique ideas that cater to every style from delicate daisy patterns to bold neon swirls, and everything in between.**

Whether you’re looking to keep it subtle with soft ombre tips or go all out with intricate floral blooms, these designs are sure to inspire. Ready to give your nails a seasonal update?

## Spring Nail Designs

![](/images/spring-nails/Spring-Nail-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Pastel Cherry French Tips

This design combines **soft green French tips** with cute cherry details on some nails for a playful spring vibe. The **nude base** keeps it understated, while the green adds a fresh look, perfect for the season. To create this style, start with a **nude or light pink polish** base.

Once dry, apply the green tips using nail tape or a fine brush. Add the cherry details with a thin brush or dotting tool small red dots for cherries and green stems for leaves.

Finish with a **top coat** for shine and durability. This design balances simplicity with a fun twist, ideal for a refreshing spring manicure.

### 2. Sunshine Daisies

These nails feature **bright yellow French tips** complemented by daisy art on two nails, perfect for a cheerful spring look.

This combination is bright yet delicate, perfect for celebrating the spring season. Finish with a top coat to seal the design and add shine, ensuring the manicure stays fresh and vibrant.
