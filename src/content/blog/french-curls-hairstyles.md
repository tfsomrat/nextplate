When it comes to hairstyles that offer the perfect blend of elegance, volume, and versatility, **French curls** are a timeless choice.

**Whether incorporated into braids or worn loose, French curls add a soft, romantic touch to any hairstyle.**

![](/images/french-curls-hairstyles/French-Curls-Hairstyles-1-1024x614.jpg)

This look has gained popularity across various hair textures and styles, thanks to its ability to elevate everyday hairstyles and add a glamorous finish.

**In this article, we’ve curated 25 stunning French curls hairstyles that highlight the versatility and beauty of this timeless look.**

From braided styles with curled ends to voluminous loose curls, you’ll find inspiration for a variety of occasions.

## French Curls Hairstyles

![](/images/french-curls-hairstyles/French-Curls-Hairstyles-ideas-for-2025-683x1024.webp)

### 1. French Curls With Ombre Ends

This hairstyle showcases **small, neat braids** transitioning into loose, voluminous curls towards the ends, with an **ombre effect** from black to a rich brown shade. The French curls add a soft bounce to the ends, giving the style a flowy look.

To achieve this style, **braid the hair from roots to mid-length** and leave the ends unbraided for curling. Using a **curling iron or flexi rods**, create loose curls to finish off the ends for a natural yet glamorous look. This hairstyle is perfect for anyone wanting to add a chic, playful element to their braids.

To recreate this look, first **braid the hair** and gather it into a high ponytail. Use **curling tools or rollers** to create large, flowing curls for the remaining hair. This hairstyle is ideal for those looking for a **bold yet elegant** look for special occasions.
