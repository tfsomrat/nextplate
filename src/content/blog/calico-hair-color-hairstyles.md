**Calico hair color hairstyles** draw inspiration from the unique, multi-toned fur patterns of calico cats.

Just like the distinctive patchwork of black, orange, and white on a calico cat, these hairstyles feature **bold contrasts and blended hues** to create a stunning, eye-catching look.

![](/images/calico-hair-color-hairstyles/Calico-Hair-Color-Hairstyles-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 22 stunning calico hair color ideas that range from bold split-dye designs to soft, flowing waves with peek-a-boo highlights.**

Whether you want to go for a high-contrast look or prefer a more subtle multi-tonal blend, these calico-inspired styles will offer the perfect touch of individuality and flair to your hair.

## Calico Hair Color Hairstyles

![](/images/calico-hair-color-hairstyles/Calico-Hair-Color-Hairstyles-ideas--683x1024.webp)

### 1. Calico Blend Waves with Bold Orange and Blonde

This **Calico-inspired** look features bold sections of **fiery orange, platinum blonde, and dark brown**, creating a striking contrast.

The hair is styled in soft waves, with the orange and blonde shades framing the face while the darker tones add depth toward the back.

This color combination mimics the patchy yet elegant style of a calico cat. To achieve this look, partition the hair into distinct sections, apply the bold colors separately, and blend them slightly for a natural transition.
