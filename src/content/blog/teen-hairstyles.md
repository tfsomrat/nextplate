Are you ever stumped on how to style your hair for school, hangouts, or special events?

**Do you find yourself scrolling endlessly through Pinterest or Instagram, searching for hairstyles that are both trendy and achievable without a trip to the salon?**

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/Teen-Hairstyles-Easily-Copy-in-2024-576x1024.jpg)

_Well, I’ve been there too, and I know how frustrating it can be when you want to switch up your look but just don’t know where to start._

**That’s why I’ve put together this list of 22 teen girl hairstyles that are not only super stylish but also easy enough to try out at home.**

Whether you’re looking for something effortlessly chic for everyday wear or a bit more polished for a special occasion, these ideas are sure to inspire you and solve your hair dilemmas!

## 22 Teen Hairstyles Easily Copy in 2025 ### 1. Messy Bun with Scrunchie

You can never go wrong with a messy bun, especially when paired with a fun scrunchie.

**This look is not only effortless but also super cute, perfect for when you’re studying or chilling with friends.**

Just pull your hair back, let a few strands loose for that carefree vibe, and secure it with your favorite scrunchie.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/lksdhkjsdhd-771x1024.png)

### 2. Half-up, Half-down Waves

For a hairstyle that’s as breezy as it is beautiful, try the half-up, half-down look with soft waves.

It’s great for showing off your locks while keeping hair out of your face. You can easily dress this style up with some sparkly clips or keep it casual for a day at the beach.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/kjfdksdjfsd-771x1024.png)

### 3. Side Braided Ponytail

Switch up your regular ponytail by adding a side braid into the mix. It’s simple: just braid a small section of your hair on one side, then pull everything into a ponytail.

This tiny twist adds a whole lot of style to an otherwise basic look, making you stand out effortlessly.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/tytrytrerter-771x1024.png)

### 4. Bubble Braids

Bubble braids are as fun to wear as they sound! This playful style involves sectioning your ponytail with hair ties to create ‘bubbles’.

It’s quirky, cool, and surprisingly easy to pull off. Plus, you can use colorful hair ties to make it even more vibrant.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/dksjfdsafoisda-771x1024.png)

### 5. Space Buns

Channel your inner space goddess with cute space buns. This style is perfect for festivals or any day you’re feeling a bit extra.

Simply part your hair down the middle, twist each section into a bun, and secure. Feel free to add some glitter for a starry effect!

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/sddsfsdds-771x1024.png)

### 6. French Braid Pigtails

Give your pigtails a French twist! Start braiding from the top and work your way down.

Not only does this look adorable, but it also keeps your hair neat all day long. Whether you’re hitting the books or the courts, these braids have got you covered.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/rtretertre-771x1024.png)

### 7. Sleek High Ponytail

A sleek high ponytail is your go-to for when you need a chic yet sporty look. It pulls your hair back from your face and elevates your entire outfit.

Whether you’re heading to a workout or a lunch date, this ponytail will keep you looking polished.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/jfgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 8. Textured Lob

If you’re looking for a low-maintenance but trendy hairstyle, the textured lob is perfect.

It works well with natural waves or you can add a bit of curl for extra volume. It’s incredibly versatile and chic, making it ideal for any occasion.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/sdasadsa-771x1024.png)

### 9. Curtain Bangs with Layers

Curtain bangs paired with layers give your hair a soft, romantic flair. This style frames your face beautifully and adds movement to your locks.

You can blow them out for a sleek look or let them air-dry for a more natural vibe.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/dsfsddsfdsfsdfds-771x1024.png)

### 10. Boxer Braids

Boxer braids are not only stylish but also practical, especially if you lead an active lifestyle.

They keep your hair tightly secured and out of the way, plus they look super cool. You can rock them at the gym or when hanging out with friends.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/dfgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 11. Beach Waves with Highlights

Nothing says summer like beach waves with some highlights. This style gives you that perfect sun-kissed look, making it seem like you’ve just come back from a fabulous beach vacation.

It’s effortless and works for just about any setting.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/lkfdjglkjfjdgj-771x1024.png)

### 12. Pin-Straight Layers

For a sleek and simple style, try pin-straight layers. This look is straightforward but always in style, giving your hair a smooth, silky appearance.

Use a flat iron to achieve that ultra-straight look and a serum to add shine.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/fdgifdjuosd-771x1024.png)

### 13. Top Knot with Wispy Bangs

The top knot with wispy bangs is both cute and functional. It keeps your hair off your neck on those hot days while the bangs add a touch of whimsy.

This hairstyle is perfect for both lazy days at home and outings with friends.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/fdgdfgfdfd-771x1024.png)

### 14. Colorful Streaks

Add some excitement to your hair with colorful streaks. Whether you go for temporary dyes, chalks, or extensions, this pop of color can express your personality and mood without a long-term commitment. It’s a fun way to spice up your look for a special event or just any day.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/sdlkfjkdsflksd-771x1024.png)

### 15. Boho Braids

Embrace a carefree, bohemian style with random, small boho braids throughout your hair.

Intertwine these with your natural locks and adorn them with tiny beads or flowers for that extra touch. It’s perfect for festivals or just a day out in the sun.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/xzcxzcxzcxz-771x1024.png)

### 16. Waterfall Braid

The waterfall braid is stunning and not as complicated as it looks. It creates a beautiful cascading effect, like a waterfall, which works beautifully with both straight and curly hair.

This braid is ideal for dates, parties, or just when you want to feel a bit fancy.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/reroierowewe-771x1024.png)

### 17. Twist-Out Curls

For those with natural curls, a twist-out is a fantastic way to define and control your curls.

**Simply twist damp hair in sections, let it dry, and then untwist for defined and bouncy curls.**

This method highlights the natural beauty of your hair while keeping it manageable.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/dfgfdgfdgsdwerwerer-771x1024.png)

### 18. Dutch Braid Crown

A Dutch braid crown not only looks regal but is also great for keeping your hair out of your face.

**Start by Dutch braiding along the hairline and wrap the braid around your head.**

This elegant look is perfect for weddings, proms, or any occasion where you want to feel a bit more dressed up.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/iytryrtytryrtry-771x1024.png)

### 19. Low Bun with Decorative Clips

A low bun is a classic go-to for a quick and elegant style. Add some flair with decorative clips or pins.

This can be your secret weapon for turning a bad hair day into a stylish look in just minutes.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/oeritoretoreioerr-771x1024.png)

### 20. Pixie Cut with Long Bangs

If you’re feeling bold, why not try a pixie cut with long bangs? It’s edgy, easy to maintain, and perfect for showcasing your features.

Plus, it’s incredibly liberating to chop off your long locks and embrace a minimalist style.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/werijoiefsdfsd-771x1024.png)

### 21. Layered Shag

The layered shag is all about creating texture and volume. It’s a carefree look that’s perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks styled.

With just a bit of mousse or texturizing spray, you’re good to go.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/fdoigjmfodjgkoifdg-771x1024.png)

### 22. Slicked-Back Hair

For a look that’s both sleek and dramatic, try slicking back your hair. It’s a great style for formal events or when you want to add a bit of glamour to your outfit.

Use some gel or pomade to get that sleek, shiny finish.

![](/images/teen-hairstyles/ewroweiuruwefhusdhfsd-771x1024.png)
