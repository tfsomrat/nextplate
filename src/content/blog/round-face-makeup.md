## Tips for Round Face Makeup

**Achieving the perfect makeup look for a round face is all about accentuating your natural features and adding dimension.**

![](/images/round-face-makeup/Space-Buns-Short-Hair-1-576x1024.jpg)

*   **Contour Strategically**: Use a matte bronzer to contour the sides of your forehead, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. This adds depth and creates a more sculpted look.
*   **Highlight the High Points**: Apply a highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your brow bones. This brings light to the center of your face, making it appear more elongated.
*   **Go for Bold Brows**: Well-defined eyebrows can frame your face beautifully. Keep them thick and slightly arched to add structure and lift to your overall look.
*   **Play with Eyeshadow**: Use darker shades on the outer corners of your eyes to create a more elongated shape. Light, shimmery shades on the inner corners and brow bones can open up your eyes.
*   **Blush Placement**: Apply blush slightly higher on your cheeks, rather than the apples, and blend it towards your temples. This technique can give the illusion of a more lifted face.
*   **Lip Focus**: Bold lip colors can draw attention away from the roundness of your face. Experiment with deep reds, vibrant pinks, or dramatic plums to add a focal point.
*   **Avoid Heavy Powder**: Too much powder can make your face look flat. Opt for a light, translucent powder only where needed to maintain a natural, glowing finish.
*   **Use a Matte Finish Foundation**: A matte foundation helps in reducing shine and can make your face appear slimmer. Avoid overly dewy foundations that can emphasize roundness.

With the incorporation of these tips into your makeup routine, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a more defined, balanced look.
