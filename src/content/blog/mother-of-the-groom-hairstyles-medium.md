As the mother of the groom, you play a significant role in your son’s wedding day, and naturally, you’ll want to look and feel your best.

**From the ceremony to the reception, the right hairstyle can enhance your entire look and complement your attire, while still allowing you to feel comfortable throughout the day.**

![](/images/mother-of-the-groom-hairstyles-medium/Mother-of-the-Groom-Hairstyles-Medium-1-1024x614.jpg)

Whether you’re seeking something elegant, classic, or with a modern twist, your hairstyle should reflect your personal style while suiting the formality of the event.

In this article, we’ve curated **22 stunning mother of the groom hairstyles** that range from timeless updos to romantic waves, ensuring there’s something for every mother to shine on her son’s big day.

## Mother of the Groom Hairstyles

![](/images/mother-of-the-groom-hairstyles-medium/Mother-of-the-Groom-Hairstyles-Medium-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Twisted Crown Updo with Elegant Bun

This **twisted crown updo** creates a beautiful, regal look perfect for a mother of the groom. The hair is divided into sections, twisted on both sides, and elegantly pinned to form a soft, voluminous crown. The back culminates in a **low bun**, adding sophistication to the style.

For added glamor, the look can be accessorized with a jeweled hairpiece. This hairstyle works best for medium-length hair and is ideal for formal weddings. To achieve this, **twist the hair tightly**, secure it at the nape, and use a hair accessory for an extra touch of elegance.

![](/images/mother-of-the-groom-hairstyles-medium/image-1024x1024.jpeg)

### 2. Textured Updo with Fascinator

This **textured updo** incorporates soft waves, creating a refined look that pairs well with a fascinator. The hair is softly pulled back with subtle curls framing the face, giving an air of elegance.

This style is perfect for a mother of the groom who plans to wear a **statement hat or fascinator**. To achieve this, loosely curl the hair for texture, then pin it into a soft updo at the back. Secure with hairspray and place a fascinator on top for an effortlessly chic appearance.

To style, blow-dry the bob straight with a deep side part and tuck one side behind the ear to showcase the undercut. This look is perfect for those who want something both polished and contemporary.
