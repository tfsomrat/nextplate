## What is Shag Haircut?

A shag haircut is a layered hairstyle that became popular in the 1970s and has since seen several revivals.

**Characterized by its choppy ends, layers of varying lengths, and a lot of texture, the shag haircut is designed to add volume and movement to the hair.**

![](/images/70s-shag-haircut-short/70s-Shag-Haircut-683x1024.webp)

It’s a versatile style that can be tailored to different hair lengths and types, from short and edgy to long and flowing.

**The key elements of a [shag haircut include feathered layers,](https://www.scantotalk.com/shag-haircuts/) a fringe (often curtain bangs), and a tousled, effortlessly cool look.**

This style is perfect for those seeking a low-maintenance yet fashionable hairstyle that can easily transition from casual to chic.
