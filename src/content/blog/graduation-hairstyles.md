Graduation day is just around the corner, and I bet you’re already planning your outfit, shoes, and makeup.

**But have you thought about your hairstyle yet? Finding the perfect graduation hairstyle can be a bit overwhelming with so many options out there.**

![](/images/graduation-hairstyles/graduation-hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

_Are you looking for something classic, elegant, trendy, and fun? Do you want to keep your hair up and out of your face, or let it flow freely?_

**Whatever your hair type or personal style, I’ve got you covered! Here are 40 graduation hairstyles to help you look and feel your best on this special day.**

_Let’s dive into these fabulous ideas!_

## 40 Easy Graduation Hairstyles to Copy in 2025 ## 1. Classic Updo

The classic updo is a timeless choice for graduation. You can achieve this sleek and elegant look by adding a bit of volume at the crown.

Use a fine-tooth comb to smooth the sides and secure your hair with bobby pins. This style keeps your hair out of your face and gives you a polished appearance.

![](/images/graduation-hairstyles/dfgfdgfdgfd-1-771x1024.png)

## 2. Loose Waves

Loose waves are perfect for adding a touch of effortless glamour to your graduation day. You can create these soft waves using a curling iron or rollers.

Just wrap sections of your hair around the barrel, hold for a few seconds, and release. Finish with a light hairspray to keep the waves intact throughout the ceremony.

![](/images/graduation-hairstyles/retretretre-771x1024.png)

## 3. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a beautiful way to frame your face and showcase your hair’s length. You can start by parting your hair in the middle and braiding each side.

Then, wrap the braids around your head and pin them in place. This style is perfect for adding a bohemian touch to your graduation look.

![](/images/graduation-hairstyles/ntygrtrter-771x1024.png)

## 4. Half-Up Half-Down

If you can’t decide between wearing your hair up or down, the half-up half-down style is a great compromise.

**Simply pull the top half of your hair into a bun or ponytail, leaving the rest to flow freely.**

This versatile and practical look keeps your hair out of your face while still showing off its length.

![](/images/graduation-hairstyles/ghtrytrreterter-771x1024.png)

## 5. Sleek Ponytail

A sleek ponytail is a chic and modern option for graduation. You can achieve this look by straightening your hair and gathering it into a high ponytail.

Wrap a small section of hair around the base to hide the hair tie and secure it with a bobby pin. This style exudes confidence and sophistication.

## 6. Curly Bob

For those with naturally curly hair, a curly bob is a stylish and manageable option. You can define your curls with a curl-enhancing product and a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

## 7. Low Bun

A low bun is a classic and sophisticated choice for graduation. You can create this look by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail and twisting it into a bun at the nape of your neck.
