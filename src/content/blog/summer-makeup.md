## Tips for Summer Makeup

When the heat cranks up, your makeup game needs to stay on point without melting away.

**First off, start with a lightweight, oil-free moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated without feeling greasy.**

Swap your heavy foundation for a tinted moisturizer or BB cream to even out your skin tone while letting it breathe.

![](/images/summer-makeup/Summer-Makeup-Looks-2025-683x1024.webp)

**Don’t forget the power of a good primer; it acts as a barrier to keep your makeup intact despite the humidity.**

For eye makeup, opt for waterproof formulas to avoid smudging, and keep your colors light and bright for a fresh, summery look.

**A setting spray can be your best friend, locking everything in place and giving you a dewy finish.**

Always keep blotting papers handy to dab away any excess shine throughout the day.
