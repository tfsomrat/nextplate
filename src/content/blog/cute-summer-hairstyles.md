Summer is the perfect time to switch things up and let your hair reflect the fun, carefree vibes of the season. Whether you’re headed to the beach, a backyard barbecue, or just trying to stay cool on a sunny day, having a go-to set of cute and easy hairstyles makes all the difference. This list isn’t just about trends—it’s about helping you look and feel amazing with minimal effort. And if you’re shopping for yourself or looking for the perfect beauty-inspired gift for a friend, these hairstyles come with practical tools and accessories available on Amazon to elevate the look. From braids to buns to heatless waves, these ideas are all about low-maintenance style that turns heads. So let’s dive into the ultimate list of summer hair inspiration you’ll actually want to try.

## 1. The Effortless Beach Waves

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/mrs.powersbeauty_1653851981_2849039371439296986_145833469-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[mrs.powersbeauty](https://www.instagram.com/mrs.powersbeauty/?e=0880bcfc-078f-45f7-88da-cc18f81c9798&g=5)

Beach waves are the epitome of summer style. They give off that laid-back, vacation-ready energy without needing a full trip to the coast. The best part? You can create them without heat, using braid techniques or sea salt sprays that enhance your natural texture. Products like the OGX Moroccan Sea Salt Spray can help give your hair that tousled, fresh-from-the-ocean look in minutes. It’s a great gift idea for someone who loves the beach but wants to skip the sun damage. Whether you’re going for a full-day look or a soft evening vibe, beach waves never disappoint.

## 2. High Ponytail with a Twist

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/rjstylesco_1713716218_3351216988961065793_31367969362-1-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[rjstylesco](https://www.instagram.com/rjstylesco/?e=46231e60-a5ca-43f6-8030-84c323133564&g=5)

This isn’t your average ponytail. A high ponytail with a twist—literally—adds volume and flair with very little effort. You can braid a small section before tying it back or wrap a piece of hair around the base to hide the elastic and give it that polished finish. Tools like a volumizing comb or a strong-hold ponytail hook can make this look easier to pull off. It’s perfect for both workout days and date nights, making it a versatile style to keep in your summer rotation. If you’re gifting a beauty basket, don’t forget to throw in some scrunchies or a ponytail cuff for added style points.

## 3. Boho Braided Crown

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/raquellyss_1656369246_2870155725789682156_282200830-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[raquellyss](https://www.instagram.com/raquellyss/?e=b30d39e2-bb73-4852-9dc5-8aec20dad1b5&g=5)

Nothing screams summer festival more than a soft, braided crown. This romantic look works well with dresses, maxi skirts, and all things flowy. It’s surprisingly easy to create by braiding two sections from either side of your head and pinning them across the top. Use lightweight bobby pins and a setting spray to keep everything in place all day. It’s also a thoughtful idea for a summer bridal shower or birthday gift—pair with a set of gold-accented pins or flower clips to complete the aesthetic. It’s functional, photogenic, and seriously cute.

## 4. Bubble Braids for a Playful Look

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/roralovestrand_1593352881_2341536144080265079_260820316-854x1024.webp)

**source @**[roralovestrand](https://www.instagram.com/roralovestrand/?e=d08ed9e1-98c5-413f-982b-7230bb7992f6&g=5)

Bubble braids are one of the most fun and youthful hairstyles to try this season. All you need are a few clear elastics spaced evenly down the length of your ponytail or pigtails. The result? A whimsical look that adds dimension and personality to your summer style. You can elevate it with ribbon or mini clips if you’re feeling creative. This one’s also perfect for gifting to teens or younger girls—just include a multi-pack of colorful hair ties from Amazon. Whether you’re lounging poolside or going to a summer fair, bubble braids are a crowd-pleaser.

## 5. Messy Bun with Statement Scrunchie

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/beautybyroha_1619919792_2564395539342164023_10578209989-824x1024.webp)

**source @**[beautybyroha](https://www.instagram.com/beautybyroha/?e=b6e7bbf4-37ea-46bf-bfc7-16a32040a140&g=5)

Let’s face it—summer is hot, and sometimes you just want all your hair up and out of the way. Enter the messy bun. The beauty of this look lies in its imperfections. Throw it up loosely and secure it with a statement scrunchie—velvet, satin, or even organza for a dreamy touch. Look for oversized scrunchies in bold prints or soft neutrals on Amazon for a small but impactful gift. Whether you’re heading to the farmer’s market or doing yoga in the park, this hairstyle brings the easy-breezy energy summer demands.

## 6. Sleek Low Bun for Chic Vibes

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/alexgaboury_1693764753_3183851972306047478_280216151-1-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[alexgaboury](https://www.instagram.com/alexgaboury/?e=c07a3328-0bbf-4568-a9a0-bca626350e12&g=5)

For a more elegant summer vibe, go for a sleek low bun. It’s ideal for humid days when you want a clean look that won’t frizz up by noon. Start with a smoothing serum and brush your hair back tightly before twisting it into a bun at the nape of your neck. Secure with discreet pins or a minimalist hair clip for that modern touch. Add a shine spray to finish the look, and you’ve got a polished style that’s wedding-guest appropriate. This look also works great for gifting someone who loves to keep their look classy and controlled.

## 7. Scarf-Wrapped Ponytail

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/tokyoblondepasta_1704896755_3277233975461766745_39752789815-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/tokyoblondepasta/?e=7156239d-efdf-4638-b95e-cd3755fe7724&g=5)[tokyoblondepasta](https://www.instagram.com/tokyoblondepasta/?e=7156239d-efdf-4638-b95e-cd3755fe7724&g=5)

Turn a simple ponytail into a fashion statement by wrapping a colorful scarf around it. You can tie it into a bow or let it hang loose for a more dramatic effect. This is a great way to add a pop of color to your outfit without having to overthink it. Lightweight summer scarves are widely available on Amazon and make fabulous add-ons to any beauty gift set. Perfect for brunches, beach days, or even travel days when you still want to look put together without the fuss.

## 8. Half-Up Space Buns

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/jhairieciprian_1638409927_2719502040218616694_18775623-1-1024x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/jhairieciprian/?e=7413fecf-b096-4a23-b3e1-75ff182e8a61&g=5)[jhairieciprian](https://www.instagram.com/jhairieciprian/?e=7413fecf-b096-4a23-b3e1-75ff182e8a61&g=5)

Space buns are quirky, cute, and totally summer-approved. The half-up version lets you enjoy the fun while still keeping some of your length down. Simply split the top half of your hair into two mini buns and secure them with elastics or decorative pins. This hairstyle is youthful and edgy, making it perfect for concerts or casual hangouts. Include fun hair accessories like glitter pins or neon ties in a gift set to make someone’s day. It’s playful, easy to style, and definitely a conversation starter.

## 9. Loose Braided Pigtails

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/cheban_alena_1734300416_3523889758537996435_2092136740-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/cheban_alena/?e=1d626bdc-230a-4897-bb89-7a8a9a57473f&g=5)[cheban\_alena](https://www.instagram.com/cheban_alena/?e=1d626bdc-230a-4897-bb89-7a8a9a57473f&g=5)

Think pigtails are just for kids? Think again. Loose braided pigtails have made a serious comeback, especially with a soft, undone finish. They work beautifully for medium to long hair and pair well with casual outfits. Add a texturizing spray to get that lived-in look, or braid only partway down for a boho effect. It’s a sweet idea for summer camping trips or outdoor events. Gift-wise, include a detangling brush and a fun set of elastics to encourage experimenting with this style.

## 10. Waterfall Braid

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/wb_upstyles_1627393788_2627091962493584714_1953355072-1-967x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/wb_upstyles/?e=6d7a28c2-f935-4bbf-aecc-29e215f39670&g=5)[wb\_upstyles](https://www.instagram.com/wb_upstyles/?e=6d7a28c2-f935-4bbf-aecc-29e215f39670&g=5)

The waterfall braid looks intricate but is actually simple once you get the hang of it. It gives off a fairytale vibe that’s perfect for summer weddings, picnics, or outdoor photoshoots. You can let the rest of your hair flow naturally or enhance it with soft curls using a cordless curler—Amazon has some fantastic portable options. Add in dainty flower clips or pearl pins for extra charm. This is a style worth gifting someone who loves feeling feminine and put together.

## 11. The Claw Clip Twist

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/twistmepretty_1693408879_3180866683806787025_535258939-1-821x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/twistmepretty/?e=7f510757-f015-418e-a923-0c9f7d88d750&g=5)[twistmepretty](https://www.instagram.com/twistmepretty/?e=8c66f828-e7ba-4385-b59f-ddb46079a5fe&g=5)

Claw clips have made a major comeback, and there’s no easier way to create a chic updo. Gather your hair in a twist and clip it up for an instantly stylish look that feels both nostalgic and current. Choose from minimalist matte finishes to sparkly statement pieces depending on your outfit or mood. They make for excellent stocking stuffers or quick add-ons to any summer self-care kit. The claw clip twist works for all hair types and lengths, making it a universally flattering go-to.

## 12. Heatless Overnight Waves

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/lesteticasalon_1724954055_3445486800074239035_45784482221-1-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[lesteticasalon](https://www.instagram.com/lesteticasalon/?e=55113ee6-ece2-403a-aaf8-711fa26cde1c&g=5)

Want soft curls without frying your hair? Heatless overnight waves are the answer. Wrap damp hair around a silk ribbon or flexible rod, sleep on it, and wake up with natural-looking curls that require zero effort in the morning. These tools are available in kits on Amazon and are perfect gifts for anyone looking to up their hair game without the heat damage. Pair it with a satin pillowcase or a leave-in conditioner to complete the look. It’s an easy, healthy way to keep your summer hair looking amazing.

## 13. Slicked-Back Wet Look

![](/images/cute-summer-hairstyles/beautybydevonn_1736352082_3541100383772746756_1706789580-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[beautybydevonn](https://www.instagram.com/beautybydevonn/?e=6586557d-aa30-4d95-bf4a-9572074b6702&g=5)

For a bold, high-fashion edge, try the slicked-back wet look. This is ideal for a night out or a rooftop party where you want to make a strong impression. All it takes is a good gel and a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly. Finish with a strong-hold hairspray, and you’ve got a head-turning style that stays put no matter the heat. It’s a great idea to gift a high-quality gel or serum along with a styling comb—perfect for someone who likes to experiment with different looks.

## Conclusion

When summer hits, the last thing you want is to spend hours in front of a mirror. These 13 cute and easy summer hairstyles are all about saving time while still looking effortlessly stylish. Whether you’re shopping for yourself or searching for thoughtful beauty gifts, the right tools and accessories can make these styles even more fun to try. From casual beach days to dressy nights out, these hairstyles have you covered for every summer occasion. Try one, try them all—and don’t forget to enjoy every sun-soaked moment while looking fabulous doing it.
