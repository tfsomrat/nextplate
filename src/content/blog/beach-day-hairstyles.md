Spending a day at the beach is one of summer’s greatest pleasures, but the combination of salty air, wind, and humidity can make keeping your hair in check a real challenge. Whether you’re sunbathing, taking a dip in the ocean, or playing beach volleyball, you want a hairstyle that not only looks cute but also holds up through all the fun. From loose and breezy styles to secure and stylish updos, this list of beach day hairstyles is designed with every hair type and length in mind. Plus, we’ve included product recommendations from Amazon where needed to help you achieve each look with ease. These effortless yet chic options are perfect for gifting ideas too—because let’s face it, great hair accessories and tools make thoughtful and useful presents!

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/beach-day-hairstyles-683x1024.webp)

## 1. Classic Messy Bun With a Twist

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/angye_abarca_makeup_1743988985_3605163367956096825_2164584596-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/angye_abarca_makeup/?e=7f821232-9d9c-4a4e-9ae5-d48f5175f9bb&g=5)[angye\_abarca\_makeup](https://www.instagram.com/angye_abarca_makeup/?e=7f821232-9d9c-4a4e-9ae5-d48f5175f9bb&g=5)

The messy bun is a timeless beach hairstyle because it’s low-maintenance and keeps your hair out of your face. To elevate this look, twist a few strands before pulling them into a bun for a soft, boho effect. This adds dimension and gives the illusion of a more styled look without extra effort. Secure the bun with spiral hair ties, which won’t snag or damage hair even when wet. You can also add a printed silk scarf to the base for flair and sun protection. Amazon offers multi-pack sets of no-pull spiral ties and lightweight beachy scarves that make excellent gifts for friends who love coastal vibes.

## 2. Dutch Braids for Double the Hold

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/moxx_salon_1717380837_3381958047617799317_7414810992-1024x1024.webp)

**source @**[moxx\_salon](https://www.instagram.com/moxx_salon/?e=13543512-eabf-40f2-ab56-a82ae559f03c&g=5)

Dutch braids offer not only a cute and sporty look but also serious staying power. They start at the crown and sit on top of your hair, making them more visible and durable compared to regular braids. These braids work well for all hair types and are especially ideal for active beach days. You can braid both sides and secure them with small elastics that blend with your hair color. A detangling brush and sectioning clips from Amazon can help streamline the braiding process, and they’re perfect items to include in a DIY beach day hair kit gift.

## 3. Sleek Low Ponytail with a Statement Scrunchie

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/isabellajanehairmakeup_1647418717_2795073243158256439_721468632-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[isabellajanehairmakeup](https://www.instagram.com/isabellajanehairmakeup/?e=8b816252-1ecf-4bf0-add5-f6ca7d3e199c&g=5)

A low ponytail can be both elegant and practical for the beach, especially when paired with a UV-protective hair serum to keep frizz at bay. Using a smoothing brush and tying your hair at the nape of your neck gives it a polished appearance. Add a large, textured scrunchie—think terrycloth or satin—for a fun, eye-catching finish that doesn’t damage strands. Amazon has bundles of oversized scrunchies in beach-friendly fabrics that make perfect summer gifts or stocking stuffers. Don’t forget to tuck a travel-sized smoothing serum in your beach bag for quick touch-ups!

## 4. High Top Knot for Instant Lift

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/sasha.mazzuca_1630343168_2651833159038977145_15877644711-1024x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/sasha.mazzuca/?e=0c402c5f-245c-469d-a221-3b385ae3472f&g=5)[sasha.mazzuca](https://www.instagram.com/sasha.mazzuca/?e=0c402c5f-245c-469d-a221-3b385ae3472f&g=5)

For those with long hair, the high top knot is a heat-free savior on hot beach days. Simply gather your hair at the crown, twist it into a coil, and secure with a thick hair tie or claw clip. This hairstyle keeps your neck cool and your hair safe from the salty sea breeze. To make it more stylish, pull out a few wispy strands around your face or use a texturizing spray for added volume. Amazon’s bestselling claw clips and texture sprays are affordable and highly giftable for any beach lover.

## 5. Beach Waves with a Salt Spray Finish

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/rizoscurls_1708549244_3307873140864353831_1369174913-885x1024.webp)

**source @**[rizoscurls](https://www.instagram.com/rizoscurls/?e=610afe23-b913-40cb-bc3b-75f6ead19971&g=5)

Nothing says “beach day” like loose, effortless waves that move with the ocean breeze. If your hair doesn’t naturally curl with seawater, you can fake the look with a sea salt spray applied to damp strands. Scrunch your hair and let it air dry or use a diffuser for extra bounce. This style works best with layered cuts and can be touched up throughout the day with minimal effort. Look for Amazon’s top-rated sea salt sprays, often bundled with leave-in conditioners—making for a thoughtful and functional beauty gift set.

## 6. Braided Crown for a Boho Goddess Look

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/braiding_by_hannah_1650877195_2824085059763316513_1687040260-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[braiding\_by\_hannah](https://www.instagram.com/braiding_by_hannah/?e=aa22a537-5f68-4318-84e4-7e52da05c15b&g=5)

A braided crown circles the head like a halo and looks absolutely dreamy against a backdrop of sand and sea. This style not only keeps hair out of your face but also works well with damp or second-day hair. You can start with two braids on either side of your head and pin them across the crown using bobby pins. Add tiny seashell or floral clips for a mermaid-inspired twist. Amazon offers beautiful hairpins and mini clip sets that turn this into a gift-worthy hairstyle idea for weddings, bridal showers, or birthdays.

## 7. Half-Up Space Buns for Playful Charm

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/braiding_by_hannah_1597224293_2374011894087632448_1687040260-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[braiding\_by\_hannah](https://www.instagram.com/braiding_by_hannah/?e=97a725cd-841b-4001-af50-ef79a374eb07&g=5)

For a youthful, fun look, half-up space buns are a favorite. Just section off the top half of your hair, twist it into two mini buns, and secure with mini elastic bands or decorative pins. This keeps the bulk of your hair down and flowing, while adding interest and structure to the top. It’s a great compromise if you want both function and flair. Amazon has themed accessories like star clips, glitter elastics, and mini combs that make this hairstyle a blast to gift and personalize.

## 8. Twisted Rope Ponytail for Elegant Simplicity

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/alexgaboury_1614635943_2520071407338805596_280216151-820x1024.webp)

**source @**[alexgaboury](https://www.instagram.com/alexgaboury/?e=61056b6a-9c9c-4734-b052-044a76360ca7&g=5)

The rope ponytail is both practical and polished—a style that looks more intricate than it really is. Divide your ponytail into two sections, twist each, then wrap them around each other and secure at the end. It’s an excellent choice for medium to long hair and resists frizz and tangles well. Pair it with a moisture-resistant styling cream to smooth the look. Amazon bundles often include creams and styling combs that pair well together, making this an easy bundle gift idea for beach goers.

## 9. Bandana-Wrapped Ponytail for Retro Cool

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/kendrascott_1717081335_3379445635373735641_20184144-820x1024.webp)

**source @**[kendrascott](https://www.instagram.com/kendrascott/?e=50465e7b-6e03-4615-8bf6-bd6eb8026c06&g=5)

If you’re looking to add a vintage vibe to your beach day, the bandana-wrapped ponytail is the way to go. Pull your hair into a low or mid-level ponytail, then wrap a colorful bandana around the base and tie it into a bow. This style gives off 1950s summer glam and adds a pop of color. Choose a lightweight cotton bandana that won’t trap heat. Amazon features dozens of retro-inspired packs that can be gifted alongside SPF hair mists or beach-themed sunglasses for the full look.

## 10. Wet Look Slick Back for Edgy Vibes

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/taya.hair_1697638073_3216343733440509944_2242001342-820x1024.webp)

**source @**[taya.hair](https://www.instagram.com/taya.hair/?e=1560d957-0105-40e8-bde4-c8b56c10819f&g=5)

If you want a more modern and edgy style, try the slicked-back wet look. Apply a generous amount of gel or mousse to damp hair, then use a comb to pull it straight back from your face. The result is clean, fashion-forward, and surprisingly beach-appropriate. This look requires strong-hold products that also resist humidity. Amazon’s humidity-proof gels and wide-tooth combs come in duo packs that are great for gifting to trend-forward friends.

## 11. Fishtail Braid for Long-Haired Beauties

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/tokyoblondepasta_1718016810_3387292976378812296_39752789815-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[tokyoblondepasta](https://www.instagram.com/tokyoblondepasta/?e=4beb4997-939d-4dd2-aef3-9a2174a4227a&g=5)

The fishtail braid is visually stunning and holds up exceptionally well against beach conditions. Though it looks complex, it’s easy to master with practice and perfect for long hair. It starts by dividing your hair into two sections and alternately weaving small strands across them. Secure it with a clear elastic and gently pull at the sides for volume. Fishtail braid kits, available on Amazon, often include elastics, how-to cards, and accessories, making them ideal beach gift ideas.

## 12. Bubble Ponytail for a Trendy Update

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/book.celina_1727986390_3470923874697810616_1658035606-847x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/book.celina/?api=postMessage&e=f827d53b-a07e-4780-9be9-4c45d6f1928d&g=5)[book.celina](https://www.instagram.com/book.celina/?api=postMessage&e=f827d53b-a07e-4780-9be9-4c45d6f1928d&g=5)

This modern ponytail variation uses multiple hair ties to create “bubbles” down the length of the ponytail. Start with a traditional ponytail and place elastic bands every few inches. Gently tug at the sections in between to puff them out. It’s cute, holds shape well, and is great for those who want something bold yet easy. You can grab colorful or neutral-toned hair ties on Amazon, many of which are packaged beautifully for gifting.

## 13. Scarf Braid for a Chic Touch

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/tokyoblondepasta_1744613488_3610402075395342681_39752789815-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[tokyoblondepasta](https://www.instagram.com/tokyoblondepasta/?e=6237fa71-e4b1-4d5d-b9fd-60a16b927dd5&g=5)

Incorporating a scarf into your braid is both stylish and practical—it helps hold the braid and adds visual interest. Simply fold a scarf lengthwise and braid it into a three-strand braid starting at the nape or side. Secure with a small elastic or tie the scarf ends for an added flourish. Choose lightweight scarves that are machine washable and sun-safe. Amazon offers elegant scarf multipacks in various patterns, perfect for matching with swimwear or gifting as part of a summer hair accessory set.

## 14. Loose Top Braid with Beachy Texture

![](/images/beach-day-hairstyles/meganlkarp_1612879852_2505340247288574365_23113419523-820x1024.webp)

**source @**[meganlkarp](https://www.instagram.com/meganlkarp/?e=93bced37-e613-432d-8f7b-ad56b7b60c7a&g=5)

A loose braid starting at the crown and trailing into a ponytail or left flowing is casual yet secure. This style adds body and works especially well with natural or sea-sprayed texture. It gives the illusion of volume and keeps hair in place without being overly tight. Use a volumizing powder or spray to plump roots before braiding. Amazon has great travel-sized volumizers that pair well with soft hair ties—perfect items to gift in a summer hair care bundle.

## 15. Claw Clip Twist for Instant Chic

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/littlehairessentials/?e=157e2605-c482-4ca1-b4e4-4a0d3b4c51bd&g=5)[littlehairessentials](https://www.instagram.com/littlehairessentials/?e=157e2605-c482-4ca1-b4e4-4a0d3b4c51bd&g=5)

Claw clips are back in a big way, and they’re incredibly convenient for beach days. Simply twist your hair and clamp it at the back using a large, secure claw clip. This keeps hair up and cool while still looking effortlessly stylish. Claw clips now come in marble, pastel, and matte finishes that match swimsuits and cover-ups beautifully. Amazon’s bestsellers come in variety packs, making them a fantastic gift for anyone building their summer beauty stash.
