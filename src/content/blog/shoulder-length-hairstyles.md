Finding the perfect hairstyle that strikes a balance between style and ease can feel like a challenge, especially for those with shoulder-length hair.

Whether you’re juggling a busy schedule or just want a versatile look that requires minimal effort, **shoulder-length hairstyles** offer endless possibilities.

![](/images/shoulder-length-hairstyles/Easy-Shoulder-Length-Hairstyles-1-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve curated 24 easy shoulder-length hairstyles that will elevate your everyday style without requiring hours in front of the mirror.**

From soft waves to sleek lobs and textured cuts, there’s a style for every mood and occasion.

## Easy Shoulder Length Hairstyles

![](/images/shoulder-length-hairstyles/Easy-Shoulder-Length-Hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Wavy Layered Bob

This **wavy layered bob** is perfect for shoulder-length hair, offering a soft, casual look. To achieve this hairstyle, start by adding loose waves using a curling iron or wand, then gently tousle them with your fingers for a more natural effect.

Layers give the hair movement and volume, making it ideal for fine or medium hair types. The subtle highlights enhance the waves and provide dimension.

For maintenance, use a **texturizing spray** to keep the waves intact throughout the day. This style is great for a laid-back, yet polished appearance, perfect for everyday wear or special occasions.

### 2. Shaggy Cut with Bangs

The **shaggy cut with bangs** adds a retro vibe to shoulder-length hair. This hairstyle features choppy layers throughout the length, with blunt bangs that frame the face.

It works best with natural waves or texture, but you can use a curling iron to add soft bends if needed. The shaggy layers create a relaxed, carefree look, making it low maintenance yet stylish.

To style, use a **light mousse or texturizing cream** to define the layers and keep the look tousled and effortless. It’s ideal for those who want an edgy, modern take on shoulder-length hair.

![](/images/shoulder-length-hairstyles/image-306.jpeg)

@[saxce](https://www.instagram.com/saxce/)

### 3. Shoulder-Length Box Braids

Box braids at shoulder length are a versatile and low-maintenance option for textured hair. These braids are neat, uniform, and fall just at the shoulders, creating a structured yet manageable look.

You can achieve this style by sectioning the hair and braiding it close to the scalp in small, even sections. Adding **curled ends** at the bottom gives the style extra flair.

This protective hairstyle is perfect for keeping your natural hair healthy while enjoying a stylish, carefree look. It’s easy to style and works for both casual and professional settings.

![](/images/shoulder-length-hairstyles/image-307.jpeg)

### 4. Vintage Waves

The **vintage waves** hairstyle offers a timeless, elegant look that’s perfect for shoulder-length hair. These structured, glossy waves are achieved using a curling iron or hot rollers.

After curling, brush through the curls to soften them into smooth, defined waves. Finish with a **shine-enhancing spray** for that polished, Old Hollywood glam.

This hairstyle is ideal for formal events or whenever you want a sophisticated appearance. It pairs well with vintage or classic outfits and can elevate your entire look with minimal effort.

### 5. Curtain Bangs with Soft Layers

This **curtain bangs with soft layers** hairstyle is perfect for a relaxed yet chic look. The curtain bangs gently frame the face, giving a soft and flattering appearance to any face shape.

The layers start from the mid-length of the hair, adding texture and movement. To achieve this style, blow dry the bangs with a round brush to get that signature split, and then loosely curl the ends of the layers for added bounce.

Finish with a **light-hold hairspray** to maintain the shape. This effortless yet stylish look works well for casual or formal occasions, adding a bit of flair to your shoulder-length hair.

### 6. Half-Up Curly Pigtails

The **half-up curly pigtails** bring a fun and playful vibe to shoulder-length hair. This hairstyle features high pigtails with soft curls cascading down, creating a youthful and voluminous look.

To achieve this style, section the hair into two parts, tying the top half into high pigtails, and then use a curling wand to add defined curls to the loose hair.

This look is perfect for events or casual outings, offering a blend of elegance and charm. To finish, use a **curl-defining mousse** to keep the curls intact while ensuring the hair stays bouncy throughout the day.

### 7. Wavy Lob with Blonde Highlights

### 9. Half-Up Braided Waves

The **half-up braided waves** hairstyle combines elegance and texture, making it a versatile look for any occasion. The top section of the hair is braided and secured at the back, while the rest of the hair is styled in soft waves.

Use a **curling iron** to create soft waves, then lightly tousle them for a natural, flowy appearance. This hairstyle is perfect for casual days or special events, adding a bohemian vibe to your look.
