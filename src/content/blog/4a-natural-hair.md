## What is 4A Natural Hair?

4A natural hair is a specific type of curly hair texture that is characterized by tight, well-defined curls.

**It falls under the Type 4 hair category, which includes the coolest and kinkiest hair types.**

### Curl Pattern

*   **4A hair** has an “S” shaped curl pattern.
*   The curls are typically about the size of a crochet needle or a coffee stirrer.
*   The curls are tightly coiled and well-defined, giving the hair a springy, bouncy appearance.

### Texture

*   4A hair is usually soft to the touch but can be prone to dryness.
*   It has a fine texture, but the curls can make it appear thicker than it is.

### Density and Volume

*   4A hair can be dense, meaning there are a lot of strands of hair on the scalp.
*   This density gives it a lot of volume and fullness.

### Shrinkage

*   One of the notable features of 4A hair is its shrinkage.
*   The hair can shrink up to 50-70% of its actual length when it dries, making it appear much shorter than it is when stretched or wet.

### Moisture Needs

*   4A hair tends to be dry because the natural oils from the scalp have a harder time traveling down the curls.
*   It requires regular moisturizing and deep conditioning to maintain its health and prevent breakage.

### Versatility

*   4A hair is highly versatile and can be styled in numerous ways, including twist-outs, braid-outs, wash-and-go styles, updos, and protective styles like braids and twists.
*   It responds well to products that enhance curl definition and provide moisture.

### Care and Maintenance

*   Gentle handling is essential to prevent breakage.
*   Regular detangling with a wide-tooth comb or fingers is recommended to minimize tangles and knots.
*   Protective styling can help retain length and protect the hair from environmental damage.

4A natural hair is beautiful and unique, offering a range of styling possibilities that showcase its natural texture and curl pattern.
