## What is Knotless Braids?

Knotless braids are a variation of traditional box braids that don’t use a knot to secure the braiding hair at the scalp.

**Instead, the braiding starts with your natural hair, and the synthetic hair is gradually added in small pieces, creating a seamless and natural look.**

![](/images/thigh-length-knotless-braids/Thigh-Length-Knotless-Braids-576x1024.jpg)

This technique reduces tension on the scalp, making it a more comfortable and healthier option for your hair.

**Knotless braids are known for their lightweight feel, natural appearance, and the versatility they offer in terms of styling.**

They can be worn in various lengths, colors, and styles, making them a popular choice for those looking to protect their natural hair while still achieving a beautiful and manageable hairstyle.
