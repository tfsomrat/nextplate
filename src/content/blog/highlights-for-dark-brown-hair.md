## What are Highlights for Hairs?

Highlights are a hair coloring technique that involves lightening specific strands of your hair to create contrast and dimension.

**This process not only adds visual interest but also enhances the natural texture and movement of your locks.**

Whether you opt for subtle, sun-kissed streaks or bold, dramatic highlights, this technique can brighten your overall look and bring a fresh, vibrant appeal to your hair.

**Highlights are a versatile option for anyone looking to update their style without committing to a full-color change, making them a popular choice for both natural and dyed hair.**
