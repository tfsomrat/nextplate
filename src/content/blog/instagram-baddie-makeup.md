## 13 Instagram Baddie Makeup Ideas

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/instagram-baddie-makeup-looks-to-copy-in-2025-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Glamorous Evening Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/bratz.lynx**

To achieve this bold evening makeup, start with a primer and a full-coverage, matte foundation for a flawless base.

**For the eyes, apply charcoal or black eyeshadow, and define with a thick, winged eyeliner extending beyond the outer corners.**

Enhance the look with shimmer on the inner corners and voluminous mascara or false lashes.

Choose a deep burgundy or black matte lipstick for the lips, ensuring they’re exfoliated first for smooth application.

Define your cheekbones with contour and highlight the high points of your face for added dimension.

Set your look with a setting spray to last all night. This style is perfect for making a statement at any upscale event.

### 2. Bold and Shimmering Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-1-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/makeupnely**

To achieve this bold and shimmering makeup look, start by applying a light, shimmering eyeshadow, like silver or pale gold, across the eyelids.

Use a black liquid eyeliner to create a dramatic wing that extends beyond the outer corners of your eyes.

For the eyebrows, opt for a strong, defined shape using a brow pomade or pencil. The skin should glow with a flawless finish.

Apply a peach-toned blush to the cheeks and a subtle highlighter to the cheekbones to enhance the radiance.

Lips are a standout with metallic bronze or copper lipstick, adding a modern touch.

This look pairs perfectly with evening wear or special occasions, ensuring you stand out with sophistication.

### 3. Soft Romantic Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-2-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/makeupbyteani**

To master this soft romantic makeup look, start with a hydrating primer for a smooth base. Use a lightweight foundation or tinted moisturizer to achieve a natural, radiant glow.

For the eyes, apply a smoky blue eyeshadow, blending it softly at the edges, and add a touch of shimmer in the inner corners to brighten the eyes.

Curl your lashes and apply a lengthening mascara to keep things subtle yet defined.

Contour lightly with bronzer to warm up the face and apply a soft pink blush for a fresh flush.

The lips are a standout, choose a rosy pink glossy lipstick to make them appear fuller and vibrant.

### 4. Polished Natural Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-3-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/makeupbyandie**

For a polished natural makeup look, start with a hydrating primer followed by a full-coverage foundation for a flawless, matte finish.

**Contour your cheekbones and jawline subtly to enhance your facial structure, and apply a warm bronzer for a sun-kissed effect.**

For the eyes, use neutral brown eyeshadows to gently define and contour your lids. Add volume to your lashes with a lengthening mascara or opt for subtle false lashes for extra impact.

Neatly fill in your eyebrows to frame your face. Finish the look with nude lipstick topped with gloss for fuller, hydrated lips.

### 5. Bold Graphic Eyeshadow Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-4-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/evyxo\_**

To recreate this bold graphic eyeshadow look, start with a flawless matte base using a high-coverage foundation.

Contour sharply along the cheekbones and nose for a sculpted effect, adding a warm peach blush for a touch of color.

**For the eyes, create a dramatic winged shape extending beyond the crease using dark brown or black eyeshadow, highlighting the lids with gold shimmer.**

Define eyebrows heavily to complement the eye makeup. Lips should be lined with a dark nude pencil, and filled with a lighter matte shade to enhance fullness.

Add playful faux freckles across the nose and cheeks for a whimsical touch.

### 6. Vibrant Blue Eyeshadow Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-5-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/facesbyfranchesca**

To achieve this vibrant blue eyeshadow look, begin with a hydrating primer and apply a luminous foundation for a dewy glow.

Contour subtly along the cheeks and jawline, and highlight the cheekbones to enhance your natural structure.

**For the eyes, sweep a bright blue eyeshadow across the lids and extend it outward in a winged shape.**

Line the lower lash line with the same blue to intensify the color. Apply voluminous mascara or attach false lashes to elevate the dramatic effect.

Keep the lips understated with a glossy nude lipstick or lip gloss, allowing the eyes to stand out.

### 7. Chic Bronzed Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-6-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/blondiemoustache**

Achieve a chic bronzed makeup look by starting with a primer for a smooth base, followed by a full-coverage matte foundation to even out the complexion.

Define your eyebrows to frame the face and use a blend of champagne and light brown eyeshadows for a subtle shimmer on the lids.

**Add winged eyeliner and voluminous false lashes to enhance the eyes. Sculpt your face with contouring on the cheeks, forehead, and jawline, and apply a warm bronzer for a sun-kissed glow.**

Use a peach-toned blush for a healthy flush. For the lips, opt for a glossy brown lipstick to complement the bronzed theme, adding volume and a touch of glamour.

### 8. Bold and Defined Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-7-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/rosiesdreamhouse**

To achieve this bold and defined makeup look, start with a primer and apply a full-coverage matte foundation for a flawless, smooth base.

Define your eyebrows with a brow pomade or pencil to create sharp, striking contours. Use a neutral palette for a cut-crease eyeshadow that enhances the depth of your eyes.

**Don’t shy away from thick, voluminous false lashes to add drama and intensity. Sculpt your face with contouring along the cheeks and jawline, and add a subtle highlight to your cheekbones.**

Opt for a nude matte lipstick to keep the focus on your dramatic eyes. This makeup is perfect for making a strong statement at any event, balancing bold eyes with a subdued lip.

### 9. Soft Glam Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-8-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/faceebyjayy**

For a soft glam look that’s both elegant and understated, start with a hydrating primer followed by a dewy foundation to achieve a luminous complexion.

Use warm brown shades to create a soft, smoky effect on your eyelids and subtly deepen the crease.

**Apply a fine line of eyeliner close to your lash line, extending slightly to elongate the eyes. Choose volumizing mascara or a light set of false lashes for a touch of drama.**

Contour gently along the cheekbones and temples, and add a warm peach blush for a natural flush. Finish with a glossy nude lipstick to make your lips look plump and hydrated.

### 10. Radiant Natural Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-9-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/blendiliciouss**

For a radiant natural makeup look that emphasizes a glowing complexion, start with a hydrating primer and apply a luminous foundation or tinted moisturizer.

Sweep a shimmering pink eyeshadow across your lids to brighten the eyes, and use a lengthening mascara to enhance your lashes subtly.

**Define your eyebrows neatly to frame your face. Apply a generous amount of pink blush on your cheeks, blending upwards towards the temples for a youthful flush.**

Highlight the high points of your face, cheekbones, bridge of the nose, and Cupid’s bow to catch the light. Finish with a glossy nude lipstick to keep the lips looking full and hydrated.

### 13. Timeless Soft Glam Makeup Look

![](/images/instagram-baddie-makeup/15-instagram-baddie-makeup-12-683x1024.jpg)

**Instagram/gabie.godfrey**

For a timeless soft glam look that’s perfect for any occasion, begin with a hydrating primer and a lightweight, dewy foundation to achieve a radiant complexion.

Fill in your eyebrows gently to frame your eyes naturally. Add a touch of blush in peach or pink to your cheeks for a youthful glow and lightly highlight the cheekbones to accentuate your features.

Complete the look with a glossy nude lipstick for an elegant finish.
