## What is Pixie Cut?

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that’s both stylish and versatile, characterized by its cropped length and often layered texture.

**Typically cut above the ears and nape of the neck, it brings out facial features and can be tailored to suit various hair types and personal styles.**

Whether sleek and sophisticated or tousled and edgy, the pixie cut is a go-to choice for those looking to make a chic, low-maintenance statement.
