## What are Space Buns?

Space buns are a playful and trendy hairstyle where the hair is divided into two sections and styled into buns, typically positioned high on the top or sides of the head, though they can also be placed lower at the nape of the neck.

![](/images/space-buns-short-hair/Space-Buns-Short-Hair-576x1024.jpg)

**This versatile look, popularized in the 1990s, can be sleek and polished or [messy](https://www.scantotalk.com/messy-bun/) and casual, often combined with braids, twists, or adorned with accessories like hairpins, ribbons, and glitter.**

Suitable for various hair lengths, especially short to medium, space buns are perfect for casual outings, festivals, parties, or even formal events, offering a fun blend of retro charm and modern flair influenced by cultural and pop culture trends.

## 18 Space Buns for Short Hair Ideas ### 1. Classic Space Buns

If you’re looking for a timeless and chic hairstyle, you can’t go wrong with classic space buns.

**To achieve this look, gather your short hair into two high, evenly-sized buns on either side of your head. Secure them with clear elastics for a sleek and polished finish.**

This style is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair off your face while still looking trendy.

**It’s versatile enough for both casual outings and more dressed-up events. Give it a try and see how effortlessly stylish you can look with classic space buns.**

![](/images/space-buns-short-hair/18-Space-Buns-Short-Hair-1-683x1024.jpg)

### 2. Messy Space Buns

For a relaxed, carefree vibe, messy space buns are the way to go. Start by loosely gathering your short hair into two small buns on the top of your head.

**Don’t worry about making them perfectly even; the charm of this style lies in its imperfect, casual look. Leave a few strands out to frame your face and enhance the messy aesthetic.**

This hairstyle is ideal for weekend hangouts or casual get-togethers, giving you that effortlessly cool appearance without much effort.

### 3. Braided Space Buns

Want to add some texture and interest to your space buns? Try incorporating braids! Divide your short hair into two sections and braid each one before twisting them into buns.

The braids add a unique touch to the classic space buns, making this style perfect for when you want to stand out.

**Whether you’re heading to a festival or just want to switch up your everyday look, braided space buns are a fun and stylish choice.**
