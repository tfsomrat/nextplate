## What is Shag Cut?

A **[shag cut](https://www.scantotalk.com/70s-shag-haircut-short/)** is a timeless and versatile hairstyle known for its effortless, edgy vibe.

**Characterized by its choppy layers, textured ends, and often paired with bangs, the shag cut creates volume and movement, making it perfect for those seeking a low-maintenance yet stylish look.**

It can be adapted to suit any hair length and type, offering a modern twist to a retro-inspired style.

**This haircut not only adds dimension and depth to your hair but also frames the face beautifully, enhancing your natural features.**
