Summer is here, and with it comes the need for fresh, new hairstyles that can keep you cool and stylish.

**Are you tired of the same old ponytail or bun? Looking for some inspiration to change up your look this season?**

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/summer-hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

_Whether you’re planning to spend your days at the beach, attending summer weddings, or just want a change for your everyday style, I’ve got you covered._

**Here are 52 fabulous summer hairstyle ideas that will help you stay chic and comfortable all season long.**

_Let’s dive in and find the perfect hairstyle for you!_

## 52 Summer Hairstyles To Copy in 2025 ### 1. Beachy Waves

You can’t go wrong with beachy waves for a relaxed, summer vibe. Simply spritz some sea salt spray on damp hair, scrunch it up, and let it air dry.

This look is effortless and gives off that just-off-the-beach charm. Perfect for any casual outing, beachy waves are both easy to achieve and maintain.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/iuyiuguh-771x1024.png)

### 2. High Ponytail

When the heat is on, a high ponytail is your best friend. Gather your hair at the crown, secure it with a hair tie, and you’re set.

This sleek style keeps your hair off your neck, making it a go-to for those hot summer days. Plus, it looks polished and chic with minimal effort.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/uhgjhhfgddfg-771x1024.png)

### 3. Braided Crown

Feeling bohemian? A braided crown is the way to go. Part your hair down the middle and create two braids, then wrap them around your head and pin them in place.

This style is perfect for festivals or any summer event where you want to stand out with an elegant, yet relaxed look.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/s.gfdgmfdlmgfd-771x1024.png)

### 4. Messy Bun

For those days when you want a quick and easy hairstyle, the messy bun is a lifesaver.

Gather your hair into a high or low bun, secure it with a hair tie, and pull out a few strands for that effortless look. It’s casual, chic, and perfect for keeping cool in the summer heat.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/rietoretoifsd-771x1024.png)

### 5. Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid looks intricate but is surprisingly easy to do. Split your hair into two sections and take small pieces from the outer edges, crossing them over to the opposite side.

Repeat until you reach the end and secure with a hair tie. This stylish braid adds a touch of elegance to any summer outfit.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/vedwefdedsad-771x1024.png)

### 6. Low Chignon

For a sophisticated summer style, try a low chignon. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck, twist it into a bun, and secure it with pins.

This elegant updo is perfect for more formal occasions or when you want a polished look that’s still cool and comfortable.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/poweriweofisdjfd-771x1024.png)

### 7. Half-Up Top Knot

The half-up top knot is a fun and trendy hairstyle. Pull the top half of your hair into a high bun and leave the rest down.

This look is great for keeping your hair out of your face while still showcasing your length. It’s playful, stylish, and perfect for any summer day.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/dsresrewrewre-771x1024.png)

### 8. Space Buns

For a playful, festival-ready look, try space buns. Divide your hair into two sections and create a bun on each side of your head.

Secure with hair ties and pins, and you’re ready to go. Space buns are fun, quirky, and a great way to add some flair to your summer style.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/sdklarehrkjerjhda-771x1024.png)

### 9. Sleek Bob

A sleek bob is a classic, low-maintenance summer hairstyle. Whether chin-length or a bit longer, this cut is easy to style and keeps you cool.

Simply straighten your hair for a polished look that works for both casual and formal settings. It’s timeless, chic, and always in style.

![](/images/summer-hairstyles/foweirjwehfsdfds-771x1024.png)

### 10. Side Braid

A side braid is a simple yet elegant way to style your hair. Sweep your hair to one side and braid it down.

This look is perfect for keeping your hair neat while adding a touch of romance. Whether you’re heading to the beach or a summer party, a side braid is always a good choice.

### 11. Pixie Cut

Thinking of going short? A pixie cut is bold and perfect for summer. This cropped style is easy to manage and keeps you cool.

### 12. French Twist

The French twist is a timeless, elegant updo. Gather your hair at the back, twist it upwards, and secure it with pins.
