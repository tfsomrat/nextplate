As Christmas approaches, it’s not just about decorating your home and finding the perfect gifts your hairstyle can also be part of the festive fun!

Whether you’re attending holiday gatherings, cozying up with family, or joining friends for a Christmas party, a touch of holiday-inspired style can make you feel extra special.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/christmas-hairstyles-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 22 enchanting Christmas hairstyles to inspire your holiday style.**

Whether you love bold bows, sparkling accessories, or intricate braids, there’s something here for everyone.

## Christmas Hairstyles

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/Christmas-Hairstyles-ideas-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Velvet Bow Half-Up Hairstyle

For a **classic Christmas look**, the velvet bow half-up hairstyle adds a festive touch. To achieve this, start by gathering the top section of your hair, leaving the rest down. Secure the gathered hair with a hair tie, then add a **soft, red velvet bow** at the tie for a vintage holiday look.

This style works best with loose, soft waves, which can be created with a curling wand or flat iron. The **red velvet bow** not only enhances the Christmas feel but also brings attention to the hairstyle. This look is ideal for **Christmas parties or cozy family gatherings** by the tree.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/image-756.jpeg)

@[amberrosepeakehair](https://www.instagram.com/amberrosepeakehair/)

### 2. Festive Braided Pigtails with Beads

This fun and detailed hairstyle involves **intricate braiding and festive decorations** for a bold holiday statement. Begin by parting the hair down the middle and creating two pigtails. French braid each section tightly, adding red, green, and white beads along the braid to symbolize Christmas colors.

Tie off the ends with small red bows, and for an extra festive touch, add a **sparkly Christmas-themed hair accessory** like a Santa hat clip. This style is perfect for kids who want a playful and spirited look for Christmas.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/image-757.jpeg)

@[mia.peinadoss](https://www.instagram.com/mia.peinadoss/)

### 3. Christmas Twist with Green and Red Accents

This twist hairstyle is perfect for **textured or natural hair** and incorporates Christmas colors in a subtle, stylish way. Begin by twisting sections of the hair and pulling them back into a low bun, securing with hairpins. Add **green and red extensions** in random twists for a festive yet understated look.

The color accents give a nod to the holiday season without overpowering the style, making it suitable for various Christmas events. This style holds well and requires minimal touch-ups, ideal for a long day of celebrations.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/image-758.jpeg)

@[naturally.akira](https://www.instagram.com/naturally.akira/)

### 4. Cozy Top Knot with Loose Curls

This **soft and romantic hairstyle** combines a casual top knot with loose curls for a festive yet relaxed look. To create it, start by gathering the hair into a high ponytail, leaving out some front strands for face-framing curls.

Twist the ponytail into a **messy bun** and secure it with a scrunchie or festive hair tie. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the face-framing pieces for a relaxed, effortless vibe. This hairstyle pairs perfectly with cozy sweaters and is ideal for a **Christmas morning look** or casual gatherings.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/image-759.jpeg)

@[kayleymelissa](https://www.instagram.com/kayleymelissa/)

### 5. Christmas Star Braided Crown

This hairstyle features a **braided crown** adorned with colorful beads and a **star accessory** at the top, adding a festive touch. To create this look, start by sectioning the front part of the hair and make a thick braid running along the head’s top edge, adding beads in red, green, and gold as you go.

The braid ends in a smaller braid that hangs loosely by the side, giving a unique holiday-inspired flair. The gold star clip completes the look, making it perfect for **holiday parties and Christmas events**.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/image-760.jpeg)

### 6. Sequin and Bow Christmas Style

This style combines **playful sparkle and holiday colors** with a braided crown featuring sequins and a red sequin bow. Start with a deep side part and create a braid along the top, securing it with red, green, and white sequins for added sparkle.

The rest of the hair can be left in soft waves or curls, giving a **festive and youthful appearance**. Finish with a big red bow to make it extra festive. Ideal for kids and perfect for **Christmas parties or family gatherings**.

### 7. Rudolph Bun Updo

Transform a simple bun into a **Rudolph-inspired updo** by adding reindeer antler accessories, googly eyes, and a red nose detail. Begin by creating a high, sleek bun, securing it firmly with pins.

Then, attach small antler clips or hairpins, and add googly eyes on either side of the bun. Finally, place a small red pom-pom in the center to represent Rudolph’s nose. This creative hairstyle is **fun and festive** for children’s holiday events or Christmas family photos.

![](/images/christmas-hairstyles/image-762.jpeg)

### 8. Glittery Braided Headband

This hairstyle incorporates **glitter and beads** into a braided headband for a magical holiday look. Begin by parting the hair to one side and creating a thin braid along the hairline, securing it with colorful beads in Christmas hues.

Add gold glitter to the scalp area to give it a sparkling effect. The braid acts as a headband, while the rest of the hair is left straight and sleek. This look is perfect for **holiday parties** or festive outings, adding a touch of sparkle to any outfit.

### 9. Starburst Braids with Low Pigtails

This unique hairstyle features a **starburst pattern** with braids leading into low pigtails, creating a festive, structured look. Start by sectioning the hair into small triangular parts from the crown, each leading into a braid. Secure the braids into two low pigtails for a balanced style.

The starburst effect adds a touch of creativity, making it perfect for **holiday events** or school Christmas programs. This hairstyle holds well throughout the day, and adding festive-colored hair ties can enhance the holiday feel.

### 21. Boho Fishtail Braid with Waves

### 22. Double Dutch Braids

This hairstyle features **double Dutch braids** for a sleek and structured look, perfect for a modern holiday twist. Begin by parting the hair down the middle and creating two tight Dutch braids from the front to the back, securing each braid with a hair tie.

The double braid style is versatile, holding up well for active Christmas celebrations or winter outings. You can add small red or green beads to the braids or use festive-colored elastics for a subtle holiday touch. This style is practical, yet can easily be dressed up with a bit of festive flair.
