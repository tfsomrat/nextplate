The **“bun with bangs”** hairstyle is a versatile look that strikes the perfect balance between sophistication and playfulness.

Whether you’re heading to a formal event or enjoying a relaxed day out, buns with bangs allow you to express both elegance and personality in a single style.

![](/images/bun-with-bangs/bun-with-bangs-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’re excited to present 20 inspiring bun with bangs ideas that showcase the beauty and variety of this trend.**

Each style is unique, whether it’s bold with blunt bangs, romantic with side-swept curls, or effortlessly chic with wispy strands.

## Bun with Bangs

![](/images/bun-with-bangs/bun-with-bangs-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Sleek Top Knot with Curtain Bangs

This **sleek top knot** bun, paired with curtain bangs, creates an elegant yet trendy look. To achieve this style, begin by gathering your hair into a high ponytail and securing it tightly.

Wrap the ponytail around itself to form a **neat top knot**, and secure it with bobby pins. Style the bangs by parting them in the center, framing the face for a **soft, face-framing effect**.

This look works well for formal events, as it balances a polished bun with relaxed bangs, making it perfect for both day and night occasions.

![](/images/bun-with-bangs/image-989.jpeg)

@[bbrriie](https://www.instagram.com/bbrriie/)

### 2. High Bun with Side-Swept Bangs

A **high bun with side-swept bangs** adds a touch of sophistication to any look. Start by brushing your hair up into a high ponytail, twisting it into a bun at the top of your head.

Secure with pins or a hair tie. For the bangs, create a deep side part, allowing them to sweep gracefully over the forehead.

This style provides a **soft and romantic vibe** while keeping the hair neatly styled in an updo, ideal for weddings or special evenings.

![](/images/bun-with-bangs/image-990.jpeg)

@[latesthair](https://www.instagram.com/latesthair/)

### 3. Messy Bun with Wispy Bangs

The **messy bun with wispy bangs** is a casual and stylish option for everyday wear. To achieve this look, gather your hair loosely into a mid-height bun, leaving a few strands out for a relaxed vibe.

Use your fingers to gently tousle the bun for a **textured, carefree finish**. Style the bangs by trimming them thin and slightly choppy for a wispy effect.

This hairstyle suits laid-back events and gives a youthful, effortless look, perfect for those who enjoy a balance of structure and softness.

![](/images/bun-with-bangs/image-991.jpeg)

@[yazmin\_zoe](https://www.instagram.com/yazmin_zoe/)

### 4. Voluminous Bun with Feathered Bangs

A **voluminous bun with feathered bangs** combines elegance and volume. To create this look, backcomb the roots slightly before gathering hair into a mid-to-high bun, adding volume at the crown.

Use bobby pins to secure and maintain the shape. The feathered bangs add a **soft, airy frame around the face**, balancing the bun’s volume with a touch of lightness.

This hairstyle works beautifully for formal settings, giving a classy and sophisticated appearance with minimal effort.

### 5. Relaxed High Bun with Natural Bangs

This **relaxed high bun with natural bangs** offers a soft, laid-back look. To recreate it, pull your hair into a high ponytail, twisting it loosely to create a bun and securing it with an elastic or pins.

Leave a few strands out for a casual, natural feel. For the bangs, style them with minimal product to keep the flow light and effortless.

This bun style is perfect for cozy, lazy days while still looking chic and feminine.
