## What is Dutch Braid?

A Dutch braid, often referred to as a “reverse French braid,” involves a three-strand braiding technique where each section of hair is crossed under the others, as opposed to over in a traditional French braid.

**This method causes the braid to appear raised or more prominent on the head, creating an embossed look that’s both eye-catching and stylish.**

The Dutch braid can be styled in various ways, from a single braid down the back, to double braids, or even incorporated into updos and more complex hairstyles.
