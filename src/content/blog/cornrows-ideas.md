## What is Cornrows?

Cornrows, also known as canerows, are a traditional African hairstyle characterized by tight, narrow braids that lie close to the scalp.

**The hair is sectioned into neat, straight lines or intricate patterns and then braided tightly from the roots to the ends.**

This style is not only a form of artistic expression but also a practical way to manage and protect natural hair.

![](/images/cornrows-ideas/cornrows-ideas-683x1024.webp)

**Cornrows can be adorned with beads, ribbons, or other accessories and can be styled in various ways, such as in updos, ponytails, or left loose.**

Cornrows have a rich cultural history and significance, particularly in African and African-American communities.

**They have been worn for centuries, symbolizing status, age, and tribal affiliation in some African cultures.**

Today, cornrows are celebrated globally for their versatility, beauty, and the cultural heritage they represent.
