Finding the perfect hairstyle to suit a round face can feel like a challenge, especially when it comes to shorter cuts.

Round faces are known for their soft, balanced features and fuller cheeks, and the right haircut can enhance these qualities beautifully.

![](/images/short-hair-with-bangs-for-round-faces/short-hair-with-bangs-for-round-faces-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, 22 Short Hair with Bangs for Round Faces, we’ve curated a collection of stunning styles tailored to enhance round faces.**

From timeless bobs and chic pixies to modern shags and layered crops, each style is designed to bring out the best in your features.

## Short Hair With Bangs For Round Faces

![](/images/short-hair-with-bangs-for-round-faces/short-hair-with-bangs-for-round-faces-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Classic Short Bob with Wispy Bangs

This **classic short bob** with wispy bangs frames a round face beautifully, softening facial features while giving structure.

The bob ends just below the chin, creating a natural contour around the jawline. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a **slight inward curve** at the ends to accentuate the face shape.

The bangs are **light and wispy**, adding a soft touch without overwhelming the forehead. A simple blow-dry with a round brush helps shape the bangs and the ends, creating a seamless, face-framing style.

### 2. Layered Shag with Curtain Bangs

The layered shag with **curtain bangs** provides texture and volume, perfect for those with fine hair and round faces. The layers add dimension, reducing the roundness by drawing attention downwards.

Curtain bangs split naturally in the middle, framing the sides of the face gently. To style, apply a **texturizing spray** and scrunch the hair while blow-drying, creating a tousled, voluminous look.

This cut is low-maintenance, allowing for a natural, effortless style with a bit of edge and volume. Ideal for anyone looking to add movement and personality to their hair.
