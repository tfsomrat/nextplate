Long hair is a blessing, but when you’re short on time or simply want a quick, polished look, updos become a lifesaver. Whether you’re heading to brunch, the office, or a casual gathering, a relaxed updo can keep your hair stylishly out of the way while still looking intentional. This list is packed with easy, beautiful, and totally wearable updos for long hair that don’t require salon-level skills. Many of these styles are perfect for gifting occasions too—pairing the right hair accessory with a new hairstyle can make for a thoughtful and practical present. Throughout this guide, you’ll also find a few hand-picked Amazon product recommendations to help you create these looks effortlessly or to give as gifts to someone who loves styling their hair.

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/casual-updos-for-long-hair-1-683x1024.webp)

## 1. Low Twisted Bun with a Scrunchie

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/alexgaboury_1692466894_3172964738610558396_280216151-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[alexgaboury](https://www.instagram.com/alexgaboury/?e=642c9eeb-bbb0-43c5-ad76-95c6c2be1979&g=5)

A low twisted bun is the definition of quick elegance, making it perfect for casual mornings when you still want to look put together. Start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail, twist it, and wrap it into a bun, securing it with a stylish scrunchie. Velvet or satin scrunchies add a soft, romantic vibe while keeping your strands in place all day. This look is ideal for women of all ages because it’s both classic and forgiving. Consider gifting a set of high-quality scrunchies from Amazon for under \\$15—they’re trendy and functional, making them a perfect little luxury.

## 2. Messy Top Knot with Texture

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/laidbylay____1684542821_3106492798983036386_44802376379-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[laidbylay\_\_\_](https://www.instagram.com/laidbylay___/?e=21e12f18-36c6-4cfe-9e2b-200e6998ef58&g=5)

For an effortless vibe that still feels chic, the messy top knot remains unbeatable. It’s a great option for second or third-day hair, especially when combined with a bit of texturizing spray. Simply gather your hair at the crown, twist loosely, and pin or secure with a claw clip for added flair. The beauty lies in its imperfection—the more relaxed, the better. A great Amazon buy is a large, neutral-toned claw clip set that works for any hair color and adds a modern twist to this updo.

## 3. Braided Crown Halo

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/weddinghairbyrhianna_1684205725_3103665031874422107_3030952404-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[weddinghairbyrhianna](https://www.instagram.com/weddinghairbyrhianna/?e=8093f40b-33d7-4aec-a194-3d320e52ee36&g=5)

The braided crown is a soft, boho-inspired updo that instantly elevates your everyday look. This style involves braiding two sections from either side of your head and wrapping them around the crown, pinning them securely. It’s perfect for music festivals, bridal showers, or laid-back weekends with friends. While it looks intricate, it’s surprisingly simple with a bit of practice. A thoughtful gift idea includes a pack of invisible bobby pins and mini clear elastics—both must-haves available in affordable multipacks on Amazon.

## 4. Low Bubble Ponytail

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/roralovestrand_1645559013_2779472916840350959_260820316-820x1024.webp)

**source @**[](https://www.instagram.com/roralovestrand/?e=ce4b0a57-a6b4-4d5b-a90c-6ed7dc376e57&g=5)[roralovestrand](https://www.instagram.com/roralovestrand/?e=ce4b0a57-a6b4-4d5b-a90c-6ed7dc376e57&g=5)

A trendy and youthful take on the traditional ponytail, the low bubble ponytail adds flair without the fuss. Simply tie your hair low, then add small elastics every few inches down the ponytail, pulling each section slightly for that “bubble” effect. This works best on longer hair and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To give it as a gift idea, consider bundling a set of no-damage elastic bands with a detangling brush from Amazon for an all-in-one styling kit.

## 5. Half-Up Knot

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/alexgaboury_1716322760_3373082249443157866_280216151-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[alexgaboury](https://www.instagram.com/alexgaboury/?e=ebc74e82-e842-40fe-a3be-1125b8db1d6d&g=5)

If you want the ease of having your hair down with the convenience of it being pulled back, the half-up knot is a go-to. Just take the top half of your hair, twist it into a loose bun, and secure it with a mini claw clip or a soft tie. It works with straight, wavy, or curled hair and adds a youthful charm to any outfit. For gifting, a collection of mini claw clips in trendy neutral or pastel colors from Amazon is a stylish and practical choice.

## 6. Rolled French Twist with Hair Fork

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/noiebeauty_1723662892_3434655738864620062_7858562862-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[noiebeauty](https://www.instagram.com/noiebeauty/?e=701c6197-c4e6-4ff1-aea7-9c6f045f806f&g=5)

A modern take on the classic French twist, this version uses a hair fork or stick for a relaxed and updated feel. Simply roll your hair upwards along the back of your head and slide a decorative fork to hold it in place. This style is perfect for long workdays or casual date nights, offering an effortlessly polished finish. A carved wooden or metal hair fork from Amazon makes for a thoughtful gift for anyone who enjoys elegant, no-fuss hairstyles.

A floral hair accessory set from Amazon is perfect to gift alongside this look, especially for spring and summer occasions.

## 11. Loose Braided Low Bun

## 14. Criss-Cross Pony Wrap

![](/images/casual-updos-for-long-hair/thedreadshed_nz_1725958449_3453912269405767230_10508763763-819x1024.webp)

**source @**[thedreadshed\_nz](https://www.instagram.com/thedreadshed_nz/?e=5437276e-0d65-41f4-b5e0-b384ebc58bf6&g=5)

A fresh take on the ponytail, the criss-cross wrap adds dimension and hides the elastic for a cleaner look. Part your hair in two above the ponytail, cross the sections underneath and over the tie, then pin. This style works great with sleek, straight hair or polished waves. For a gift that ties in with this style, Amazon’s hair finishing kits with pins, shine spray, and tail combs offer a salon-like touch at home.
