## What are Subtle Blonde Highlights?

Subtle blonde highlights are a hair coloring technique that adds a gentle touch of blonde to your natural hair color.

**Unlike bold and dramatic highlights, subtle blonde highlights are designed to blend seamlessly with your base color, creating a natural-looking, sun-kissed effect.**

These highlights are typically a few shades lighter than your natural hair, adding dimension and depth without overwhelming your overall look.

**Perfect for those who want to brighten their hair subtly and elegantly, this technique enhances your hair’s natural beauty and gives you a fresh, radiant appearance.**
