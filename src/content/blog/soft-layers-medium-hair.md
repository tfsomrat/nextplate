## 29 Soft Layers Medium Hair Ideas for 2025

![](/images/soft-layers-medium-hair/Soft-Layers-Medium-Hair-ideas-for-2025-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers are perfect if you want to add some softness and dimension to your hair.

By gently layering around your face, you can create a flattering shape that highlights your best features.

**This style is especially great if you have medium-length hair because it adds movement without sacrificing length.**

To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut the layers to start around your cheekbones or jawline, depending on your face shape.

You can easily style it by blow-drying with a round brush or using a curling iron for a more defined look.

![](/images/soft-layers-medium-hair/sdifopjusewririweoprewre-771x1024.png)

### 2. Subtle Wispy Ends

If you’re looking for a light and airy feel, subtle wispy ends are a great choice. This style focuses on adding softness to the ends of your hair, creating a delicate and feminine look.

**It’s perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks polished. To get this look, your stylist will thin out the ends of your hair, making them appear wispy and light.**

This technique works well with both straight and wavy hair, and you can enhance the wispy effect by using a texturizing spray.
