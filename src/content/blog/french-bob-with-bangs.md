The **French bob with bangs** has remained a beloved classic in the world of haircuts, merging timeless elegance with an effortless, chic appeal.

Defined by its blunt lines, jaw-grazing length, and the addition of bangs, this style embodies that certain _je ne sais quoi_ associated with Parisian fashion.

![](/images/french-bob-with-bangs/french-bob-with-bangs-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 20 inspiring French bob with bangs ideas to help you find the perfect variation of this iconic hairstyle.**

From sleek, sharp micro bobs to voluminous wavy cuts, each look offers a unique twist on the classic style.

**Let’s dive in!**

## French Bob with Bangs

![](/images/french-bob-with-bangs/french-bob-with-bangs-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Classic French Bob with Tortoiseshell Glasses

This **classic French bob** sits just above the jawline, giving it a chic, Parisian flair. Styled with **soft, blunt bangs**, this cut emphasizes the natural volume of the hair, creating a fuller look around the face.

To achieve this, ask your stylist for a blunt cut that follows the jawline and pair it with bangs that skim the eyebrows. Use a round brush when blow-drying to add slight inward curls, enhancing the bob’s volume and shape.

This look is versatile and pairs well with statement accessories like tortoiseshell sunglasses, adding a modern, bold touch to the classic French style.

### 2. Shaggy French Bob with Texture

This **textured French bob** brings a casual vibe with its **shaggy layers** and relaxed bangs. The cut is kept slightly longer, allowing for a bit of natural wave and movement, perfect for anyone seeking a low-maintenance style.

To recreate this look, ask for a French bob with choppy layers throughout, especially around the ends, and a soft fringe that sits just above the eyes.

Using a texturizing spray or a bit of sea salt spray enhances the natural waves and gives the bob a tousled, lived-in appearance, ideal for effortless, everyday chic.

### 3. Half-Up French Bob with Loose Bangs

This **half-up French bob** offers a playful twist, combining a relaxed bob with a fun, **top-knot bun**. The bangs are kept loose, framing the face delicately, which adds a soft and casual touch.

To achieve this look, section off the top half of the hair and create a small bun, securing it with an elastic or hairpin. Keep the bangs slightly longer, allowing them to blend naturally with the rest of the bob.

A styling cream or lightweight mousse helps keep the loose ends defined, giving a fresh, youthful look that’s perfect for a carefree day out.

### 4. Blunt French Bob with Straight Bangs

This style exemplifies the **traditional French bob** with a straight-across cut and blunt bangs that cover the forehead. The bob itself is kept at a precise length, just touching the jawline, which adds to its sophistication.

For this hairstyle, ask your stylist for a blunt, jaw-length bob and a fringe that falls straight and even across the forehead.

Use a flat iron to keep the hair smooth and sleek, and add a touch of serum for extra shine. This style is perfect for those who love a sharp, defined look with a timeless, Parisian essence.

### 5. Voluminous Blonde French Bob

This **voluminous blonde French bob** features soft layers and a smooth finish, creating a sophisticated and elegant look. The ends are slightly curled outward, adding volume and dimension around the face.

To achieve this, ask for a layered bob that sits just above the shoulders, and blow-dry with a round brush, curling the ends outward. For added lift, use a volumizing mousse before styling.

This hairstyle is ideal for those who want a polished, classic French look with a bit of extra body and flair, perfect for formal events or a refined everyday style.

### 7. Textured Bob with Curtain Bangs

This versatile hairstyle is perfect for those seeking a relaxed, slightly undone look that’s still chic and flattering for various face shapes.
