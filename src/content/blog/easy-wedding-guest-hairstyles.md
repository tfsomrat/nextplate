Attending a wedding and unsure how to style your hair? Do you find yourself wondering, “What’s an easy yet elegant hairstyle I can do at home?” or “How can I make my hair look fabulous without spending hours on it?”

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/Easy-Wedding-Guest-Hairstyles-1-1024x576.jpg)

_We all want to look our best as wedding guests without the stress of complicated hairstyles._

**That’s why I’ve put together a list of 21 easy wedding guest hairstyles that will have you looking stunning with minimal effort.**

From loose waves to chic updos, these ideas are here to solve your hair dilemmas and help you shine at any wedding.

**Let’s dive in!**

## 21 Easy Wedding Guest Hairstyles for 2024

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/Easy-Wedding-Guest-Hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Loose Waves with a Side Part

Loose waves with a side part are effortlessly glamorous and perfect for any wedding guest.

**To achieve this look, you can use a curling iron to create soft, loose waves, then part your hair deeply to one side. Let the waves cascade over one shoulder for an elegant touch.**

This hairstyle works great with long hair and can be easily dressed up with a few sparkly hairpins or a delicate barrette.

It’s simple yet sophisticated, making you look effortlessly chic without spending hours in front of the mirror.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/kdsjfiwerijeoiwrewrew-771x1024.png)

### 2. Low Chignon

The low chignon is a classic and chic option for a wedding guest hairstyle. Start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck and twisting it into a bun.

**Secure it with bobby pins and hairspray to keep everything in place. Leave a few strands loose around your face to soften the look.**

This hairstyle is not only easy to do but also incredibly elegant. It’s perfect for pairing with statement earrings or a bold lip color.

The low chignon will keep your hair out of your face while still looking polished and stylish.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/kjkhhniuhiuhijritojuoitr-771x1024.png)

### 3. Half-Up, Half-Down with Braids

The half-up, half-down hairstyle with braids is both playful and elegant. Begin by creating loose waves throughout your hair.

**Then, take two small sections from the front and braid them. Pull these braids to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins or a small hair tie.**

The rest of your hair can flow freely in soft waves. This look is perfect for showing off your natural texture and adding a boho-chic vibe to your wedding guest ensemble.

It’s also a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still enjoying the beauty of loose waves.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/dsoifjweoijruweoiriweorew-771x1024.png)

### 4. Sleek High Ponytail

A sleek high ponytail is a versatile and stylish choice for a wedding guest. To achieve this look, brush your hair up into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

**For a polished finish, take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the base to hide the hair tie, securing it with a bobby pin.**

Use a flat iron to ensure your ponytail is sleek and straight. This hairstyle is not only easy to do but also looks incredibly chic, making it perfect for any formal occasion.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/sjfoidjweoijruiweorwere-771x1024.png)

### 5. Boho Braid Crown

The boho braid crown is an enchanting and whimsical hairstyle that’s perfect for a wedding guest. Start by parting your hair down the middle.

**Create two braids, one on each side of your head, and bring them around to the back to form a crown. Secure the braids with bobby pins and let the rest of your hair fall in loose waves.**

This hairstyle is ideal for outdoor or bohemian-themed weddings. It adds a touch of romance and fairy-tale charm to your look, making you feel like you’ve stepped right out of a storybook.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/djsoifjeuriweoir-771x1024.png)

### 6. Textured Low Ponytail

A textured low ponytail is a simple yet stylish option for wedding guests. Start by creating texture in your hair with a curling iron or texturizing spray.

**Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. For added flair, wrap a small section of hair around the base to hide the hair tie.**

This hairstyle is perfect for a relaxed yet elegant look. It’s easy to achieve and can be dressed up with a decorative hair tie or ribbon, adding a touch of sophistication to your overall appearance.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/dsjfoiwerweirweiorwe-771x1024.png)

### 7. Classic French Twist

The classic French twist is a timeless and elegant hairstyle perfect for any wedding guest.

**To achieve this look, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it upwards, securing it with bobby pins as you go.**

Tuck the ends into the twist and use more pins to hold everything in place. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to ensure your style stays put all day.

This sophisticated updo is ideal for formal weddings and pairs beautifully with statement jewelry. It’s a classic look that exudes elegance and refinement.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/sdiofewoiruiweouroiewrew-771x1024.png)

### 8. Messy Bun with Tendrils

A messy bun with tendrils is a relaxed yet chic hairstyle for wedding guests. Start by pulling your hair into a loose bun at the back of your head, allowing a few strands to escape for a soft, romantic look.

**Secure the bun with bobby pins and gently tug at sections to create a messy, textured effect. Let a few tendrils fall around your face to frame it softly.**

This hairstyle is perfect for a more casual or bohemian wedding and looks great with floral accessories or a delicate headband.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/sdjfioewrweoiriwere-771x1024.png)

### 9. Loose Curls with a Headband

Loose curls with a headband are a fun and flirty option for wedding guests. Begin by curling your hair with a curling iron, creating soft, bouncy curls.

**Once your curls have cooled, gently run your fingers through them to separate and loosen them up. Place a decorative headband over your curls, just a few inches back from your hairline.**

This adds a touch of sparkle and keeps your hair out of your face. It’s an easy way to elevate your look without much effort, and it works well with both short and long hair.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/sdjiopjweioruweoirwere-771x1024.png)

### 10. Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is a stylish and modern choice for a wedding guest hairstyle. To create this look, divide your hair into two sections.

**Take a small piece from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the right section. Then, take a small piece from the outside of the right section and cross it over to the left.**

Continue this pattern until you reach the end of your hair, then secure it with a hair tie.

The fishtail braid looks intricate but is quite simple to do. It’s perfect for adding a boho-chic touch to your outfit.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/elkfjdsjoiwerewr-771x1024.png)

### 11. Elegant Side Bun

The elegant side bun is a sophisticated and stylish option for wedding guests. Start by parting your hair to one side.

**Gather your hair into a low ponytail on the opposite side of the part and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and hairspray to keep everything in place.**

This hairstyle is perfect for showcasing statement earrings or a bold makeup look.

It’s a polished and refined style that works well for formal weddings and adds a touch of elegance to your overall appearance.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/sdlkfjweoijriweorewr-771x1024.png)

### 12. Twisted Half-Up Style

The twisted half-up style is a simple yet elegant hairstyle for wedding guests. Start by taking two small sections of hair from the front and twisting them back towards the back of your head.

**Secure the twists with bobby pins or a small hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down in loose waves or curls.**

This hairstyle is perfect for keeping your hair out of your face while still showing off your length and texture. It’s an easy and quick style that looks effortlessly chic.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/dsiofewioruweoiurwe-771x1024.png)

### 13. Voluminous Blowout

A voluminous blowout is a glamorous and eye-catching choice for a wedding guest hairstyle.

**To achieve this look, use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and body in your hair. Focus on lifting the roots and creating big, bouncy waves.**

Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place. This hairstyle is perfect for adding a touch of old Hollywood glamour to your look.

It’s ideal for any formal event and pairs beautifully with a bold lip color or statement jewelry.

![](/images/easy-wedding-guest-hairstyles/dsjoifweoijruweoijruoweirwe-771x1024.png)

### 14. Braided Updo

A braided updo is a creative and elegant option for wedding guests. Start by braiding small sections of your hair and incorporating them into a larger updo at the back of your head.

**Secure everything with bobby pins and hairspray. The braids add texture and interest to the updo, making it look more intricate and polished.**

This hairstyle is perfect for formal weddings and can be accessorized with decorative pins or flowers. It’s a beautiful and unique style that will make you stand out in the crowd.

Soft beach waves are a relaxed and chic hairstyle for wedding guests. To achieve this look, use a curling iron or a beach wave spray to create loose, tousled waves.
