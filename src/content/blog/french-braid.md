## What are French Braids?

French braids are a classic and elegant hairstyle where the hair is braided close to the scalp, incorporating sections of hair as you go to create a sleek, woven pattern.

**This timeless style is not only versatile and suitable for various occasions, from casual outings to formal events, but it’s also a great way to keep hair neat.**

Whether you’re dressing up for a special occasion or looking for a chic everyday look, French braids offer a perfect blend of simplicity and sophistication, making them a favorite choice for many.
