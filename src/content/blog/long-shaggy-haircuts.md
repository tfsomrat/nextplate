Long shaggy haircuts have become a staple in modern hairstyling, merging effortless texture with a carefree attitude that’s hard to resist.

This iconic cut, popularized in the ’70s, has evolved into a versatile style that flatters all hair types and face shapes.

![](/images/long-shaggy-haircuts/long-shaggy-haircuts-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ll take you through 24 stunning long shaggy haircut ideas that celebrate the versatility of this style.**

From bold, voluminous looks with edgy micro bangs to sleek, minimal layers that add just a hint of texture, there’s a shaggy cut here for every taste.

## Long Shaggy Haircuts

![](/images/long-shaggy-haircuts/long-shaggy-haircuts-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Silver Shaggy Layers with Curtain Bangs

This style features **long, silver-toned layers** that create volume and movement, accentuated with curtain bangs that frame the face.

The cut is perfect for those embracing natural gray tones, offering a blend of **edgy yet elegant vibes**.

To achieve this look, request **long, wispy layers** starting from the cheekbones, paired with curtain bangs that softly split in the middle.

Style by blow-drying with a round brush to enhance volume, followed by a light mist of texturizing spray for added depth and movement.

![](/images/long-shaggy-haircuts/image-1008.jpeg)

### 2. Sleek Shag with Feathered Ends

This shaggy hairstyle combines **smooth, long layers** with feathered ends that create a soft, blended look.

The hair flows naturally with minimal layers starting mid-length, gradually feathering out at the ends for subtle volume.

To achieve this look, ask for **soft, long layers** with feathered edges, focusing on framing the face.

Blow-dry with a paddle brush for sleekness, and use a flat iron to smooth the hair while flicking out the ends slightly for texture.

Styling is simple: blow-dry with a round brush to boost volume, followed by a spritz of shine spray to accentuate the auburn color.
