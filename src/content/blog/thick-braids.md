## What is Thick Braids?

Thick braids are a type of hairstyle where the hair is braided into large, voluminous sections.

**This style emphasizes the natural fullness and density of the hair, creating a bold and striking appearance.**

![](/images/thick-braids/23-Thick-Braids-Style-hairs-to-copy-in-2025-2-683x1024.webp)

Thick braids can be achieved through various braiding techniques, such as three-strand braids, French braids, Dutch braids, and more.

### Benefits of Thick Braids:

1.  **Versatility**: Thick braids can be styled in numerous ways, from simple everyday looks to intricate updos for special occasions.
2.  **Protective Style**: Braiding your hair helps protect it from damage and breakage by reducing exposure to environmental elements and constant manipulation.
3.  **Low Maintenance**: Once braided, thick braids require minimal upkeep, making them an excellent option for those with busy lifestyles.
4.  **Long-Lasting**: Depending on the type of braid, they can last for several days or even weeks, providing a durable and long-lasting hairstyle.
5.  **Showcase Thickness**: Thick braids highlight the volume and texture of your hair, making it look fuller and more robust.

### Popular Thick Braid Styles:

*   **Classic Three-Strand Braid**: A simple yet elegant braid that suits all occasions.
*   **Box Braids**: Individual braids that offer versatility and can last for weeks.
*   **Dutch Braid**: A reverse French braid that stands out and adds dimension to your hairstyle.
*   **Goddess Braids**: Larger cornrows that create a bold and dramatic look.
*   **Fishtail Braid**: An intricate braid that looks detailed and unique.

Thick braids can be customized with various accessories, such as beads, ribbons, and hair ties, to add a personal touch and enhance the overall look.

Whether you have naturally thick hair or use extensions to achieve the desired volume, thick braids are a stylish and practical choice for many.

## 23 Thick Braids Style Ideas ### 1. Classic Three-Strand Braid

The classic three-strand braid is a timeless style that suits all hair lengths. It’s perfect for any occasion, whether you’re heading to work or going out with friends.

**To create this look, simply divide your hair into three sections and alternate crossing the outer sections over the middle.**

It’s a simple yet elegant style that keeps your hair neat and manageable. Plus, it’s a great way to showcase the thickness and length of your hair.

![](/images/thick-braids/fgfdgrdftrtretrtr-771x1024.png)

### 2. French Braid

A French braid is a sophisticated style that starts at the crown and continues to the nape of your neck. This braid is perfect for a chic and polished look.

**To achieve this style, begin by taking a section of hair at the top of your head and dividing it into three strands.**

As you braid, add small sections of hair from the sides. The result is a neat braid that keeps your hair secure and stylish.

![](/images/thick-braids/dfjhsdfieirueirueoirere-771x1024.png)

### 3. Dutch Braid

The Dutch braid is a reverse French braid that stands out and adds a bold touch to your hairstyle. It’s great for making a statement.

**To create this look, start by dividing a section of hair at the top of your head into three strands. Instead of crossing the outer sections over the middle, cross them under.**

Continue adding sections of hair as you braid down. The result is a beautiful, raised braid that showcases the thickness of your hair.

![](/images/thick-braids/ifdjgusdhihfweiurewre-1-771x1024.png)

### 4. Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is intricate and detailed, perfect for those who love a unique and stunning look.

**This braid splits your hair into two sections, and small pieces are woven together to create a beautiful effect.**

To start, divide your hair into two equal sections. Take a small piece from the outside of one section and cross it over to the other section.

Repeat this process, alternating sides. The fishtail braid looks complicated but is surprisingly easy to do once you get the hang of it.

![](/images/thick-braids/e-iudhysfuweuiree-771x1024.png)

### 5. Crown Braid

The crown braid is a regal style that wraps around your head like a crown. It’s perfect for formal events or achieving a boho vibe.

**To create this look, start by parting your hair down the middle and braiding each side. Then, wrap the braids around your head and secure them with bobby pins.**

This elegant and romantic style is perfect for weddings, festivals, or any occasion where you want to feel like royalty.

![](/images/thick-braids/doifjoidjfoiesjioepfree-771x1024.png)

### 6. Box Braids

Box braids are thick, individual braids that offer versatility and low maintenance. They can last for weeks, making them a great option for those who want a long-lasting style.

**To create box braids, divide your hair into small, square sections and braid each section from the root to the tip.**

Box braids can be styled in various ways, including updos, ponytails, and buns, allowing you to switch up your look easily.

![](/images/thick-braids/iueryfeuhfuhyeiuw-771x1024.png)

### 7. Cornrow Braids

Cornrow braids are braided close to the scalp in straight lines or intricate patterns. This style offers a neat and protective look that can be customized to your preference.

**To create cornrows, part your hair into small sections and braid each section tightly against the scalp.**

Cornrows are perfect for those who want a stylish and low-maintenance option that can last for several weeks.

![](/images/thick-braids/gkjjfsdnhhdsjhfwerfwe-771x1024.png)

### 8. Halo Braid

The halo braid is a beautiful braid that encircles your head, creating a halo effect. It’s ideal for weddings and special occasions.

**To achieve this look, start by braiding a section of hair from behind one ear and continue braiding around your head, adding sections of hair as you go.**

Secure the end with bobby pins. The halo braid is an elegant and enchanting style that makes you feel like a princess.

![](/images/thick-braids/sdjfsdkjhfweiurhweirew-771x1024.png)

### 9. Milkmaid Braids

Milkmaid braids are two braids wrapped around your head, giving a sweet and vintage look. This style is great for a playful, romantic vibe.

**To create this look, part your hair down the middle and braid each side. Then, wrap the braids over the top of your head and secure them with bobby pins.**

Milkmaid braids are perfect for casual outings, festivals, or any occasion where you want a charming and whimsical hairstyle.

![](/images/thick-braids/jifghsuriewujiwrwe-771x1024.png)

### 10. Rope Braid

The rope braid is a two-strand twist that looks like a rope, adding a unique texture to your hairstyle.

**To create this look, divide your hair into two sections and twist each section in the same direction.**

Then, twist the two sections together in the opposite direction. Secure the end with a hair tie. The rope braid is a simple yet stylish option that adds a touch of elegance to your look.

![](/images/thick-braids/erwiupyweurfeiwuriwe-771x1024.png)

### 11. Waterfall Braid

The waterfall braid is a partial braid where sections of hair are left to flow, creating a cascading effect. This style is both whimsical and elegant.

**To create this look, start by taking a small section of hair at the front and dividing it into three strands.**

Begin braiding as you would a French braid, but drop the bottom strand and let it hang.

Pick up a new section of hair to replace the dropped strand and continue braiding. The waterfall braid is perfect for adding a romantic touch to any hairstyle.

![](/images/thick-braids/sdfusdhfuhweiuhriuewrew-771x1024.png)

### 12. Pull-Through Braid

The pull-through braid is a voluminous braid created by pulling sections of hair through one another. It’s perfect for a thick and full appearance.

**To create this look, start by tying a small section of hair at the crown with a hair tie. Create another ponytail directly below the first one.**

Split the top ponytail in half and pull the bottom ponytail through it. Secure the top sections together with another hair tie and repeat the process.

The pull-through braid is a great way to add volume and texture to your hairstyle.

![](/images/thick-braids/rgjfdigherithjoerwe-771x1024.png)

### 13. Goddess Braids

Goddess braids are larger and more intricate cornrows that provide a bold, dramatic look. These braids offer a touch of elegance and can be styled in various ways.

**To create goddess braids, part your hair into large sections and braid each section tightly against the scalp.**

Goddess braids can be styled into updos, buns, or left down for a striking appearance. This style is perfect for making a statement and showcasing the beauty of your thick hair.

![](/images/thick-braids/kjsdfnhkjsdhfhnwerwe-771x1024.png)

### 14. Pigtail Braids

Pigtail braids are two symmetrical braids that are perfect for a youthful and playful look. They can be styled tightly or loosely, depending on your preference.

**To create pigtail braids, part your hair down the middle and braid each side from the top to the ends.**

Pigtail braids are versatile and can be dressed up or down, making them a great choice for any occasion. They’re also a fun way to keep your hair neat and stylish.

![](/images/thick-braids/fdgfdjjtoijierjiertr-771x1024.png)

### 15. Lace Braid

A lace braid is a variation of the French braid that adds sections of hair to one side only, creating a lace-like effect.

**This braid is perfect for adding a touch of elegance to your hairstyle. To create a lace braid, start by taking a small section of hair at the front and dividing it into three strands.**

Begin braiding as you would a French braid, but only add new sections of hair to the top strand. The lace braid is a beautiful and delicate style that’s perfect for special occasions.

![](/images/thick-braids/jskdfhdkjhsfweiuhriuwerew-771x1024.png)

### 16. Bubble Braid

Bubble braids are a fun and modern twist on traditional braids. Sections of hair are tied with bands to create a bubble-like appearance.

**To create this look, start by tying a small section of hair at the crown with a hair tie. Move down a few inches and tie another band around the hair, creating a bubble.**

Gently pull the hair between the bands to create volume. Repeat this process down the length of your hair. Bubble braids are perfect for a playful and trendy style.

![](/images/thick-braids/jhdckjsdhrweiurhweuirwere-771x1024.png)

### 17. Bohemian Braids

Bohemian braids are loose, textured braids with a carefree, boho vibe. They’re often adorned with beads and accessories for a unique look.

**To create bohemian braids, start by braiding your hair loosely and adding texture with your fingers. You can also add small braids or twists throughout your hair for added detail.**

Finish the look by adding beads, feathers, or other accessories. Bohemian braids are perfect for festivals, beach days, or any occasion where you want a relaxed and stylish look.

![](/images/thick-braids/sdfefrjedsfhniuweiuhruiwerfew-771x1024.png)

### 18. Snake Braid

The snake braid is a unique braid that snakes down your back, offering a creative and eye-catching style.

**To create this look, start by taking a small section of hair at the front and dividing it into three strands. Begin braiding as you would a traditional braid, but leave one strand out.**

Braid the remaining two strands together and weave the third strand in and out of the braid.

The snake braid is perfect for those who love to experiment with their hairstyles and want to stand out.

![](/images/thick-braids/dlksjflksdjfowejriweojurweoi-771x1024.png)

### 19. Butterfly Braid

A butterfly braid is styled to resemble butterfly wings, perfect for a whimsical and enchanting look.

**To create this look, start by parting your hair down the middle and creating two braids on each side.**

Pull the braids apart slightly to create a wing-like shape and secure them with bobby pins.

This braid is perfect for special occasions or whenever you want to add a touch of magic to your hairstyle.

![](/images/thick-braids/sfsdfjhsdhfjwerjwerew-771x1024.png)

### 20. Four-Strand Braid

The four-strand braid adds complexity and elegance to the traditional braid. This style creates a fuller and more intricate appearance.

**To create this look, divide your hair into four sections. Cross the outer left strand over the two middle strands, then the outer right strand over the new middle strand.**

Repeat this process, alternating sides. The four-strand braid is a beautiful and sophisticated style that’s perfect for formal events.

![](/images/thick-braids/jgjhgffgffyfyufgyugyu-771x1024.png)

### 21. Side Braid

The side braid is a simple yet chic braid that drapes over your shoulder. It’s perfect for an effortless, everyday look.

**To create this look, gather your hair to one side and divide it into three sections. Braid your hair down and secure it with a hair tie.**

The side braid is versatile and can be worn tight or loose, depending on your preference. It’s a great way to keep your hair neat while adding a touch of elegance to your style.

![](/images/thick-braids/dshfkjsdfhweiuorrew-771x1024.png)

### 22. Mohawk Braid

The Mohawk braid is a bold braid that runs along the center of your head, offering a fierce and edgy style.

**To create this look, start by sectioning off a strip of hair down the center of your head. Braid this section tightly from the front to the back.**

The Mohawk braid can be left as is or styled into an updo for added drama. This braid is perfect for those who love to make a statement with their hairstyle.

![](/images/thick-braids/fgngfhjfgfdgfdgfd-771x1024.png)

### 23. Braided Bun

A braided bun combines braids and buns for a sophisticated updo. This style is perfect for formal events and professional settings.

**To create this look, start by braiding your hair from the front to the back. Gather the braid into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.**

The braided bun is an elegant and timeless style that keeps your hair neat and stylish. It’s perfect for any occasion where you want to look polished and put together.
