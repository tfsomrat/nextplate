A **very short pixie haircut** is the ultimate expression of confidence and boldness. This timeless style not only accentuates facial features but also makes a powerful statement about individuality.

From sleek and polished to textured and spiky, these ultra-short styles are perfect for those who want a fuss-free yet fashionable look.

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/very-short-pixie-haircut-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve curated 20 very short pixie haircut ideas that embrace the beauty of simplicity while offering various textures, colors, and techniques.**

Whether you’re after something soft and feminine or edgy and daring, these pixie cuts provide a wide range of inspiration for your next chic transformation.

## Very Short Pixie Haircut

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/very-short-pixie-haircut-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Buzzed Pixie with Geometric Line

This **buzzed pixie** features a sharp geometric line shaved across the head, adding a stylish edge to the minimalist cut. The sides are shaved extremely short, blending seamlessly into the top, which remains slightly longer to create texture.

**Achieving this look** involves a close buzz with clippers, followed by a straight razor to etch the precise line.

It’s ideal for those who prefer a low-maintenance, striking style with clean lines. Regular touch-ups are essential to maintain the sharpness of the geometric design.

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/13957b8a-9a7e-4c2b-be28-a1bea3de0c6b.jpg)

@[adrianfanus](https://www.instagram.com/adrianfanus/)

### 2. Wavy Pixie Crop with Sideburns

This **wavy pixie crop** is cut close to the scalp but leaves enough length to highlight natural wave patterns. The sides are kept short, while the top is slightly longer to allow soft waves to form.

The **sideburns add definition** to the overall look, enhancing facial structure. To achieve this style, hair should be cut with clippers on the sides and back, while the top can be textured with scissors to encourage natural waves.

A lightweight mousse or cream will help enhance the waves without weighing the hair down.

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/070d8fd2-0498-4954-9da6-1487372a149d.jpg)

@[jalykasmith](https://www.instagram.com/jalykasmith/)

### 3. Platinum Blonde Pixie with Skin Fade

This **platinum pixie** embraces a bold color and a tight skin fade, perfect for making a striking statement. The fade starts high on the sides, blending smoothly into the slightly longer top.

**Bleaching the hair to a bright platinum** is essential to achieve the full effect, and it requires maintenance to keep the color vibrant.

A high-quality toner is needed to avoid brassiness. For styling, keep the hair short on top but textured, which adds dimension to the sharpness of the fade.

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/image-533.jpeg)

@[kiva.rosescu](https://www.instagram.com/kiva.rosescu/)

### 4. Tapered Pixie with Finger Waves

This **tapered pixie cut** blends a close crop with defined **finger waves** across the top. The sides are tapered down, while the top features carefully styled waves, reminiscent of vintage glamour.

To achieve this look, the hair is cropped close with clippers, while the finger waves are created using a gel or setting lotion and a comb to sculpt the hair into smooth curves. The waves are set and dried under a hooded dryer for maximum hold.

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/image-534.jpeg)

@[kimberlymartinshairsalon](https://www.instagram.com/kimberlymartinshairsalon/)

### 5. Simple Buzz Cut Pixie

This **buzz cut pixie** is the epitome of minimalist beauty. With an extremely close shave all around, this cut exudes confidence and highlights facial features effortlessly.

The **buzz cut** keeps hair uniformly short, making it a no-fuss style ideal for those looking for a clean and low-maintenance haircut.

Achieving this look involves using clippers set to a low guard, ensuring the hair remains even throughout.

This cut works well for any hair type and requires minimal upkeep beyond regular trims to maintain the sharp, short length.

### 6. Textured Platinum Pixie

The **textured platinum pixie** stands out with its icy hue and edgy finish. This style keeps the hair **closely cropped** with a slightly longer top for texture.

The platinum color adds a modern twist to the classic pixie, giving it a high-fashion edge. To achieve this look, the sides are cut short with clippers, while the top is trimmed with scissors to create a textured finish.

Bleaching and toning are essential to achieve the striking platinum color, with regular touch-ups needed to keep the hue vibrant.

### 7. Soft Blonde Shaved Pixie

This **soft blonde pixie** combines the edgy feel of a shaved cut with the softness of light blonde hair. The close shave around the sides blends into a slightly longer top that retains a soft texture.

Achieving this style requires using clippers on the sides and keeping the top just a bit longer for a more feminine touch.

The light blonde color softens the look, making it versatile for different settings. Regular trims are necessary to maintain the shape and texture of this chic pixie.

### 8. Sculpted Finger Wave Pixie

The **sculpted finger wave pixie** takes inspiration from vintage glamour with its carefully defined waves. This pixie is cut short, but the finger waves add texture and elegance, making it a standout choice for special occasions.

Achieving this style involves cutting the hair close on the sides and back while keeping the top long enough to create **finger waves** using a comb, gel, and setting lotion.

The waves are set under a hooded dryer for a polished finish, giving the hair a soft, glamorous sheen.

![](/images/very-short-pixie-haircut/image-538.jpeg)

### 9. Asymmetrical Pixie with Long Layers

This **asymmetrical pixie** combines short sides with a longer, sweeping top layer, giving the style both elegance and edge.

The sides and back are closely cropped with clippers, while the top layers are kept long and styled to one side for added volume and movement. This creates a dynamic contrast between the lengths, perfect for adding texture.

To achieve this look, the top can be blow-dried with a round brush for volume, and light styling cream helps define the longer layers. This pixie cut works well for those who want a chic yet versatile style.

### 10. Textured Pixie with Undercut

The **textured pixie with an undercut** emphasizes texture on top with a dramatic undercut. The undercut, shaved close to the scalp, contrasts with the **choppy, voluminous top**.

To style, the top layers can be rough-dried and texturized with a pomade or styling wax to enhance the choppy finish.

Clippers are used for the undercut, while scissors add dimension to the top. This style suits those looking for a bold, edgy haircut that’s easy to maintain with regular trims.

### 11. Classic Ginger Pixie Cut

This **classic ginger pixie** embraces simplicity with an all-over close crop. The bold ginger color adds personality and warmth to the cut, making it a standout choice for those with light or fair complexions.

The hair is trimmed evenly across the scalp, keeping it sleek and uniform. This low-maintenance style requires little more than regular trims to maintain the neatness, while the vibrant ginger hue can be enhanced with color-protecting products.

It’s ideal for those who prefer a **wash-and-go** hairstyle that still carries a bold statement.

### 12. Short Pixie with Side Taper

This **short pixie with a side taper** balances soft texture with a defined structure. The sides are closely tapered, while the top is left slightly longer and styled to one side.

The contrast between the tapered sides and the textured top adds a modern edge to this pixie cut. To style, use a light styling cream to give the top layers definition and shine.

This cut is ideal for anyone seeking a refined look with a touch of **playful texture**, and it’s easy to maintain with routine trims.

### 13. Short Tapered Pixie

This **short tapered pixie** is a neat and clean style, featuring a taper on the sides and slightly longer strands on top. This cut is perfect for someone who wants a simple, low-maintenance style that still looks sharp.

The tapering effect from the crown down to the nape adds shape and movement, while the top offers just enough length for a soft texture.

To achieve this look, clippers are used on the sides and back, while scissors add detail to the top for a more personalized finish. It’s an easy-to-maintain style with regular trims.

### 14. High Fade Pixie with Textured Top

The **high fade pixie** features a dramatic fade on the sides, leaving the top textured and voluminous. This style gives off a bold and edgy vibe while maintaining a sleek overall look.

Clippers are used to create the high fade, keeping the sides very close to the scalp, while the top is styled with a textured finish using a light pomade or wax.

This pixie cut is ideal for someone who wants a high-contrast, striking look without much effort in styling beyond adding texture to the top.

### 15. Soft Silver Pixie

This **soft silver pixie** combines a close crop with a trendy silver hue. The cut is short and uniform across the head, which keeps it easy to manage, while the metallic color adds sophistication and modern flair.

Achieving this look requires bleaching the hair and applying a silver toner to get that cool-toned finish. The short length allows the silver to shine without being overwhelming.

It’s a wash-and-go style, but the color needs touch-ups to maintain the tone.

### 16. Blended Pixie with Blonde Layers

The blonde highlights add depth and dimension to the pixie, enhancing the layered effect. To achieve this look, clippers are used for the tapered sides, while the top layers are cut with scissors to create movement.

This style is perfect for someone seeking a natural, easygoing pixie with a hint of color for depth.
