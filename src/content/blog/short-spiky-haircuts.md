## What are Short Spiky Haircuts?

Short spiky haircuts are a trendy and versatile hairstyle option that involves cutting the hair short and styling the top into pointed, upward spikes.

**This look can be achieved with various lengths and textures, offering a dynamic and edgy appearance.**

![](/images/short-spiky-haircuts/bangs-hairstyle-5-683x1024.webp)

Spiky haircuts can range from neat and polished to messy and tousled, making them suitable for a wide range of personal styles and occasions.

**Whether you’re looking for a low-maintenance everyday look or a bold statement hairstyle, short spiky haircuts provide a modern way to express your individuality and keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.**

## 18 Short Spiky Haircuts for 2025 ### 1. Classic Short Spiky Haircut

Looking for a timeless style? You can’t go wrong with a classic short spiky haircut.

_This look features short sides and a slightly longer top, allowing you to style the hair into sharp, upward spikes using a moderate amount of gel._

**It’s a neat and polished look that’s perfect for both casual and formal settings. The simplicity of this style makes it easy to maintain, and it works well with a variety of hair types.**

Just a little product and you’re ready to go, making this a go-to choice for busy mornings.

### 2. Messy Spiky Haircut

If you’re aiming for a more relaxed vibe, a messy spiky haircut is the way to go.
