## What is Curtain Bang Hairstyles?

Curtain bangs are the epitome of effortless chic. These bangs parted down the middle or slightly off-center, frame your face like curtains, hence the name.

**They gained popularity in the ’60s and ’70s, thanks to style icons like Brigitte Bardot, and have made a major comeback in recent years.**

![](/images/curtain-bangs/Curtain-Bangs-Hairstyles-for-2025-683x1024.webp)

What makes curtain bangs so appealing is their versatility – they can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair type.

**Whether you prefer them wispy and soft or thick and dramatic, curtain bangs can be styled to match your vibe.**

They’re also low-maintenance compared to other bang styles, growing out gracefully and blending seamlessly with the rest of your hair.
