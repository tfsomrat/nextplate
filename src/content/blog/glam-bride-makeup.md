## 20 Easy Glam Bride Makeup Ideas to Copy

![](/images/glam-bride-makeup/Glam-Bride-Makeup-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Classic Red Lip Glam

For a timeless and glamorous bridal look, go for the classic red lip glam. You can achieve this with a flawless complexion, bold red lips, smoky eyes with winged eyeliner, and long, voluminous lashes.

**Style your hair in loose waves and top it off with a delicate tiara for that regal touch.**

This look is perfect if you want to feel like a Hollywood star on your big day. It’s bold, it’s beautiful, and it’s bound to make a lasting impression.

![](/images/glam-bride-makeup/oipsdfjuioeureoiprr-771x1024.png)

### 2. Golden Goddess

Transform into a golden goddess with a sun-kissed glow. Use shimmering gold eyeshadow, soft peach lips, and a bronzed contour to achieve this radiant look.

**Style your hair in a sleek, low bun adorned with golden hairpins to add a touch of elegance.**

This makeup is perfect for outdoor weddings, giving you a warm, luminous look that shines in the sunlight. Embrace your inner goddess and let your beauty glow.

![](/images/glam-bride-makeup/sdjoifjweoirweiojruoiewjruiweor-771x1024.png)

### 3. Vintage Hollywood Glam

Channel the timeless elegance of old Hollywood with vintage glam. Opt for bold red lips, defined eyebrows, and dramatic winged eyeliner.

**Style your hair in soft finger waves and complete the look with a vintage birdcage veil. This look is perfect for brides who love classic, retro styles.**

It’s sophisticated, and stylish, and adds a touch of nostalgia to your wedding day look. Feel like a Hollywood starlet as you walk down the aisle.
