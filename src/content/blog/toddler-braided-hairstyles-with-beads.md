Braided hairstyles have long been a go-to for toddlers, offering a combination of practicality and style that parents love.

Whether it’s keeping hair neat for school or adding a playful touch for special occasions, **toddler braided hairstyles with beads** are a fun and functional choice.

![](/images/toddler-braided-hairstyles-with-beads/toddler-braided-hairstyles-with-beads-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 22 adorable toddler braided hairstyles with beads, showcasing a range of styles from intricate cornrows to playful pigtails and elegant twists.**

Whether you’re looking for a simple everyday style or something more elaborate for a special event, these bead-accented braids offer the perfect blend of creativity and charm.

## Toddler Braided Hairstyles with Beads

![](/images/toddler-braided-hairstyles-with-beads/toddler-braided-hairstyles-with-beads-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Cornrow Braids with Colorful Beads and Top Puff

This **cornrow braids** style features neatly aligned cornrows braided to the back, ending in colorful beads for a playful finish.

A **top puff** made from natural hair or synthetic extension adds a cute and fun element, giving height and flair to the style. To achieve this look, part the hair into sections, braid the hair close to the scalp, and secure the ends with **bright beads**.

Finish by gathering the top into a puff for extra volume. This style is perfect for keeping the hair tidy while adding a fun touch with beads and a puff.

### 2. Spiral Cornrows with Pigtail Beads

This intricate style features **spiral-pattern cornrows**, designed to swirl around the head, ending in cute pigtails adorned with vibrant beads.

The pigtails are accessorized with **bright-colored bows**, making it perfect for toddlers. To recreate this look, braid the hair in **curved, spiral sections**, tying off each end with beads for a decorative touch.

This style not only keeps hair well-managed but also offers a playful look, perfect for special occasions or everyday wear. The addition of beads and bows enhances its youthful and cheerful vibe.

### 3. Classic Feed-In Cornrows with Beads and Bow

This sleek style consists of **feed-in cornrows**, perfectly aligned and flowing toward the back. The braids are tied off with **neutral-colored beads** and gathered into a simple bow.

This look is achieved by sectioning the hair into neat parts, then braiding the feed-in cornrows from front to back, adding extensions for length if needed.

Finish the ends with beads and tie the braids into a bow for a polished look. This style is ideal for toddlers as it’s neat, secure, and easy to maintain while allowing for customization with beads and accessories.

### 4. Half-Up Braided Top Knot with Beads

This trendy hairstyle combines a **half-up, half-down style** with braids and a top knot. The top section is braided and gathered into a **top knot**, while the remaining braids are left to fall freely and adorned with beads.

To achieve this look, section the top half of the hair for braiding, then gather it into a bun. The remaining braids can be decorated with **white and pink beads** for a fun pop of color. This style is playful and perfect for active toddlers who need a secure yet stylish look.
