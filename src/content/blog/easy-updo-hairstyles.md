When it comes to hairstyles, updos are the perfect combination of elegance and convenience.

Whether you’re getting ready for a formal event, a casual outing, or just want to keep your hair out of your face while still looking chic, **easy updo hairstyles** offer versatility and style without the hassle.

![](/images/easy-updo-hairstyles/easy-updo-hairstyles-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ll explore 19 easy updo hairstyles that will elevate your look with minimal effort.**

Whether you’re aiming for a polished bridal bun or a relaxed, tousled style for a casual event, these updos cater to all hair types and occasions.

## Easy Updo Hairstyles

![](/images/easy-updo-hairstyles/Easy-Updo-Hairstyles-ideas-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Voluminous Twisted Updo

This **voluminous twisted updo** combines elegance with texture. The hair is gathered into a **twist**, adding height at the crown for a fuller appearance, while the front strands are softly curled and left to frame the face.

Achieve this look by sectioning the hair, creating a **loose twist**, and pinning it into place. Allow a few tendrils to fall naturally around the face for a romantic finish.

This hairstyle is ideal for **formal events** or special occasions, offering a **sophisticated** and effortless look.

![](/images/easy-updo-hairstyles/image-397.jpeg)

@[rjstylesco](https://www.instagram.com/rjstylesco/)

### 2. Soft Twisted Low Bun

A **soft twisted low bun** is an effortless way to create a chic updo. The hair is loosely gathered at the nape of the neck, twisted into a bun, and secured with bobby pins.

The front strands are curled to soften the face. To achieve this, divide the hair into sections, loosely twist, and form a **low bun**.

This hairstyle is perfect for casual or semi-formal occasions where a touch of elegance is desired. It’s simple yet polished.

![](/images/easy-updo-hairstyles/image-398-1024x1024.jpeg)

@[sarahwhair](https://www.instagram.com/sarahwhair/)

### 3. Sleek Low Bun

The **sleek low bun** is a minimalistic and classic updo that is perfect for any event. Hair is smoothed back, free from flyaways, and gathered at the nape of the neck into a **neat bun**.

To achieve this look, use a fine-tooth comb and styling cream to smooth the hair, then twist it into a bun and secure it with pins.

This updo works well for **professional settings** and is perfect for keeping the hair out of the face while maintaining a polished appearance.

![](/images/easy-updo-hairstyles/image-399-1024x1024.jpeg)

@[hairbycaseyveda](https://www.instagram.com/hairbycaseyveda/)

### 4. Twisted Formal Bun

This **twisted formal bun** combines sleek elements with a textured twist. The hair is pulled back into a sleek base and twisted intricately to form a polished bun.

To recreate this, smooth the hair back, leaving no flyaways, and carefully twist the strands before pinning them in place.

It’s ideal for **formal events**, offering a balanced mix of elegance and ease. The **sleek finish** around the base makes it a refined choice.

![](/images/easy-updo-hairstyles/image-400.jpeg)

### 5. Twisted Braided Bun

This **twisted braided bun** adds texture and dimension to a classic updo. The hair is sectioned into multiple twists and braids, which are then pinned into a low bun.

Front tendrils are loosely curled to soften the overall look. To achieve this style, start by creating a few loose twists on the top and sides, braid the back section, and then wrap everything into a bun.

This updo is great for **romantic occasions** or **formal events** where you want an intricate yet easy-to-achieve hairstyle.

### 6. Tousled Textured Updo

For a laid-back yet chic look, the **tousled textured updo** is perfect. The hair is loosely gathered into a low, messy bun, with curled strands left loose at the front.

To achieve this style, start by curling the hair, gather it loosely at the nape, and pin it up with bobby pins for a slightly messy finish.

This style works well for **casual events** and adds a touch of effortless charm to your look.

### 7. Curled Elegant Updo

This **curled elegant updo** is perfect for special occasions. The hair is curled and gathered into a voluminous bun, creating a soft yet glamorous look.

Front sections are curled and left out to frame the face. Achieve this style by curling the entire head of hair, then loosely gathering it into a bun and securing it with pins.

This look is ideal for weddings, formal events, or any occasion where you want a **sophisticated** hairstyle with minimal effort.

This updo is great for both **formal settings** and **everyday wear**, offering an elegant and simple finish.

### 12. Soft Twisted Bridal Bun

This **soft twisted bridal bun** is ideal for weddings or other romantic occasions. The hair is twisted and pinned into a voluminous bun, with loose tendrils framing the face.

To recreate this look, twist sections of the hair and secure them at the back, leaving the front pieces curled and loose for a soft finish.

### 13. Sleek French Twist Updo

The **sleek French twist updo** is a timeless and sophisticated hairstyle, perfect for formal occasions like weddings, red carpet events, or upscale dinners.

This classic updo involves twisting the hair vertically from the nape of the neck up toward the crown, creating a smooth and structured appearance.

To achieve this look, start by brushing the hair back, gather it as if creating a ponytail, then twist it tightly, folding the twist upwards and securing it with bobby pins.

### 14. Tousled Low Bun

For a more relaxed yet polished look, the **tousled low bun** is the ideal choice. This hairstyle combines an effortless, undone feel with just the right amount of structure, making it perfect for **casual events** like brunches or even romantic dates.

The hair is loosely gathered at the nape of the neck and twisted into a bun, with soft, face-framing tendrils left to fall naturally around the face.

To create this style, backcomb the roots for extra volume, loosely twist the hair into a bun, and secure it with pins. This updo provides a laid-back yet stylish option for those seeking a balance of texture and elegance.

### 15. Sleek Knotted Bun

The **sleek knotted bun** offers a modern, minimalist approach to the traditional bun. Perfect for occasions where you want a neat, clean finish whether for professional settings, elegant dinners, or minimalist weddings this updo is all about **precision and shine**.

To achieve this, pull the hair back tightly using a fine-tooth comb and apply serum to ensure a glossy finish. Knot the hair at the nape of the neck and secure it with bobby pins for a sleek, low bun.

The clean, polished look makes it an excellent choice for those who prefer simple yet stunning hairstyles that focus on structure and elegance.
