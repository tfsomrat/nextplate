Curly hair is known for its personality, texture, and volume, but finding the perfect medium-length haircut to complement those natural curls can be a challenge.

Medium-length curly haircuts offer the ideal balance between versatility and ease of styling. They’re long enough to showcase the bounce and definition of curls, yet short enough to manage daily without being weighed down.

![](/images/curly-haircuts-medium/curly-haircuts-medium-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve curated 20 curly haircuts medium ideas to inspire your next salon visit. From bold bangs to layered lobs, these cuts are designed to enhance your natural curl pattern while offering stylish, easy-to-maintain options.**

Each idea highlights different techniques to shape and define your curls, ensuring that no matter your curl type, there’s a look here for you.

## Curly Haircuts Medium

![](/images/curly-haircuts-medium/curly-haircuts-medium-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Shaggy Curly Layers with Bangs

This **shaggy curly cut with bangs** is a perfect medium-length style for someone who wants a low-maintenance yet trendy look. The **soft, tousled layers** add volume and shape, giving the hair a natural, messy-chic vibe.

The bangs blend effortlessly into the rest of the curls, framing the face with a soft texture. Achieving this look involves **cutting layers throughout** to enhance the curl definition while maintaining length. The bangs are typically textured and feathered to complement the curly texture.

Styling involves using a curl-enhancing cream and air-drying or diffusing for a defined, voluminous look. This cut works well for **naturally curly or wavy hair types** that want added movement.

![](/images/curly-haircuts-medium/image-589-823x1024.jpeg)

### 2. Voluminous Shoulder-Length Curls

This hairstyle offers a classic, shoulder-length cut that enhances the natural curl pattern. The **even length** keeps the look polished while the curls themselves provide plenty of volume.

To achieve this look, the hair is typically **trimmed evenly around the shoulders**, with minimal layers to maintain fullness. For styling, use a leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining gel to enhance and **lock in the curls**.

Diffusing the hair upside down or letting it air-dry will create extra volume. This haircut is great for those who love **defined, bouncy curls** and want a style that offers both structure and playfulness.

![](/images/curly-haircuts-medium/image-590-1024x1024.jpeg)

@[rentedhair](https://www.instagram.com/rentedhair/)

### 3. Curly Bob with Soft Layers

This curly bob features **soft layers that gradually shorten toward the crown**, creating volume at the top while leaving the sides and ends free for curl definition.

This style keeps the overall length around the shoulders, but the layers give it a **full, rounded shape** that complements the natural curl pattern. To achieve this, cut layers in a graduated manner, focusing on keeping the curls tight around the crown.

Use a lightweight styling cream to prevent frizz and define the curls. This cut works beautifully on **medium-thick curly hair**, making it ideal for someone who wants volume without losing length.

![](/images/curly-haircuts-medium/image-591.jpeg)

### 4. Textured Curly Lob with Highlights

The **textured curly lob** with subtle highlights is a fantastic medium-length cut for anyone looking to add dimension to their curls.

The layers are cut to enhance movement, while the **highlights bring out the natural texture** and give the curls a sun-kissed glow.

To style, apply a curl-enhancing mousse and diffuse for added volume. The highlights can be balayage or foil-applied, depending on your preference, and should focus on the topmost layers for a natural effect.

### 5. Blonde Shaggy Curls with Soft Waves

This **blonde shaggy cut** with soft waves is an effortlessly cool, medium-length hairstyle. The loose, tousled curls create a casual, beachy vibe, while the subtle layers give it texture and volume.

Achieving this look involves cutting in **long layers** to maintain the length while adding volume through the ends. A curling iron or wand can be used to define the waves, but for naturally curly hair, a **curl cream** and scrunching technique will enhance texture.

This cut is perfect for those looking to add movement and volume to their naturally curly or wavy hair, while keeping it lightweight and easy to manage.

### 6. Curly Mullet with Short Bangs

This edgy **curly mullet** with short bangs is ideal for someone seeking a bold, trendy look. The short bangs frame the face, while the back is left longer, creating a contrast in length that enhances the natural curl texture.

To achieve this, the sides are cut shorter, leaving more length at the top and back, creating a **layered, tapered effect**. For styling, apply a curl mousse or gel to damp hair, scrunch, and let air dry or diffuse.

This cut gives a lot of personality and is great for those who love to embrace their curls with an unconventional twist.

### 7. Rounded Shoulder-Length Curls

This **rounded curly bob** offers a balanced shape that highlights the natural curl pattern while giving the hair a voluminous, rounded look. The cut falls at the shoulders, creating a structured yet bouncy effect.

To achieve this, layers are cut evenly throughout the hair to ensure the curls maintain a cohesive, **rounded silhouette**. Use a lightweight styling cream or mousse to enhance curl definition, and air-dry or diffuse to maintain volume and shape.

This haircut works beautifully for those with medium-thick curly hair and is easy to maintain with regular trims to keep the shape intact.

### 9. Curly Shag with Micro Bangs

### 10. Defined Ringlet Curls with Full Bangs
