Finding the perfect hairstyle on days when your hair isn’t freshly washed can feel challenging, but with the right styling tricks, you can turn second-day (or even third-day) hair into a chic statement.

While we often think clean hair is a must for great hairstyles, sometimes a bit of natural texture and oil can actually enhance the look, adding volume and hold without extra products.

![](/images/hairstyles-dirty-hair/hairstyles-dirty-hair-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, 19 Hairstyles for Dirty Hair, we explore a range of styles that work beautifully on unwashed hair.**

From sleek low braids that hide oily roots to messy buns that add instant volume and structure, these hairstyles are designed to transform your hair into a stylish look without the need for a wash.

## Hairstyles Dirty Hair

![](/images/hairstyles-dirty-hair/hairstyles-dirty-hair-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Twisted Low Bun

This **twisted low bun** is perfect for managing dirty hair, as it hides any greasiness and gives a chic look. To achieve this style, start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck.

Twist the length of the ponytail and coil it around itself to create a bun.Secure it with a few bobby pins, ensuring it feels stable. This style keeps the hair off your face, making it ideal for oily roots.

The **textured finish** of the twist adds volume and elegance, making it versatile for both casual and formal settings.

![](/images/hairstyles-dirty-hair/image-884.jpeg)

### 2. High Ponytail with Face-Framing Strands

A **high ponytail with face-framing strands** is a quick fix for dirty hair, adding lift and structure. Begin by brushing your hair to smooth out any tangles.

Pull it up high on your head and secure it with a sturdy elastic. Pull out a few strands in the front for a soft, face-framing effect. This style keeps the hair sleek at the crown while the loose pieces soften the look.

**Perfect for second-day hair**, the high ponytail conceals any oiliness at the roots and makes you look effortlessly polished.

![](/images/hairstyles-dirty-hair/image-885.jpeg)

@[oneshinybee](https://www.instagram.com/oneshinybee/)

### 3. Bubble Ponytail

The **bubble ponytail** is a fun, stylish option that transforms oily hair into an intentional, voluminous look.

Start by tying your hair into a high ponytail. Secure additional hair ties at intervals down the length of the ponytail, gently pulling each section to create a “bubble” effect.

This style works particularly well on slightly dirty hair, as the natural oils add shine and texture, making each bubble pop.

**Low-maintenance yet eye-catching**, this hairstyle is ideal for days when your hair needs a pick-me-up without a wash.

![](/images/hairstyles-dirty-hair/image-886.jpeg)

@[sara\_hairstylista](https://www.instagram.com/sara_hairstylista/)

### 4. Double Space Buns

Double **space buns** are a playful, modern hairstyle that works well with unwashed hair, giving it texture and volume.

To create this look, part your hair down the middle and secure each side into a high pigtail.Twist each pigtail around itself to form a bun, securing with bobby pins.

This style not only hides greasy roots but also adds a youthful vibe. It’s perfect for a fun day out or casual settings, allowing you to embrace your hair’s natural texture while keeping it contained.

### 5. Elegant Twisted Updo

This **elegant twisted updo** is ideal for covering up dirty hair while adding a polished, sophisticated look.

Start by sectioning off two side pieces, then twist the remaining hair at the back into a low bun, securing it with bobby pins. Use the side sections to wrap around the bun for a layered, twisted effect. This style keeps greasy roots concealed and offers a sleek, classy appearance.

Perfect for both professional and formal settings, this **updo transforms oily hair** into a stylish statement without the need for fresh washing.

### 6. Messy High Bun with Loose Strands

The **messy high bun with loose strands** is a go-to hairstyle for unwashed hair, providing volume and texture.

To achieve this look, pull your hair into a high ponytail, then twist and wrap it around to form a messy bun. Pull out a few strands at the front and sides for a relaxed, undone vibe.

This style embraces the natural texture of slightly oily hair, making it look effortlessly chic and voluminous. **Ideal for casual outings**, it’s a quick, stylish fix that hides any greasiness.

### 7. Loose Curly Half-Up

This **loose curly half-up** hairstyle works wonderfully with curly hair, especially when it needs a refresh.

Simply section off the top part of your curls and secure them loosely with a clip or hair tie, allowing the lower curls to cascade naturally.

The half-up style keeps the hair off your face while showcasing the curls’ volume and texture, which often looks better on slightly dirty hair.

### 8. Tousled Updo with Face-Framing Pieces

The **tousled updo with face-framing pieces** is a chic solution for concealing oily roots while maintaining a relaxed appearance.

Begin by gathering your hair loosely at the crown and securing it in a bun or twist, leaving a few strands to frame your face.The tousled effect embraces the texture that second-day hair offers, giving a **soft, romantic look**.

This style is perfect for those who want to look polished without too much effort, as the face-framing strands add a soft touch to the messy updo.

### 9. Loose French Braid

The **loose French braid** is an ideal hairstyle for dirty hair days, adding elegance while keeping hair secure.

Start at the crown and loosely braid your hair down to the ends, keeping each section slightly loose to create a soft, relaxed look.This style helps hide oily roots and adds a boho vibe to your appearance.

The loose braid **holds well with natural oils**, making it suitable for all-day wear and easy touch-ups.

### 10. Double Dutch Braids with Wrap

These **double Dutch braids** are not only stylish but also practical for managing dirty hair. Begin by parting your hair down the middle and creating two tight Dutch braids on each side, securing them with elastics at the nape.

Wrap a colorful ribbon or scarf around the ends for a unique finish. The braids keep oily roots hidden while adding a fun, sporty look.

This style is great for active days, as it **keeps hair off your face** and maintains a neat, fresh appearance without the need for washing.

To create this look, take small sections from each side of your head, twist them back, and secure with bobby pins or a small clip at the crown.

This style keeps hair away from your face while showcasing its natural texture, which can look even better with a bit of oil.
