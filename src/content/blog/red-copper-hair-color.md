Red copper hair color has taken the beauty world by storm, blending the warmth of copper with the boldness of red to create a fiery, multi-dimensional look that suits a variety of styles and skin tones.

This shade is versatile yet striking, offering a spectrum that ranges from deep burgundy-infused reds to bright, almost orange-toned coppers.

![](/images/red-copper-hair-color/red-copper-hair-color-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’re diving into 24 stunning red copper hair color ideas that showcase the adaptability and appeal of this color trend.**

Whether you’re drawn to a glossy Hollywood wave, vintage-inspired curls, or a subtle, natural red copper, we’ve got inspiration to match every style and personality.

## Red Copper Hair Color

![](/images/red-copper-hair-color/red-copper-hair-color-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Rich Burgundy Red Copper

This **rich burgundy red copper** look offers a deep, elegant hue, perfect for a bold statement. The **intense burgundy base** merges with subtle copper undertones, giving a **multi-dimensional effect** under light.

To achieve this, start with a dark base and blend in **red and copper highlights** using a balayage technique.

This allows the colors to mix seamlessly, adding depth and shine. Ideal for **straight or slightly wavy hair**, this shade maintains its vibrant look with regular color-safe shampoo and monthly gloss treatments.
