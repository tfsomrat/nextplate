Curly hair brings a unique charm and volume that stands out, and pairing it with the right headband can elevate any look effortlessly.

Whether you’re dealing with wild ringlets or soft, bouncy curls, a headband adds both flair and function, helping to tame those curls while showcasing their natural beauty.

![](/images/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, we’ve gathered 18 headband hairstyles for curly hair that are both trendy and practical.**

Each style celebrates the versatility of curls, with looks for every occasion whether you’re heading to work, out for a casual day, or dressing up for a special event.

## Headband Hairstyles for Curly Hair

![](/images/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair-576x1024.jpg)

### 1. Twisted Front Braided Headband with Loose Curls

This look combines a **twisted braid** across the front, acting as a **headband** for soft curls cascading down. The hairstyle is ideal for both casual and special events, giving a whimsical vibe.

To achieve this, part a small section at the front and braid it, securing it like a headband across the forehead. Let the rest of the hair flow naturally in **defined curls**. This style suits medium to long curly hair and can be enhanced with colorful hair ties woven into the braid for added texture.

![](/images/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair/image-821.jpeg)

@[eclairbraids](https://www.instagram.com/eclairbraids/)

### 2. Voluminous Curls with Puffed Headband

In this style, a thick, **textured headband** sits atop voluminous curly hair. The headband not only keeps the curls out of the face but also adds a chic, elegant touch. This look works perfectly for those with naturally full, **afro-textured curls**.

To achieve it, apply a curl cream for definition and place a wide, textured headband around the head to push the curls back slightly. The contrast between the band and the curly texture adds depth and sophistication.

![](/images/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair/image-822.jpeg)

@[anna.divinee](https://www.instagram.com/anna.divinee/)

### 3. Center-Parted Curly Headband Look with Baby Hairs

This style features a **center part with soft curls** falling on each side, enhanced with a simple band for a sleek look. The baby hairs along the hairline add a delicate touch. Start by parting the hair in the center, then place a narrow, soft headband just behind the hairline.

Smooth down any baby hairs using a styling gel. This style is versatile and great for highlighting facial features, creating a **frame of curls** that’s both casual and polished.

![](/images/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair/image-823.jpeg)

@[olovesuuu](https://www.instagram.com/olovesuuu/)

### 4. Braided Low Bun with Ribbon Headband

A **braided low bun** paired with a ribbon or scarf headband creates a polished, elegant look. This style involves sectioning the hair into braids and tying it into a low bun at the nape. Wrap a ribbon headband around the front for added flair.

It’s a beautiful option for those seeking a neat, controlled hairstyle that still embraces the **natural curly texture**. This look is ideal for formal occasions or when you need an updo with character and style.

![](/images/headband-hairstyles-curly-hair/image-824-1024x1024.jpeg)

### 5. Floral Scarf Headband with Natural Curls

For a boho-inspired look, use a **floral scarf** to accentuate natural curls. Drape the scarf over the head and tie it at the back, allowing the curls to frame the face while the scarf adds color and style.

This is perfect for outdoor or casual events, as it holds curls in place while adding a vibrant element. Simply drape a large scarf over the top, wrap the ends under your hair, and secure it at the back for a comfortable, bohemian vibe.

### 6. Brightly Colored Stretch Headband

This look features a **stretchy, bright headband** with wet-look curls. It’s ideal for adding a pop of color to any look while holding curls back, making it perfect for active days.

Apply curl cream or gel for definition, then slip a wide, colorful band over the head. This keeps curls controlled and stylish with minimal effort. It’s a great way to showcase curls without fuss.

### 7. High Curly Ponytail with Wide Printed Headband

This **high ponytail** uses a wide, printed headband to bring flair and focus to tight curls. The hairstyle works well for both casual and semi-formal occasions, as it frames the face while lifting the curls up for volume.

Pull the curls into a high ponytail, place the headband at the front, and let a few curls fall naturally around the face. This style keeps curls off the face while maintaining a bold, voluminous look.

A **simple black headband** paired with short, defined curls creates a clean and polished look. This versatile style is perfect for any occasion, providing a sophisticated contrast between the headband and the natural curl pattern.

To achieve it, place a thick black headband right above the forehead, allowing curls to naturally fall back. The simplicity of the headband keeps the focus on the curls, making it both practical and stylish.

This playful look features a **leopard print bow headband** that holds up a high curly puff. The bold bow adds a touch of personality and keeps the hair out of the face, making it a practical yet stylish choice for kids and young adults.
