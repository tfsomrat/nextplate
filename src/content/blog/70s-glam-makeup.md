The 1970s was an iconic decade for beauty, defined by bold colors, daring contrasts, and an unmistakable sense of glam.

From disco dance floors to high-fashion editorials, ’70s glam makeup embraced everything from shimmering eyeshadows and neon hues to dramatic cat eyes and frosted finishes.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/70s-glam-makeup-1-1024x576.jpg)

**In this article, 15 ’70s Glam Makeup Ideas, we dive into the vibrant world of ’70s-inspired beauty, showcasing looks that capture the magic of this glamorous era.**

Whether you’re looking to channel disco fever with a golden cut crease or experiment with neon greens and floral face art, these makeup ideas will bring a touch of retro glam to your style.

## 70s Glam Makeup Ideas

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/70Sglam-makeup-looks-for-2025-683x1024.webp)

### 1. Bold Graphic Liner

Achieve a classic ’70s glam look with **bold graphic eyeliner**. This style involves applying thick, elongated black liner on the upper lash line, extending it outward for a dramatic, winged effect. The lower lash line can be softly lined for added intensity.

Pair this look with nude or peachy lips to balance the strong eyes. Start by creating a thin line close to the lashes and gradually build thickness. A liquid or gel eyeliner works best for sharp edges. **Finish with mascara on both upper and lower lashes** for a wide-eyed look.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-721.jpeg)

### 2. Smokey Green Eyeshadow with Rhinestone Accents

This **smokey green eyeshadow** look embodies ’70s glam with a touch of modern sparkle. Apply a vibrant green shadow across the lids, blending it outward into a smoky effect. Add small rhinestones around the eyes to mimic the disco ball glamor of the era.

To recreate this, apply a primer on the lids, then layer on the green shadow, adding rhinestones with lash glue for precision. Pair this look with **glossy nude lips** to keep the focus on the eyes.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-722.jpeg)

### 3. Sun-Kissed Freckled Look with Bold Lips

Channel a natural ’70s vibe with **sun-kissed freckles and a bold lip**. Achieve this by blending peach or terracotta blush on the cheeks and bridge of the nose, then add freckles with a fine-tipped eyebrow pencil.

Finish with a **deep red or brown lip color** for contrast. For freckles, gently dot the pencil on the face and smudge slightly for a realistic look. This look is fresh yet sophisticated, balancing earthy tones with a glamorous lip.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-723-1024x1024.jpeg)

@[ateliermgmt](https://www.instagram.com/ateliermgmt/)

### 4. Soft Half-Up Hair and Winged Eye Makeup

Pair a **soft half-up hairstyle** with dramatic winged eyeliner for an iconic ’70s glam vibe. Begin by applying a neutral eyeshadow base, then wing out the liner on the upper lash line, emphasizing the outer corners.

Pair with voluminous lashes and a light pink or peach lip gloss. To style the hair, section the top part and secure it with a clip, adding subtle waves to the rest. This look combines soft femininity with bold eye makeup, perfect for a vintage glam feel.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-724-904x1024.jpeg)

@[renee.rumler](https://www.instagram.com/renee.rumler/)

### 5. Pastel Pink and Blue Frosted Eyes

Embrace the soft glam of the ’70s with **pastel pink and blue frosted eyeshadow**. Apply a pastel pink shade on the inner half of the lid and a light blue on the outer half, blending seamlessly for a gradient effect.

Add a hint of sparkle on the inner corners for extra shine. Pair this eye look with **sheer pink lips** for a harmonious finish. To achieve this look, use a fluffy brush to blend the colors and top with a glitter shadow for frostiness. This look is perfect for a dreamy, soft-focus effect that evokes the pastel trend of the ’70s.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-725.jpeg)

### 6. Metallic Silver Disco Eyes with Bold Lashes

For an ultimate ’70s disco vibe, go for **metallic silver eyeshadow and bold lashes**. Apply silver shadow all over the lid and extend it slightly beyond the outer corners. Finish with thick, dramatic lashes to amplify the eyes.

Pair with **glossy nude lips** to let the eyes be the focus. Start with an eyeshadow primer, layer on the silver shadow, and apply voluminous lashes. This look brings a high-energy, glamorous touch reminiscent of ‘70s dance floors.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-726.jpeg)

### 7. Rainbow Gradient Eyeshadow

Capture the colorful side of ’70s glam with **rainbow gradient eyeshadow**. Blend shades of pink, purple, blue, and green across the lid in a smooth transition. Add a thin winged liner for definition and finish with **glossy peach lips**.

To achieve, use a variety of eyeshadows, starting with lighter colors on the inner corner and moving to darker shades outward. This look showcases the playful and vibrant makeup style of the disco era.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-727.jpeg)

### 8. Smoky Lavender Eyes with Glitter Highlights

Create a glamorous look with **smoky lavender eyeshadow and glitter highlights**. Start by applying a lavender base, then add a darker purple to the outer corners for depth. Lightly dab glitter on the inner corners for added shimmer.

Finish with **soft pink lips**. To recreate, blend the purple shades well and use a glitter adhesive for the sparkly areas. This style captures the soft, smoky essence of ’70s glam with a modern glitter twist.

### 9. Pink Frosted Waves with Glossy Lips

For a soft ’70s-inspired glam, try **pink frosted waves and glossy lips**. This look pairs pastel pink hair with nude makeup to achieve a fresh, ethereal vibe. Begin with a light pink blush on the cheeks and a glossy, sheer pink lip gloss.

Use a volumizing mousse to style the waves, creating a soft texture. Pair this look with **neutral nails** to keep the focus on the face. This style embodies the whimsical side of ’70s glam, combining soft pastel hues with a glossy finish.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-729.jpeg)

### 10. Bold Cat-Eye with Gold Accents

Channel the dramatic flair of ’70s glam with a **bold cat-eye lined with gold accents**. Start with a classic black cat-eye, then outline it with a shimmering gold liner. Complete the look with voluminous lashes and a **glossy, nude lip**.

To achieve this, use a fine-tipped brush for precise gold detailing. This look is all about creating a high-contrast eye that radiates sophistication and drama. Pair with **gold-toned accessories** to amplify the glamorous vibe.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-730.jpeg)

### 11. Neon Green Cut Crease with Glossy Skin

Go bold with a **neon green cut crease** paired with glossy, dewy skin. Apply a bright green shadow on the crease and keep the rest of the lid neutral for contrast. Add a winged liner and finish with a clear, high-shine gloss on the lips.

Achieve the glossy skin by using a hydrating primer and a dewy foundation. This look is ideal for capturing the vibrant, futuristic side of ’70s glam. Pair with **minimalist silver accessories** for a sleek, polished finish.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-731.jpeg)

### 12. Floral Face Art with Soft Pink Eyeshadow

For a softer approach to ’70s glam, try **floral face art** with pink eyeshadow. Use a soft pink shadow on the lids and create delicate floral designs around the eyes with white liner. Finish with a muted pink lip for a cohesive look.

To achieve this, outline the flower shape gently and fill it in with a white pencil. This look is reminiscent of the flower-power influence of the ’70s and adds a whimsical, artistic touch to glam makeup. Pair with **neutral nails** to let the floral details shine.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-732.jpeg)

### 9. Neon Green Eyeshadow with Bold Red Lips

Achieve a daring ’70s look with **neon green eyeshadow and bold red lips**. Apply a vibrant neon green shade across the entire lid, blending slightly above the crease. Finish with a classic, matte red lip to contrast the bright eyes.

Use a fluffy brush to soften edges and add layers of mascara to enhance the lashes. For nails, a **matching red polish** completes the bold statement. This look embodies the fearless spirit of ’70s glam with high-impact colors and contrasting tones.

### 10. Sunset Gradient Eyes with Winged Liner

This **sunset gradient eyeshadow** look combines pink, purple, and orange tones for a ‘70s-inspired vibe. Blend pink on the inner corners, purple on the lid, and orange at the outer edges, then finish with a winged liner.

Pair with **nude lips** to keep the focus on the vibrant eye colors. Achieve this look by blending each shade gradually for a seamless transition. **Soft pink nails** complement the warm hues without overpowering the look, giving a modern twist to a retro trend.

![](/images/70s-glam-makeup/image-734.jpeg)

### 11. Golden Cut Crease with Glitter Accents

Bring out the disco glamour with a **golden cut crease and glitter accents**. Start by applying a shimmery gold shadow in the crease and a darker gold on the lid. Add glitter accents on the inner corner for extra sparkle, finishing with thick, winged eyeliner.

Pair with **matte brown lips** for a chic, balanced look. To recreate this, use a flat brush for precise shadow application and a glitter adhesive to hold the sparkles in place. This look embodies the shimmering beauty of the disco era, perfect for evening glam.
