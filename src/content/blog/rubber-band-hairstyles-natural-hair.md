## 25 Rubber Band Hairstyles Natural Hair ### 1. Classic Box Parting

If you’re aiming for a neat and orderly look, you can try the Classic Box Parting. This style uses rubber bands to create small, box-shaped sections across your head.

_It’s perfect for highlighting the volume and texture of your natural hair while keeping it manageable._

**The puff at each section’s root adds a playful twist that not only looks cute but also gives your scalp a bit of a breather.**

This style works wonderfully for both casual outings and more formal events, giving you a polished appearance without too much fuss.

### 2. Triangle Partings

For a sharp and distinct look, Triangle Partings are the way to go. This style uses rubber bands to divide your hair into triangular sections, making each segment stand out.

**You can let your curls cascade freely from these defined points, which adds a dramatic flair to your overall appearance.**

It’s a fantastic choice if you want to add some geometric intrigue to your hairstyle repertoire and works exceptionally well for showcasing the natural beauty of densely curled hair.
