Curly hair has a unique charm, offering endless opportunities for stylish and versatile hairstyles.

**Whether you’re embracing natural curls, enhancing them with products, or playing around with different textures, curled hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, from casual outings to formal events.**

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/Hairstyles-with-Curls-1-1024x576.jpg)

The beauty of curls lies in their ability to add volume, texture, and character to any look, making them a go-to choice for many.

**In this article, we’ve gathered 18 stunning hairstyle ideas for curled hair, offering you inspiration for every occasion.**

Whether you’re aiming for a polished, sleek look with a ponytail or a romantic updo adorned with accessories, these styles will show you how to make the most of your curls.

## Hairstyles with Curls

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/Hairstyles-With-Curled-for-2025--683x1024.webp)

### 1. Half-Up Curly Pigtails

This **half-up curly pigtail hairstyle** is a fun and youthful look perfect for any occasion. The hair is parted down the middle, with the top section gathered into two small pigtails, while the rest of the hair is left loose to showcase natural curls.

To achieve this style, start by defining your curls using a **curl-enhancing mousse** or gel for maximum volume and definition.

Then, section off the top portion of your hair and secure each side with elastic bands or scrunchies. This style works well for **medium to long curly hair**, offering both playful and elegant vibes.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-285.jpeg)

### 2. High Curly Puff with Headscarf

This **high curly puff hairstyle** is elevated by the addition of a stylish headscarf, making it a versatile and chic choice for curly hair.

Start by gathering all of your curls at the crown of your head and securing them with a hair tie. The curls should be fluffed out for a voluminous look.

For the finishing touch, wrap a **colorful headscarf** around the base of the puff, adding both flair and functionality. This style is ideal for hot days or when you want your curls off your face while still looking put together.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-286-897x1024.jpeg)

@[stylefeen](https://www.instagram.com/stylefeen/)

### 3. Messy Curly Updo with Loose Tendrils

The **messy curly updo** is perfect for a casual yet stylish look. The hair is loosely gathered into a high updo, allowing the natural curls to frame the face with soft tendrils left out in the front.

To achieve this, pull your hair into a loose bun at the top or middle of your head, using bobby pins to secure it without making it too tight.

Allow the curls to naturally fall, enhancing the soft, carefree vibe. This style works well for **curly or wavy hair**, offering an effortlessly chic look that’s perfect for casual outings or laid-back events.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-287.jpeg)

@[luana.nuta](https://www.instagram.com/luana.nuta/)

### 4. Voluminous Curly Updo

This **voluminous curly updo** gives a more polished, elegant look while still celebrating natural curls. The hair is gathered loosely into a high updo, with the curls pinned to maintain volume at the crown.

Start by applying a curl cream or gel to define the curls, then gather the hair into a loose bun, ensuring the natural curls are emphasized.

Bobby pins are used to secure the bun, while some tendrils are left loose for a soft touch around the face. This style is ideal for **formal events** or nights out, giving a refined yet relaxed vibe.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-288.jpeg)

@[curly.hair.boo](https://www.instagram.com/curly.hair.boo/)

### 5. Defined Curly Bob

The **defined curly bob** is a short, well-shaped hairstyle perfect for those with naturally curly hair. This look features tight, bouncy curls that are evenly shaped, creating a soft bob that frames the face.

To achieve this style, start with **moisturizing curl cream** to hydrate the curls and enhance their natural pattern. Then, use a diffuser to dry the hair while maintaining curl definition.

The result is a controlled yet voluminous style that is easy to maintain. This look is ideal for those who prefer a simple yet defined hairstyle, perfect for both everyday wear and formal occasions.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-289.jpeg)

@[rey\_mashell](https://www.instagram.com/rey_mashell/)

### 6. Curly Shag with Bangs

This **curly shag with bangs** is a trendy, textured look that incorporates layers and fringe to add volume and movement. The shag cut works well for naturally curly hair, as it emphasizes the texture while creating shape.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a layered cut with curtain bangs. Apply a **lightweight curl cream** or mousse to damp hair, then diffuse to enhance the curls.

The bangs are styled softly, blending into the rest of the curly texture. This look is playful and youthful, ideal for those looking for an effortless, voluminous style with a modern twist.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-290.jpeg)

@[beautytherapystudio](https://www.instagram.com/beautytherapystudio/)

### 7. Voluminous Curly Mohawk

The **voluminous curly mohawk** is a bold, statement-making style that showcases natural curls in an edgy way. The sides are sleeked back or pinned, while the curls are left to stand tall in the center, creating a dramatic mohawk effect.

To achieve this style, start by slicking the sides with a **strong-hold gel** or mousse, then pinning them tightly. Leave the top section loose, fluffing and defining the curls to add volume.

This look works well for formal events when paired with decorative hair accessories, adding both flair and elegance to your style.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-291.jpeg)

@[travelingwithjessica](https://www.instagram.com/travelingwithjessica/)

### 8. Big, Bouncy Afro Curls

The **big, bouncy afro curls** are all about embracing volume and texture. This style features defined curls that are fluffed out to create a voluminous afro shape, making it ideal for those with tightly coiled or curly hair.

To achieve this look, start with a curl-defining cream or gel, scrunching the product through damp hair. Use a pick or wide-tooth comb to gently lift the roots and fluff out the curls for maximum volume.

This look is perfect for those who want a bold, full-body hairstyle that celebrates natural texture while maintaining definition.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-292.jpeg)

@[hairbyreema](https://www.instagram.com/hairbyreema/)

### 9. Soft Curly Bob

The **soft curly bob** is a shoulder-grazing hairstyle that combines volume with effortless curls. This style works perfectly for those with naturally curly hair who want a short yet full-bodied look.

To achieve this, apply a **curl-enhancing mousse** to damp hair, scrunching the curls to create bounce and definition. Let the hair air dry or use a diffuser for extra volume.

This look is ideal for anyone seeking a low-maintenance yet stylish cut that frames the face beautifully. The soft curls create a romantic and carefree vibe, perfect for both casual outings and more formal events.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-293.jpeg)

### 10. Side-Parted Long Curls

The **side-parted long curls** are perfect for anyone wanting to showcase the length and volume of their natural curls. The deep side part adds drama and structure, making the look versatile for both day and night.

To achieve this style, apply a **curl-defining gel** or cream to damp hair, parting it deeply to one side. Diffuse or air-dry the hair, allowing the curls to fall naturally.

This look works best for those with long, loose curls and creates a voluminous, elegant effect. It’s ideal for anyone wanting a simple, glamorous style that lets the curls shine.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-294.jpeg)

### 11. Defined Spiral Curls

The **defined spiral curls** are a striking, full-bodied hairstyle for those with naturally tight curls. This look emphasizes curl definition and adds volume throughout the hair.

To achieve this, use a curl cream or **curl-defining gel** on damp hair, scrunching each section to enhance the spiral shape. Diffuse to lock in the curl pattern while maintaining bounce and volume.

### 12. Bouncy Mid-Length Curls

The **bouncy mid-length curls** offer a balance between length and volume, making them perfect for those with natural curls looking for a fuller, lifted look.

To create this style, apply a **lightweight curl mousse** to damp hair, ensuring the curls maintain their definition without weighing them down. Use a diffuser to dry and add extra bounce.

This style is perfect for medium-length hair, providing volume without the weight of longer curls.

![](/images/hairstyles-with-curled-hair/image-296.jpeg)

### 13. Middle-Parted Defined Curls

The **middle-parted defined curls** create a sleek and polished look that showcases the natural beauty of your curls. This hairstyle is perfect for those with **long curly hair** looking for a well-defined and structured style.

To achieve this look, start by parting your hair down the center, then apply a **curl-defining cream** or gel from root to tip to enhance the curl pattern. Use a diffuser to dry the curls or air-dry for a more natural finish.

This look is ideal for formal occasions, providing an elegant and sophisticated vibe while still letting your natural curls take the spotlight.

### 14. Sleek Low Curly Ponytail

The **sleek low curly ponytail** is a chic, polished hairstyle perfect for any occasion. This look combines a smooth, sleek base with defined curls at the back for a clean yet curly style.

Start by using a **strong-hold gel** to slick down the top of your hair, then gather it into a low ponytail. Use a curl-defining product on the ponytail portion to enhance your curls and keep them looking fresh.

The sleek top contrasts beautifully with the voluminous curls, making this look ideal for a night out or a professional setting.

### 15. Curly Updo with Floral Accents

The **curly updo with floral accents** is a romantic and feminine hairstyle, ideal for special occasions like weddings or formal events. The curls are gathered into a high bun, with some tendrils left out for a soft touch.

Small floral accessories are added to enhance the elegance of the look. To recreate this style, use a curl-enhancing product to define the curls before loosely gathering them into a bun at the crown.

Pin the bun securely and place delicate flowers around it for a whimsical finish. This look is perfect for anyone wanting a soft, ethereal style for a special event.

### 16. Voluminous Curly Updo

The **voluminous curly updo** offers a refined and elegant look, perfect for formal events or special occasions. The curls are gathered loosely into an updo, with volume maintained throughout the style for a soft, romantic feel.

To achieve this look, apply a curl-enhancing mousse or gel to damp hair, then gather the curls into a loose bun. Pin the curls in place with bobby pins, allowing some strands to fall naturally for a more textured, relaxed finish.

This look is ideal for weddings, proms, or any event where you want to add a touch of elegance to your curly hair.

### 17. High Curly Ponytail with Sleek Edges

The **high curly ponytail with sleek edges** is a stylish blend of slicked-back sophistication and voluminous curls. The hair is gathered into a high ponytail, while the edges are carefully styled into swoops for a polished finish.

To achieve this look, start by smoothing your hair with a **strong-hold gel** or edge control, securing it into a ponytail at the crown.

Define the curls in the ponytail using a curl-enhancing cream or mousse. The sleek edges provide a contrast to the playful curls, making this hairstyle a great option for both formal and casual events.

### 18. Long Curly Hair with Braided Detail

The **long curly hair with braided detail** adds an intricate touch to voluminous curls. In this look, one or more braids are created at the top of the head, while the rest of the hair remains loose and curly.

To achieve this style, part the hair and braid the top section, using a small amount of **styling gel** to keep the braid smooth. The rest of the hair should be treated with curl-defining cream and left to flow naturally.
