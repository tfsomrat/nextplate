## 30 Crochet Bob Hairstyles

A crochet bob hairstyle is a fun and versatile way to wear your hair. It combines the neatness of a bob cut with the flair of crochet braids.

**Essentially, it involves braiding your natural hair into cornrows and then attaching crochet braids in the style of a bob haircut.**

![](/images/crochet-bob-hairstyles/Crochet-Bob-Hairstyles-576x1024.jpg)

_It’s a protective style, meaning it helps shield your natural hair from damage while still letting you rock a chic look._

**To get this style, start by having your natural hair braided into cornrows. The pattern of the cornrows can vary based on the final look you want.**

Once your cornrows are set, you’ll use a crochet needle to loop the bob-length crochet hair through the braids.

**You can choose from a variety of textures and colors of crochet hair to achieve different effects—curly, wavy, straight, or even kinky.**

After looping the hair through, secure each piece with a knot. The great thing about crochet braids is that they’re relatively easy to install and can last up to eight weeks with proper care, including regular washing and conditioning.
