## 18 Low Maintenance Brunette Balayage Hair ### 1. Soft Caramel Balayage

If you’re looking for a natural sun-kissed effect, try the soft caramel balayage. With a brunette base and subtly blended caramel highlights, this look is perfect for medium-length hair.

You can style it with loose waves to add texture and dimension. The best part? It’s super low-maintenance, so you won’t need to worry about frequent touch-ups.

![](/images/low-maintenance-brunette-balayage-hair/dfgertertertertret-771x1024.png)

### 2. Chestnut Ombre Balayage

A chestnut ombre balayage is a chic choice for a sophisticated look. Starting with dark brunette roots that transition seamlessly into chestnut tones, this style works wonderfully on shoulder-length, straight hair.

It’s smooth, elegant, and perfect if you want a stylish yet low-maintenance hair color that doesn’t require constant attention.
