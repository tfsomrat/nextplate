Your wedding day is one of the most memorable and significant events in your life, and every detail matters – from the dress to the decor.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/Wedding-Hairstyles-1024x614.jpg)

**Among these details, your hairstyle plays an important role in defining your bridal look. The right hairstyle can enhance your features, complement your gown, and boost your confidence as you walk down the aisle.**

A well-chosen hairstyle can set the tone for your wedding, whether you’re aiming for timeless elegance, boho chic, or modern sophistication.

**To help you find the perfect look, here are 25 of the best bridal hairstyles to try on your wedding day**.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/trendy-wedding-hairstyles-1-683x1024.webp)

Let’s dive in!

## 25 Trendy Wedding Hairstyles ### 1. Loose Waves with Floral Accents

Achieve a dreamy, romantic look with loose waves adorned with delicate floral accents. Use a curling iron for soft waves, then pin small flowers throughout your hair.

This style adds whimsy and elegance, perfect for outdoor or garden weddings. Its versatility complements any hair length, making it an effortless yet enchanting choice for brides.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704505417ikat7cu1-771x1024.png)

### 2. Classic Chignon

A classic chignon exudes timeless sophistication. Gather your hair into a low bun at the nape, securing it with bobby pins. Smooth flyaways with hairspray for a polished finish.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704624207uy3zheak-771x1024.png)

### 3. Messy Bun

Opt for a chic yet relaxed vibe with a messy bun. Gather your hair into a high or low bun, leaving a few strands loose to frame your face. Tease for added volume and texture. This effortless style suits boho or rustic weddings, providing a laid-back yet stylish appearance.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704699367kvvilfcn-771x1024.png)

### 4. Half-Up, Half-Down with Braids

Combine elegance and romance with a half-up, half-down hairstyle featuring braids. Braid small sections and pin them back, leaving the rest of your hair in loose curls or waves. This versatile look showcases your locks while adding intricate detail, perfect for a soft, romantic bridal appearance.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704734967w6w2havr-1-771x1024.png)

### 5. Sleek Ponytail

For a modern, polished look, choose a sleek ponytail. Straighten your hair for a smooth finish, then gather it into a high ponytail. Wrap a small section around the base to conceal the hair tie. This elegant style keeps your hair in place all day, blending practicality with sophistication.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704769079gk60xm21-2-771x1024.png)

### 6. Vintage Finger Waves

Channel 1920s glamour with vintage finger waves. Sculpted waves close to the head create a sophisticated, timeless look. Use a curling iron and hair gel to set the waves in place. Ideal for vintage-themed weddings, this hairstyle adds a touch of old Hollywood elegance to your bridal ensemble.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/17157047995830u7hfgwq-1-771x1024.png)

### 7. Boho Braided Crown

Embrace bohemian beauty with a braided crown. Braid your hair or use a hairpiece to encircle your head. This whimsical style is perfect for outdoor weddings, pairing beautifully with flowy dresses and flower crowns.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704838438zdtjit79-771x1024.png)

### 8. Hollywood Waves

Opt for classic Hollywood waves for a touch of old-school glamour. Use a large-barrel curling iron to create glossy, defined waves, and hold them in place with hairspray. Pair with bold red lipstick for a sophisticated, head-turning appearance that screams timeless elegance.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/1715704873926j579iim8-1-771x1024.png)

### 9. Side-Swept Curls

Achieve a romantic, dramatic look with side-swept curls. Curl your hair and sweep it to one side, securing it with bobby pins. This style highlights statement earrings or a beautiful neckline, making it ideal for both long and medium-length hair.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wedding-771x1024.png)

### 10. Low Bun with Twists

A low bun with twists offers a modern twist on a classic style. Twist sections of your hair and pin them into a low bun at the nape. This refined, textured look is perfect for formal weddings, providing an elegant.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wedding-1-771x1024.png)

### 11. Textured Updo

For a modern, edgy look, try a textured updo. Tease your hair for volume and pin it into an updo, leaving some strands loose for a relaxed vibe. This chic style offers a unique yet elegant appearance, ideal for brides seeking a contemporary twist on traditional elegance.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wedddddding-771x1024.png)

### 12. Loose Fishtail Braid

A loose fishtail braid delivers boho-chic charm. Braid your hair into a fishtail and gently pull sections to loosen it. This relaxed style complements both long and medium-length hair, pairing beautifully with floral accessories or a simple veil for an effortlessly stylish bridal look.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wwwwwwwwwwwwwwww-771x1024.png)

### 13. High Bun with Accessories

Elevate your bridal look with a high bun accented by accessories. Gather your hair into a high bun and secure it with pins. Add a sparkling headband, comb, or flowers for extra flair.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wwwwwwwwwwwwwwwwwwwwwww-771x1024.png)

### 14. Curly Updo

Showcase your natural curls with a romantic curly updo. Pin your curls into a loose updo, adding floral accents or a jeweled hairpiece for sparkle. This soft, romantic style keeps hair off your face while highlighting your curls, offering a gorgeous and elegant bridal look.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wqqq-771x1024.png)

### 15. Braided Updo

Try a braided updo for an intricate, eye-catching hairstyle. Braid sections of your hair and pin them into an updo, creating a beautiful, textured style. Add small flowers or jeweled pins for extra detail.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/fdvgfdd-771x1024.png)

### 16. Romantic Low Ponytail

A low ponytail with loose curls provides a romantic touch. Curl your hair and gather it into a low ponytail, leaving a few strands to frame your face. Wrap a small section around the base to hide the tie.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/adsfsdafda-771x1024.png)

### 17. Sleek Bob

Opt for a sleek bob for modern, minimalist sophistication. Straighten your hair for a smooth, polished finish, and use hairspray to keep it in place. This chic, contemporary style is easy to manage and looks stunning with any bridal outfit, offering simplicity and elegance.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/fdtgerstr-771x1024.png)

### 18. Classic French Twist

A classic French twist offers timeless elegance. Twist your hair up and secure it with pins for a sophisticated updo. Perfect for formal weddings, this hairstyle pairs beautifully with veils or jeweled accessories, exuding grace and refinement for a classic bridal look.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/ererere-771x1024.png)

### 19. Side Bun with Braid

For a stylish twist, try a side bun with a braid. Braid a section of your hair and pin it into a side bun, adding texture and interest. This chic, elegant style is a modern take on the classic bun, perfect for brides seeking a unique yet sophisticated look.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/wewewew-771x1024.png)

### 20. Loose, Beachy Waves

Choose loose, beachy waves for a relaxed, boho vibe. Use a curling iron to create loose waves, then soften them by running your fingers through them. Ideal for outdoor or beach weddings, this laid-back style offers a beautiful, effortless appearance that complements any bridal dress.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/tytytyt-771x1024.png)

### 21. Glamorous Updo with Volume

For a bold, glamorous appearance, opt for an updo with added volume. Tease your hair to create height, then pin it into an elegant updo. Enhance with a jeweled hairpiece or veil for extra drama.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/rggfgf-771x1024.png)

### 22. Elegant Low Ponytail

An elegant low ponytail offers chic sophistication. Gather your hair into a low ponytail, wrapping a small section around the base to hide the tie. This polished style is easy to manage and complements any bridal look, providing a simple yet refined appearance.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/ghjgdhfgdfhgf-771x1024.png)

### 23. Half-Up Top Knot

Achieve a trendy, stylish look with a half-up top knot. Gather the top section of your hair into a small bun, leaving the rest down. This modern style showcases statement earrings or a beautiful neckline, suitable for both straight and curly hair, offering an elegant yet contemporary feel.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/jgytytdh-771x1024.png)

### 24. Intricate Braided Updo

For a detailed, eye-catching hairstyle, try an intricate braided updo. Braid sections of your hair and pin them into an updo, creating a textured, romantic style. Add small flowers or jeweled pins for added detail.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/sdsdwdw-771x1024.png)

### 25. Soft, Romantic Updo

A soft, romantic updo exudes timeless elegance. Pin your hair into a loose updo with tendrils framing your face. Add floral accents or a jeweled hairpiece for extra sparkle. This delicate style enhances any wedding dress, making you feel like a princess on your special day.

![](/images/trendy-wedding-hairstyles/dsdsdsdsdds-771x1024.png)
