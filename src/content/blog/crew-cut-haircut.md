## What is Crew Cut?

A crew cut is a type of men’s hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top.

![](/images/crew-cut-haircut/Crew-Cut-Haircut-1-683x1024.webp)

It’s a versatile and low-maintenance haircut that has been popular for decades due to its clean, neat appearance and easy upkeep.

1.  **Short Length**: The hair on the sides and back is usually cut very short, often with clippers, and the top is kept slightly longer but still relatively short.
2.  **Tapered Sides and Back**: The hair is tapered or faded, meaning it gradually gets shorter as it moves down the sides and back, creating a smooth transition.
3.  **Versatility**: The crew cut can be styled in various ways, from leaving the top natural and slightly messy to adding texture or even slicking it back for a more polished look.
4.  **Low Maintenance**: Because of its short length, the crew cut requires minimal styling and is easy to maintain with regular trims.

**The crew cut is suitable for many face shapes and hair types, making it a universally flattering option.**

It’s also a popular choice for those who lead active lifestyles or prefer a straightforward, no-fuss hairstyle.
